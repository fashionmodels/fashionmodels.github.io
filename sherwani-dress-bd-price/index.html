Factors that Affect Sherwani Dress BD Price

The price of a sherwani dress in Bangladesh varies depending on several factors. The quality of the fabric is the most important factor that affects the price. Sherwani dresses made from high-quality fabrics like silk, cotton, and linen are more expensive than those made from synthetic fabrics. The design of the sherwani dress is another factor that affects the price. The more intricate the design, the higher the price. Finally, the workmanship also affects the price. Sherwani dresses made by skilled craftsmen are more expensive than those made by less skilled workers.
