La Charreada

La Charreada is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving authentic Mexican cuisine for over 35 years. The restaurant's menu features classic Mexican dishes like tacos, burritos, and fajitas, as well as more unique options like chile rellenos and tamales. The atmosphere is casual and welcoming, with friendly staff and colorful decor that transports you to Mexico.

Sabor Latino

Sabor Latino is another excellent option for Mexican cuisine in Bloomington Indiana. The restaurant features a wide range of dishes, from classic tacos and burritos to more unique options like pupusas and empanadas. The atmosphere is cozy and intimate, making it a great place to catch up with friends or have a romantic dinner with your significant other.

El Ranchero

El Ranchero is a popular Mexican restaurant in Bloomington Indiana that offers a wide variety of dishes, including traditional Mexican favorites like enchiladas and chile rellenos. The restaurant's atmosphere is festive and lively, with colorful decorations and a friendly staff that make you feel right at home.

Lucca Grill

Although not strictly a Mexican restaurant, Lucca Grill offers a variety of Mexican-inspired dishes that are worth trying. The restaurant's menu features dishes like quesadillas, nachos, and tacos, as well as classic Italian cuisine like pizza and pasta. The atmosphere is relaxed and casual, making it a great place to grab a quick bite or have a leisurely dinner with friends.
