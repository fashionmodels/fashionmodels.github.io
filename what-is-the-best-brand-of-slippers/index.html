How to Care for Your UGG Slippers

To keep your UGG slippers looking and feeling their best, you need to take proper care of them. First, you should always store your slippers in a dry and cool place. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight or heat, as this can damage the sheepskin lining. You should also avoid getting your slippers wet, as this can damage the sheepskin lining as well.

If your UGG slippers do get wet, you should stuff them with newspaper and let them air dry. Do not put them in the dryer, as this can damage the sheepskin lining. To clean your UGG slippers, use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dirt or debris. You can also use a damp cloth to spot clean any stains.
