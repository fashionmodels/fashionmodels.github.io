Old School Gangster Clothing Brands A Guide To Classic Style

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a fan of classic gangster movies and want to dress the part? Do you love the timeless style of old school gangster clothing but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this guide, we will explore the world of old school gangster clothing brands and help you achieve the iconic look you desire.

When it comes to old school gangster clothing, there are a few pain points to consider. Many people are intimidated by the bold and confident style that is associated with gangster fashion. Others may not know where to find authentic brands and may end up with cheap imitations. But fear not, with a little guidance, you too can master the art of old school gangster style.

So, what is the target of old school gangster clothing brands? The answer is simple: to create a look that is both powerful and sophisticated. These brands aim to capture the essence of classic gangster style, which is characterized by well-tailored suits, fedoras, and flashy accessories. The goal is to exude confidence and command respect.

To summarize, old school gangster clothing brands are all about capturing the classic style of gangsters from the past. They aim to create a look that is both sophisticated and powerful, with well-tailored suits, fedoras, and flashy accessories.

The Godfather of Gangster Style: Giorgio Armani

My personal experience with old school gangster clothing brands began with Giorgio Armani. Armani is known for his sleek and stylish suits that have been worn by countless movie stars and celebrities. His brand is a perfect example of classic gangster style, with its emphasis on sophistication and luxury.

Armani's suits are made from the finest materials and are tailored to perfection, creating a look that is both powerful and elegant. The brand also offers a range of accessories, including ties, cufflinks, and pocket squares, that can help you complete your gangster look.

The Iconic Fedora: Stetson

Another iconic brand in the world of old school gangster clothing is Stetson. This brand is known for its high-quality fedoras, which have been a staple of gangster style for decades. Stetson fedoras are made from the finest materials and are available in a range of colors and styles, making it easy to find the perfect hat to complement your outfit.

My personal experience with Stetson fedoras has been nothing but positive. The hats are not only stylish but also comfortable to wear, thanks to their high-quality construction and materials. Whether you're going for a classic gangster look or a more modern interpretation, a Stetson fedora is sure to make a statement.

The Power of Accessories: Gucci

When it comes to old school gangster style, accessories are key. And there's no better brand for accessories than Gucci. This iconic brand offers a wide range of accessories, including belts, wallets, and watches, that can help you complete your gangster look.

Gucci accessories are known for their high-quality construction and luxurious materials. They are designed to add a touch of sophistication and elegance to any outfit, making them the perfect complement to old school gangster clothing.

How to Achieve the Old School Gangster Look

If you're looking to achieve the old school gangster look, there are a few key elements to keep in mind. First, invest in a well-tailored suit that fits you perfectly. This will help you achieve the powerful and sophisticated look that is associated with gangster style.

Next, add a fedora to your outfit. This iconic hat is a must-have for any old school gangster look. Choose a hat that complements your suit and adds a touch of personality to your outfit.

Finally, don't forget the accessories. A stylish belt, cufflinks, and a watch can all help you complete your gangster look and add a touch of luxury to your outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What colors should I choose for my old school gangster suit?

A: Classic gangster suits are typically black, gray, or navy. These colors are timeless and sophisticated, making them a perfect choice for any old school gangster outfit.

Q: Can I wear old school gangster clothing to a formal event?

A: Absolutely! Old school gangster clothing is perfect for formal events, as it exudes confidence and sophistication. Just be sure to choose a well-tailored suit and complement it with stylish accessories.

Q: Can women wear old school gangster clothing?

A: Absolutely! Old school gangster style is not just for men. Women can also rock this classic look by choosing well-tailored suits, fedoras, and stylish accessories.

Q: Where can I find authentic old school gangster clothing brands?

A: There are many retailers that specialize in old school gangster clothing brands, both online and in-store. Do your research and choose a retailer that offers high-quality, authentic brands.

Conclusion of Old School Gangster Clothing Brands

Old school gangster clothing brands offer a timeless style that is both powerful and sophisticated. By investing in well-tailored suits, stylish fedoras, and luxurious accessories, you too can achieve the iconic gangster look. Remember to choose authentic brands and stay true to your personal style, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
