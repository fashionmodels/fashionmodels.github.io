What Does The Bride Wear At An Indian Wedding

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, grandeur, and traditions. One of the most important aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire worn by the bride. It is a reflection of the culture and heritage of India. In this blog post, we will discuss what the bride wears at an Indian wedding and how it is an integral part of the wedding ceremony.

Choosing the right outfit for a wedding can be a daunting task, especially for the bride. It is essential to choose an outfit that is not only comfortable but also reflects the traditions and culture of the wedding. Indian weddings are known for their elaborate ceremonies and rituals, and the attire worn by the bride plays a significant role in these events.

The bride typically wears a saree or a lehenga on her wedding day. A saree is a long piece of cloth draped around the body, while a lehenga is a long skirt paired with a blouse and a dupatta. The saree and the lehenga come in a wide range of colors, fabrics, and designs. The bride usually chooses a color that is considered auspicious, such as red, gold, or green.

In addition to the saree or lehenga, the bride also wears a lot of jewelry. This includes a necklace, earrings, bangles, and rings. The jewelry worn by the bride is usually made of gold and is often adorned with precious stones. The bride also wears a bindi on her forehead, which is a symbol of marriage and is considered auspicious.

In summary, the bride wears a saree or a lehenga along with a lot of jewelry on her wedding day. The attire is chosen based on the traditions and culture of the wedding, and the color is usually considered auspicious.

Personal Experience

As an Indian, I have attended several weddings and have seen firsthand the importance of the attire worn by the bride. I vividly remember my sister's wedding, where she wore a beautiful red lehenga and looked like a princess. The jewelry she wore was exquisite, and every piece was carefully chosen to match her outfit. The entire ensemble was a reflection of the traditions and culture of our family.

The Significance of the Wedding Attire

The attire worn by the bride at an Indian wedding is not just a piece of clothing but is a reflection of the family's culture and traditions. Each region in India has its unique style of attire, and the bride's outfit is often a blend of various styles. For example, a bride from North India may wear a lehenga, while a bride from South India may wear a saree. The attire is often passed down from generation to generation and is considered a family heirloom.

The Role of Jewelry

Jewelry is an essential part of the bride's attire at an Indian wedding. It is considered a symbol of prosperity and is often passed down from generation to generation. The jewelry worn by the bride is usually made of gold and is often adorned with precious stones such as diamonds, rubies, or emeralds. The jewelry is often chosen to match the color of the bride's outfit and is a reflection of the family's wealth and status.

How to Choose the Right Attire

Choosing the right attire for an Indian wedding can be overwhelming. There are so many options to choose from, and it is essential to choose an outfit that reflects the family's culture and traditions. It is also important to choose an outfit that is comfortable and easy to move around in. The bride should also consider the color of the outfit and choose a color that is considered auspicious.

Personal Experience

When I got married, I chose a traditional red saree with gold jewelry. The saree had intricate embroidery, and the jewelry was adorned with diamonds and rubies. The outfit was a reflection of my family's culture and traditions, and I felt like a queen on my wedding day.

Question and Answer

Q: Can the bride wear something other than a saree or a lehenga?

A: Yes, the bride can wear other styles of attire such as a salwar kameez or a gown, but the saree and lehenga are the most popular choices.

Q: Can the bride wear white on her wedding day?

A: No, white is considered a mourning color in Indian culture and is not worn on festive occasions such as weddings.

Q: Can the bride wear Western attire for the wedding?

A: While it is not common for the bride to wear Western attire, it is becoming more acceptable in modern times.

Q: Can the bride wear black on her wedding day?

A: Black is not considered an auspicious color for weddings and is not worn by the bride.

Conclusion

The attire worn by the bride at an Indian wedding is an integral part of the wedding ceremony. It is a reflection of the family's culture and traditions and is often passed down from generation to generation. The bride typically wears a saree or a lehenga along with a lot of jewelry, and the color is usually considered auspicious. Choosing the right attire for the wedding is essential, and it should be comfortable, easy to move around in, and reflect the family's culture and traditions.
