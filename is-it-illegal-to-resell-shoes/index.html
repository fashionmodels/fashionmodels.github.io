Is It Illegal To Resell Shoes

Written by Luffy Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered whether it is legal to resell shoes or not? This is a common question among shoe enthusiasts who want to make a profit from their collections. Unfortunately, there are several pain points you need to consider before jumping into the reselling business. For instance, you risk getting sued for trademark infringement, selling counterfeit products, or violating the manufacturer's policies. In this article, we will answer the most critical question: is it illegal to resell shoes?

Reselling shoes is legal as long as you are not infringing on the manufacturer's intellectual property rights. For instance, if you are selling authentic Nike shoes that you bought from an authorized dealer, you are not breaking any laws. However, if you are selling counterfeit shoes or using the manufacturer's trademark without permission, you risk facing legal consequences. It is important to note that some shoe manufacturers have strict policies that prohibit their retailers from reselling their products. Therefore, it is essential to understand the manufacturer's policies before reselling their shoes.

In conclusion, it is legal to resell shoes as long as you are not infringing on the manufacturer's intellectual property rights or violating their policies. To avoid legal issues, ensure that you are selling authentic shoes and not counterfeit products. Additionally, it would be best to understand the manufacturer's policies before reselling their products.

Trademark Infringement and Counterfeit Products

The biggest risk of reselling shoes is trademark infringement and selling counterfeit products. Trademark infringement occurs when you use a manufacturer's trademark without permission. For instance, if you are selling Nike shoes, you cannot use the Nike logo without permission. Selling counterfeit products is illegal and could land you in jail. Therefore, it is crucial to ensure that you are selling authentic shoes and not counterfeit products.

Understanding the Manufacturer's Policies

Some shoe manufacturers have strict policies that prohibit their retailers from reselling their products. For instance, Adidas has a policy that prohibits its retailers from reselling their products online. Therefore, it is essential to understand the manufacturer's policies before reselling their shoes.

How to Ensure that You are Selling Authentic Shoes

To ensure that you are selling authentic shoes, you need to buy from authorized dealers. Additionally, you need to inspect the shoes for any signs of counterfeiting. For instance, authentic Nike shoes have a serial number on the inside of the shoe. Counterfeit Nike shoes do not have a serial number. Therefore, it is crucial to inspect the shoes for any signs of counterfeiting before selling them.
