The first fashion designer in the world is widely believed to be Charles Frederick Worth, who started his fashion house in Paris in the mid-19th century.

Charles Frederick Worth was a British designer who moved to Paris in the 1850s and started working for a fabric store. He had a keen eye for design and quickly became popular among Parisian women.
