Discover The Best Clothing Shops In Edinburgh A Shopaholic s Guide

Written by Christine Oct 08, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're a fashion lover, you know that finding the best clothing shops in Edinburgh can be overwhelming. With so many options and styles to choose from, it's easy to feel lost in the sea of clothes. But fear not! This guide will help you navigate the city's fashion scene and find the best shops for your style.

Are you tired of shopping at the same old stores and not finding anything that suits your taste? Do you want to explore new styles and trends but don't know where to start? We've got you covered. This guide will help you discover the best clothing shops in Edinburgh that cater to different styles and budgets.

Let's dive into the best clothing shops in Edinburgh and what makes them stand out.

1. Armstrong's Vintage Emporium

Armstrong's Vintage Emporium is a must-visit for anyone who loves vintage clothing. Located in the Grassmarket area, this small but cozy shop is filled with unique pieces from different eras. From 1920s flapper dresses to 1970s bell-bottoms, Armstrong's has it all. The prices are reasonable, and the staff is friendly and knowledgeable about vintage fashion.

2. Topshop

If you're looking for the latest trends and styles, Topshop is the place to be. Located on Princes Street, this iconic store has a wide range of clothing, shoes, and accessories for women. From casual wear to party dresses, Topshop has something for everyone. The prices are a bit on the higher side, but the quality and design of the clothes make up for it.

3. Walker Slater

For men who love classic and timeless clothing, Walker Slater is the perfect store. Located on Victoria Street, this shop specializes in tweed jackets, suits, and accessories. The clothes are made from high-quality materials and are designed to last a lifetime. The staff is friendly and helpful, and they'll help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion.

4. W. Armstrong & Son

If you're looking for traditional Scottish clothing, W. Armstrong & Son is the place to be. Located on the Royal Mile, this shop has been selling kilts, tartans, and other Scottish clothing for over 150 years. The staff is knowledgeable about the history and culture of Scottish clothing, and they'll help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time to visit the clothing shops in Edinburgh?
A: The best time to visit the clothing shops in Edinburgh is during the summer months when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the city is beautiful throughout the year, so you can visit the shops anytime you like.

Q: Are there any affordable clothing shops in Edinburgh?
A: Yes, there are many affordable clothing shops in Edinburgh, such as Primark, H&M, and Zara. These stores offer trendy and fashionable clothes at reasonable prices.

Q: Can I find vintage clothing in Edinburgh?
A: Yes, you can find many vintage clothing shops in Edinburgh, such as Armstrong's Vintage Emporium, Herman Brown, and Godiva Boutique. These shops offer unique and one-of-a-kind pieces from different eras.

Q: Are there any sustainable clothing shops in Edinburgh?
A: Yes, there are many sustainable clothing shops in Edinburgh, such as Rêve en Vert, The Tartan Blanket Co., and The Edinburgh Remakery. These shops offer eco-friendly and ethical clothing options.

Conclusion of Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh

Edinburgh is a fashion lover's paradise, with a wide range of clothing shops that cater to different styles and budgets. Whether you're looking for vintage clothing, the latest trends, or traditional Scottish clothing, you'll find it all in this city. So, put on your walking shoes and explore the best clothing shops in Edinburgh!
