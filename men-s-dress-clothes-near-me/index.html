Question and Answer

Q: What should I wear to a job interview?

A: It depends on the job and the company culture, but a classic suit and dress shoes are usually a safe bet.

Q: Can I wear a colored suit to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and the wedding theme, but a navy or gray suit is usually a better choice than a brightly colored suit.

Q: What's the difference between a dress shirt and a casual shirt?

A: Dress shirts are typically made from higher-quality materials and have a more formal cut. Casual shirts are often made from softer materials and have a more relaxed fit.

Q: How should I care for my dress clothes?

A: Follow the care instructions on the label, and avoid washing your clothes too frequently. Hang them up after each wear, and consider taking them to a professional cleaner for a deeper clean.
