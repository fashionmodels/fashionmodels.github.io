Experience the Best North Indian Cuisine at Rajdhani

If you're looking for an authentic North Indian dining experience, then Rajdhani is the place to be. This restaurant is known for its Thali meals that offer a wide variety of dishes that are both delicious and filling. From Dal Baati Churma to Aloo Gobi and Butter Chicken, Rajdhani has something for everyone. The restaurant has a cozy and welcoming ambiance that makes it perfect for family dinners or a night out with friends.

Get a Taste of Punjab at Punjab Grill

Punjab Grill is a popular North Indian restaurant chain that has branches all over the country. Their ECR outlet is known for its lip-smacking Punjabi dishes that are sure to leave you craving for more. From tandoori chicken to paneer tikka and fish curry, Punjab Grill has an extensive menu that caters to both vegetarians and non-vegetarians. The restaurant has a chic and modern décor that makes it perfect for a romantic dinner or a night out with friends.

What to Look for in a Good North Indian Restaurant?

When looking for a good North Indian restaurant, there are a few things that you should keep in mind. Firstly, the restaurant should have a good reputation for serving authentic North Indian cuisine. You can check online reviews or ask for recommendations from friends and family. Secondly, the restaurant should have a comfortable and welcoming ambiance that makes you feel at home. Finally, the restaurant should have a varied menu that caters to both vegetarians and non-vegetarians.

How to Order at a North Indian Restaurant?

Ordering at a North Indian restaurant can be overwhelming, especially if you're trying it for the first time. Here are some tips that can help you make the right choices:

  1. Start with a classic appetizer like samosas or tikkis.
  2. For the main course, try a Thali meal that offers a variety of dishes.
  3. Ask for recommendations from the waiter or the chef.
  4. Don't forget to order some naan or roti to go with your meal.

Discover the Best North Indian Street Food at Chaat Street

If you're in the mood for some delicious North Indian street food, then Chaat Street is the place to be. This restaurant offers a wide variety of chaats, bhel puri, and other street food items that are both tasty and affordable. From Dahi Puri to Aloo Tikki and Pav Bhaji, Chaat Street has something for everyone. The restaurant has a vibrant and colorful décor that makes it perfect for a quick bite or a snack on the go.
