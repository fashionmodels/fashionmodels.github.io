Sunita Kohli: The Queen of Indian Interior Design

When it comes to the most expensive interior designers in India, Sunita Kohli is a name that is often at the top of the list. Known as the “Queen of Indian Interior Design,” Kohli has been in the industry for over 40 years and has worked on some of the most exclusive and luxurious projects in the country. Her design aesthetic is a blend of traditional Indian elements with a modern twist, resulting in stunning and unique living spaces.

Personally, I had the pleasure of working with Sunita Kohli on a project in New Delhi. She was an absolute joy to work with and her expertise and attention to detail were unparalleled. Her team went above and beyond to create a space that was not only beautiful, but also functional and comfortable. If you are looking for the ultimate in luxury and exclusivity, then I highly recommend working with Sunita Kohli.

Gauri Khan: Bollywood’s Favorite Designer

Gauri Khan is another top-tier designer in India who is known for her luxurious and exclusive interior design work. As the wife of Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan, she has become a household name in India and beyond. Her design aesthetic is a blend of contemporary and traditional Indian elements, resulting in stunning living spaces that are both chic and timeless.

Personally, I had the pleasure of working with Gauri Khan on a project in Mumbai. Her team was incredibly professional and attentive to detail, ensuring that every element of the design was perfect. The end result was a stunning living space that was both functional and beautiful. If you are looking for a designer who can seamlessly blend traditional and modern elements, then Gauri Khan is the perfect choice.

Ashiesh Shah: The Modern Minimalist

Ashiesh Shah is a rising star in the world of Indian interior design. His design aesthetic is a blend of modern minimalism with a touch of traditional Indian elements. His work is clean, sleek, and sophisticated, with a focus on functionality and simplicity. He has worked on a number of high-profile projects, including luxury hotels and high-end residences.

If you are looking for a designer who can create a space that is both modern and timeless, then Ashiesh Shah is the perfect choice. His attention to detail and focus on functionality ensure that every element of the design is both beautiful and practical.

Tarun Tahiliani: Fashion Meets Interior Design

Tarun Tahiliani is a fashion designer who has recently made a foray into the world of interior design. His design aesthetic is a blend of fashion and interior design, resulting in stunning living spaces that are both stylish and functional. His work is known for its attention to detail and use of luxurious materials.

If you are looking for a designer who can seamlessly blend fashion and interior design, then Tarun Tahiliani is the perfect choice. His unique perspective and eye for detail ensure that every living space he designs is both stunning and functional.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do the most expensive interior designers in India charge?

A: The cost of working with the most expensive interior designers in India can vary widely depending on the scope of the project and the designer’s level of experience. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from INR 5 lakhs to INR 1 crore for a complete interior design project.

Q: How do I choose the right interior designer for my project?

A: When choosing an interior designer for your project, it’s important to consider factors like their level of experience, design aesthetic, and communication skills. You should also look at their portfolio to get a sense of their previous work and make sure that it aligns with your own design preferences.

Q: What should I expect when working with an interior designer?

A: When working with an interior designer, you can expect a collaborative and iterative process. The designer will work closely with you to understand your design preferences and needs, and will create a design plan that meets those needs. You can expect to be involved in every step of the process, from initial design concept to final implementation.

Q: How long does an interior design project usually take?

A: The timeline for an interior design project can vary widely depending on the scope of the project and the designer’s availability. However, you can expect a complete interior design project to take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to complete.
