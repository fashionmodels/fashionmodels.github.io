Men s Wedding Guest Outfits 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Wedding season is upon us, and it can be a daunting task to find the perfect outfit to wear as a guest. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed and end up with an outfit that doesn't quite hit the mark. In this guide, we'll be sharing our top tips and outfit ideas for men's wedding guest outfits in 2020.

As a wedding guest, you want to look great while still adhering to the dress code and not upstaging the bride and groom. It can be challenging to strike the right balance, especially if you're not used to dressing up for formal occasions.

So, what should you wear as a male wedding guest in 2020? The answer will depend on a few factors, including the time of day, the location, and the dress code specified on the invitation.

In this article, we'll be covering some general rules to follow when choosing wedding guest outfits, as well as specific outfit ideas based on different dress codes and wedding styles. We'll also be discussing color choices, accessories, and footwear options, so you'll have everything you need to look sharp and stylish at any wedding you attend this year.

Formal Wedding Attire

If the invitation specifies black tie or white tie attire, you'll need to dress in your most formal outfit. For black tie, this typically means a tuxedo with a black bow tie, while white tie requires a tailcoat, white bow tie, and formal dress shoes. If you don't own a tuxedo or tailcoat, you can usually rent one from a formalwear shop.

When choosing a tuxedo or tailcoat, opt for a classic style that will never go out of fashion. A black or midnight blue suit is always a safe bet, and you can add a touch of personal style with a patterned or textured shirt, cufflinks, or a pocket square. Make sure to wear dress shoes that are comfortable and polished, and don't forget to shine them before the big day.

Semi-Formal Wedding Attire

If the dress code is semi-formal or cocktail attire, you have a bit more flexibility in your outfit choices. A dark suit or blazer and dress pants combination is a good place to start, and you can add a tie or bow tie for a more formal look. Lighter colors and patterns are also acceptable for daytime weddings or summer weddings, but make sure to avoid anything too casual or flashy.

When choosing your accessories, opt for a statement piece like a bold tie or pocket square, but keep the rest of your outfit relatively simple. A leather belt, dress shoes, and a watch are all you need to complete the look.

Beach Wedding Attire

If you're attending a beach wedding, the dress code will likely be more relaxed than a traditional wedding. However, this doesn't mean you should show up in shorts and a t-shirt. Instead, opt for lightweight fabrics like cotton or linen, and choose light colors like beige, khaki, or pastels.

A linen shirt paired with dress shorts or chinos is a great option for a beach wedding, and you can add a pair of loafers or boat shoes for a comfortable yet stylish look. Make sure to wear sunglasses and a hat to protect yourself from the sun, and bring a light jacket or cardigan in case it gets chilly in the evening.

Casual Wedding Attire

For a casual wedding, you can afford to be a bit more adventurous with your outfit choices. A polo shirt paired with dress pants or chinos is a good option, or you can opt for a patterned button-down shirt and jeans. Make sure to choose colors and patterns that complement each other, and avoid anything too loud or distracting.

Accessories like a leather belt, watch, and dress shoes can elevate your outfit and make it look more polished. You can also add a blazer or sport coat for a more formal touch, although this may not be necessary for all casual weddings.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I wear to a summer wedding?

A: For summer weddings, opt for lightweight fabrics and light colors like beige, khaki, or pastels. A linen shirt paired with dress shorts or chinos is a great option, and you can add a pair of loafers or boat shoes for a comfortable yet stylish look.

Q: Can I wear a patterned shirt to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a patterned shirt to a wedding, but make sure to choose a pattern that complements the rest of your outfit. A subtle pattern like stripes or checks can add interest to your outfit without being too distracting.

Q: Can I wear a tie with a casual outfit?

A: Yes, you can wear a tie with a casual outfit, but make sure to choose a tie that matches the level of formality of the event. For a casual wedding, a knit tie or a cotton tie in a fun pattern can be a great addition to your outfit.

Q: Can I wear brown shoes to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear brown shoes to a wedding, but make sure they are dress shoes and not casual shoes like sneakers or sandals. Brown shoes can be a great alternative to black shoes and can add a touch of warmth to your outfit.

Conclusion of Men's Wedding Guest Outfits 2020

Choosing the perfect outfit for a wedding can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and inspiration, it's easy to look stylish and sophisticated. Whether you're attending a formal black tie event or a casual beach wedding, there's an outfit that will suit your style and the dress code. Remember to choose classic pieces that will stand the test of time, and don't be afraid to inject a bit of personality with accessories and color choices.
