Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a well-known brand in India that offers premium shirts for men. The brand is known for its elegant and sophisticated designs that are perfect for formal occasions. The logo of Van Heusen features a black shield with the brand name written in white letters. The logo represents power, authority, and sophistication, which is what the brand stands for.

Personally, I have been a fan of Van Heusen for a long time. Their shirts are of excellent quality, and the designs are perfect for any formal occasion. I love how their logo represents the brand's values and makes it stand out from other brands.

Arrow

Arrow is another famous shirt brand in India that is known for its premium quality shirts. The brand has been around for over 160 years and has a rich history. The logo of Arrow features a blue arrow, which represents the brand's name, and is known for its simplicity and elegance.

My personal experience with Arrow has been great. Their shirts are of excellent quality, and the designs are perfect for any occasion. I love how the logo represents the brand's simplicity and elegance, which is what the brand stands for.

Peter England

Peter England is a popular shirt brand in India that offers affordable shirts for men. The brand is known for its trendy designs that are perfect for casual occasions. The logo of Peter England features the brand name in bold letters with a red and black color combination. The logo represents the brand's values of affordability, style, and quality.

Peter England has been my go-to brand for casual occasions. Their shirts are stylish, affordable, and of good quality. I love how the logo represents the brand's values and makes it stand out from other brands in the market.

How to Choose the Right Brand?

Choosing the right shirt brand can be overwhelming, considering the plethora of options available in the market. Here are some tips that can help you choose the right brand:

  • Consider the occasion: Choose a brand that offers shirts suitable for the occasion you are attending.
  • Quality: Always go for a brand that offers good quality shirts.
  • Budget: Choose a brand that fits your budget.
  • Personal style: Choose a brand that aligns with your personal style.
