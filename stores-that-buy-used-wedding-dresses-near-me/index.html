Before you sell your wedding dress, there are a few things to consider. First, you should research the market to get an idea of how much your dress is worth. This will help you set a fair price and ensure that you're not overcharging or undercharging for your dress.

Second, you should ensure that your dress is clean and in good condition. Most stores that buy used wedding dresses will not accept dresses that are damaged or dirty. If your dress needs to be cleaned, you should take it to a professional cleaner before attempting to sell it.
