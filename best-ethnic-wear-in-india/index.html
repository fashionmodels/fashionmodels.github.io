The Best Ethnic Wear In India A Guide To Finding The Perfect Outfit

Written by Robby May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

India is a country rich in culture and tradition, and one of the most beautiful aspects of this culture is its clothing. Ethnic wear in India is diverse, colorful, and unique, and it can be a challenge to find the perfect outfit. Whether you are attending a wedding, festival, or simply want to add some Indian flair to your wardrobe, finding the best ethnic wear in India can be overwhelming. But don't worry, we are here to help!

When it comes to ethnic wear in India, the options are endless. From sarees and lehengas to salwar suits and kurtas, it can be difficult to know where to start. One of the biggest challenges is finding an outfit that is both beautiful and comfortable. Many traditional outfits can be heavy and bulky, making it difficult to move around and enjoy the festivities. Additionally, finding the right size and fit can be a challenge, especially if you are not familiar with Indian sizing.

The target of this article is to guide you in finding the best ethnic wear in India that is both beautiful and comfortable. We will provide tips on how to find the right size and fit, as well as recommend some of the best places to shop for ethnic wear in India.

In summary, this article will discuss the pain points of finding the best ethnic wear in India, offer tips on how to find the right size and fit, and recommend some of the best places to shop for ethnic wear in India.

Sarees: A Timeless Classic

When it comes to ethnic wear in India, sarees are a timeless classic. A saree is a long piece of fabric that is draped around the body, and it can be worn in many different styles. Sarees come in a variety of fabrics, including silk, cotton, and chiffon, and they can be embellished with embroidery, sequins, and beads.

Personally, I love wearing sarees for weddings and special occasions. There is something about the way the fabric drapes around my body that makes me feel elegant and sophisticated. One of my favorite sarees is a silk saree that my mother wore on her wedding day. It has been passed down to me, and I wear it with pride.

Lehengas: The Perfect Party Wear

Lehengas are another popular choice for ethnic wear in India. A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a matching blouse and dupatta. Like sarees, lehengas come in a variety of fabrics and styles, and they can be embellished with embroidery, sequins, and beads.

Personally, I love wearing lehengas to parties and other festive events. They are fun and flirty, and they allow me to move around and dance without feeling weighed down. One of my favorite lehengas is a vibrant pink and orange one that I wore to a friend's wedding. I received so many compliments on it, and I felt like a princess.

Salwar Suits: Comfortable and Stylish

Salwar suits are a popular choice for everyday wear in India. A salwar suit consists of a loose-fitting tunic (kameez), pants (salwar), and a scarf (dupatta). They are comfortable, stylish, and perfect for running errands or attending casual events.

One of my favorite salwar suits is a bright yellow one that I wear on lazy weekends. It is made of cotton, which makes it breathable and comfortable, and it has a fun floral print that always puts me in a good mood.

How to Find the Right Size and Fit

One of the biggest challenges of finding the best ethnic wear in India is getting the right size and fit. Indian sizing can be different from Western sizing, so it's important to take accurate measurements before making a purchase.

When shopping for sarees, lehengas, and salwar suits, make sure to measure your bust, waist, and hips. Most websites and stores will have a size chart that you can use to determine which size to order. If you are in doubt, it's always better to go up a size, as it's easier to take in a garment than to let it out.

The Best Places to Shop for Ethnic Wear in India

If you're looking for the best ethnic wear in India, there are many places to shop. Here are some of our top recommendations:

1. Fabindia: Fabindia is a popular chain of stores that sells a variety of traditional and contemporary Indian clothing. They have a wide range of sarees, lehengas, salwar suits, and kurtas, and they are known for their high-quality fabrics.

2. Myntra: Myntra is an online retailer that sells a variety of Indian clothing. They have a wide selection of sarees, lehengas, salwar suits, and kurtas, and they offer free shipping on orders over Rs. 1,000.

3. Utsav Fashion: Utsav Fashion is an online retailer that specializes in ethnic wear. They have a huge selection of sarees, lehengas, salwar suits, and kurtas, and they offer free shipping on orders over Rs. 8,000.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the most popular ethnic wear in India?

A: Sarees are one of the most popular types of ethnic wear in India. They come in a variety of styles and fabrics, and they can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

Q: What should I look for when buying ethnic wear in India?

A: When buying ethnic wear in India, it's important to look for high-quality fabrics and craftsmanship. You should also consider the occasion you will be wearing the outfit for, as well as your personal style and comfort.

Q: How can I style my ethnic wear in India?

A: You can style your ethnic wear in India in many different ways. For sarees and lehengas, you can experiment with different draping styles and accessories, such as jewelry and hair accessories. For salwar suits and kurtas, you can pair them with different types of pants and shoes to create a unique look.

Q: What is the best fabric for ethnic wear in India?

A: The best fabric for ethnic wear in India depends on the occasion and the season. Silk and chiffon are popular choices for formal events, while cotton and linen are great for everyday wear.

Conclusion of Best Ethnic Wear in India

Finding the best ethnic wear in India can be a challenge, but with the right tips and recommendations, it is possible to find the perfect outfit. Whether you choose a saree, lehenga, salwar suit, or kurta, make sure to prioritize comfort and style. By shopping at reputable retailers such as Fabindia, Myntra, and Utsav Fashion, you can ensure that you are getting high-quality garments that will last for years to come. Happy shopping!
