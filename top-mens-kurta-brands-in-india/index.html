Top Mens Kurta Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

India is known for its rich cultural heritage, and one of the most iconic pieces of clothing that reflects this heritage is the kurta. A kurta is a loose-fitting shirt that is traditionally worn by men in India. It is a comfortable and versatile garment that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. However, with so many brands offering men's kurtas in India, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this post, we will guide you through the top mens kurta brands in India, and help you find the perfect kurta for your style and budget.

If you're looking for a high-quality kurta, it's important to consider factors such as material, design, and price. Some of the pain points related to top mens kurta brands in India are that there are so many options to choose from, and it can be difficult to know which brands offer the best quality and value for money.

The target of this post is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the top mens kurta brands in India, and help you make an informed decision when it comes to choosing a kurta that suits your style and budget.

So, let's dive into the main points related to top mens kurta brands in India. In this post, we will cover the top brands, their unique features, and what sets them apart from the competition. We will also provide some tips on how to choose the perfect kurta for your needs.

1. Fabindia

As one of the most popular menswear brands in India, Fabindia offers a wide range of kurtas that are perfect for both casual and formal occasions. Their kurtas are made from high-quality materials such as cotton and silk, and feature intricate embroidery and prints that reflect India's rich cultural heritage. What sets Fabindia apart is their commitment to sustainable and ethical fashion, with many of their kurtas being made from organic cotton and other eco-friendly materials.

2. Manyavar

Manyavar is a brand that is known for its stylish and contemporary kurtas. Their kurtas feature modern designs and cuts that are perfect for young men who want to make a fashion statement. Manyavar kurtas are made from high-quality materials such as silk and cotton, and feature unique embroidery and prints that reflect the brand's commitment to modern Indian fashion.

3. W for Women

While W for Women is primarily a women's clothing brand, they also offer a wide range of kurtas for men. Their kurtas are perfect for casual occasions and feature bright colors and bold prints that are sure to make a statement. W for Women kurtas are made from high-quality materials such as cotton and silk, and are designed to be comfortable and easy to wear.

4. Raymond

Raymond is a brand that is known for its high-quality fabrics, and their kurtas are no exception. Their kurtas are made from premium materials such as linen and silk, and feature classic designs that are perfect for formal occasions. What sets Raymond apart is their attention to detail, with their kurtas featuring intricate embroidery and embellishments that reflect the brand's commitment to quality.

Personal Experience with Top Mens Kurta Brands in India

As a fashion enthusiast, I have tried out a variety of mens kurta brands in India, and have found that each brand offers its unique features and styles. However, my personal favorite is Fabindia, as their kurtas are not only stylish but also eco-friendly and sustainable. I love the intricate embroidery and prints on their kurtas, which reflect India's rich cultural heritage.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Kurta

When it comes to choosing the perfect kurta, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the occasion you will be wearing the kurta for. If it's a formal event, opt for a classic design and high-quality materials such as silk or linen. For casual occasions, you can experiment with bold prints and bright colors.

Secondly, consider the fit of the kurta. A well-fitted kurta can make all the difference, so make sure to try on a few different sizes before making a purchase. Lastly, consider the brand's commitment to sustainability and ethical fashion, especially if you want to make a conscious choice that supports the environment and local artisans.
