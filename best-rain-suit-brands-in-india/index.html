Best Rain Suit Brands In India Stay Dry And Comfortable During Monsoon

Written by Luffy Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Monsoon in India can be a delight for some and a nightmare for others. While the rains bring respite from the scorching heat, it can also lead to waterlogging, traffic snarls, and flooding. One of the biggest challenges during monsoon is staying dry and comfortable. This is where rain suits come to the rescue. However, choosing the right rain suit can be a daunting task, given the plethora of options available in the market.

If you are looking for the best rain suit brands in India, you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will help you choose the perfect rain suit that will keep you dry and comfortable during the monsoon season.

To begin with, let us summarize the main points of this article. We will be discussing the best rain suit brands in India, their features, and what makes them stand out from the rest. We will also cover the types of rain suits available in the market and how to choose the right one based on your needs and budget.

The North Face

When it comes to rain suits, The North Face is a brand that needs no introduction. The company has been in the business for over 50 years and is known for its high-quality outdoor gear. The North Face rain suits are made of Gore-Tex material, which is waterproof and breathable. This means that you will stay dry and comfortable even in the heaviest of downpours. The North Face rain suits also come with features like adjustable hoods, pit zips for ventilation, and multiple pockets for storage. The only downside is that they can be quite expensive.

Columbia Sportswear

Columbia Sportswear is another popular brand when it comes to rain suits. Their rain suits are made of Omni-Tech material, which is also waterproof and breathable. Columbia rain suits come with features like adjustable cuffs, hoods, and waistbands, and are available in a variety of colors and sizes. Columbia rain suits are more affordable than The North Face, making them a popular choice among budget-conscious buyers.

Frogg Toggs

If you are looking for a rain suit that is both lightweight and affordable, then Frogg Toggs is the brand for you. Their rain suits are made of a patented material called DriPore, which is waterproof and breathable. Frogg Toggs rain suits are available in a range of colors and sizes and come with features like adjustable hoods, cuffs, and waistbands. The only downside is that they may not be as durable as the other brands mentioned above.

How to Choose the Right Rain Suit

Choosing the right rain suit can be a confusing task, given the variety of options available in the market. Here are a few factors to consider when choosing a rain suit:

Material: Look for rain suits that are made of waterproof and breathable material like Gore-Tex or Omni-Tech.

Features: Look for rain suits that come with features like adjustable hoods, cuffs, and waistbands, as well as pit zips for ventilation and multiple pockets for storage.

Size: Make sure to choose a rain suit that fits you well and allows for layering if needed.

Budget: Rain suits can range from affordable to expensive, so choose one that fits your budget.

Question and Answer

Q. Which rain suit brand is the most affordable?

A. Frogg Toggs rain suits are the most affordable among the brands mentioned in this article.

Q. Which rain suit brand is the most durable?

A. The North Face rain suits are known for their durability, but they are also the most expensive.

Q. Can rain suits be worn while riding a bike?

A. Yes, rain suits can be worn while riding a bike. Look for rain suits that come with reflective strips for visibility in low light conditions.

Q. How do I clean my rain suit?

A. Follow the care instructions mentioned on the label of your rain suit. Most rain suits can be washed in a washing machine using a mild detergent.

Conclusion of Best Rain Suit Brands in India

Choosing the right rain suit can make all the difference during the monsoon season. We hope this article has helped you choose the perfect rain suit that will keep you dry and comfortable. Remember to consider factors like material, features, size, and budget when choosing a rain suit. Happy monsoon!
