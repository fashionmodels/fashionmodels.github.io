The most popular car brand in South Korea is Hyundai. Founded in 1967, the company has grown to become one of the world's largest automakers and is a household name in South Korea. The History of Hyundai

Hyundai was founded in 1967 by Chung Ju-yung and started as a construction company before branching out into the automotive industry. The company's first car, the Pony, was released in 1975 and was a huge success, becoming the best-selling car in Canada in 1985. Since then, Hyundai has continued to grow and has become one of the world's largest automakers, with a presence in over 190 countries.
