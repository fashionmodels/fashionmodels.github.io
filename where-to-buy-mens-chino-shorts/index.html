Where To Buy Men s Chino Shorts A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to update your wardrobe with some stylish and comfortable chino shorts. But where do you even start looking for the perfect pair? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the best places to buy men's chino shorts, and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right pair for you.

Shopping for men's chino shorts can be daunting, especially if you're not sure what you're looking for. You may be overwhelmed with choices, unsure of which stores sell the best quality shorts, or simply unsure of what style suits you best. These pain points can make shopping for chino shorts a frustrating experience.

There are many options available when it comes to buying men's chino shorts. You can visit brick-and-mortar stores, or shop online. You can buy from high-end designer brands or budget-friendly options. Ultimately, the best place for you to buy chino shorts depends on your budget, style preferences, and personal shopping habits.

In summary, when it comes to buying men's chino shorts, you have plenty of options. You can choose to buy from a physical store or shop online, and there are plenty of brands and price points to choose from. The key is to find a store that fits your budget, style preferences, and offers good quality shorts.

Department Stores and Retailers

Department stores and retailers are a great option for buying men's chino shorts. They offer a wide selection of brands and styles, and you can try the shorts on in-store to ensure a good fit. Some popular department stores and retailers that sell men's chino shorts include Macy's, Nordstrom, J.Crew, and Gap.

When shopping at department stores and retailers, keep an eye out for sales and promotions. You can often find great deals on chino shorts during the summer months. Additionally, consider signing up for the store's loyalty program to receive discounts and exclusive offers.

Online Retailers

If you prefer to shop online, there are plenty of options for buying men's chino shorts. Online retailers offer convenience and a wider selection of brands and styles. Some popular online retailers that sell men's chino shorts include Amazon, Zappos, and ASOS.

When shopping online, be sure to check the store's return policy and sizing chart. It's important to ensure a good fit and be able to return the shorts if they don't meet your expectations. Additionally, consider reading reviews from other customers to get a better idea of the quality and fit of the shorts.

Specialty Stores

If you're looking for a specific style or brand of chino shorts, specialty stores may be your best bet. These stores offer a more curated selection of shorts and often carry higher-end brands. Some popular specialty stores that sell men's chino shorts include Bonobos, Club Monaco, and Brooks Brothers.

When shopping at specialty stores, be prepared to spend more money on chino shorts. However, the quality and fit of the shorts may be worth the investment. Additionally, consider signing up for the store's email list to receive notifications about sales and promotions.

Thrift Stores and Consignment Shops

If you're on a tight budget or enjoy the thrill of the hunt, consider shopping for men's chino shorts at thrift stores and consignment shops. These stores often offer gently used clothing at a fraction of the cost of new clothing. You may be able to find unique styles or vintage shorts that you wouldn't find elsewhere.

When shopping at thrift stores and consignment shops, be prepared to spend some time searching for the right pair of shorts. Additionally, be sure to thoroughly inspect the shorts for any signs of wear or damage before purchasing.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying men's chino shorts?

A: When buying men's chino shorts, consider the fit, material, and style. Look for shorts that fit comfortably around the waist and thigh, and are made from a breathable material like cotton or linen. Consider your personal style and choose a pair of shorts that reflect your taste.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on men's chino shorts?

A: The cost of men's chino shorts can vary greatly depending on the brand and quality. Budget-friendly options can be found for under $20, while higher-end brands can cost upwards of $200. On average, you can expect to spend between $40-$80 on a pair of chino shorts.

Q: Can I wear chino shorts to work?

A: It depends on your workplace's dress code. Some workplaces allow chino shorts as part of a casual dress code, while others require more formal attire. Check with your HR department or supervisor to determine if chino shorts are appropriate for your workplace.

Q: How do I care for my men's chino shorts?

A: To care for your men's chino shorts, machine wash them in cold water and hang them to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as these can damage the fabric. Iron the shorts on a low heat setting if necessary.

Conclusion of Where to Buy Men's Chino Shorts

Shopping for men's chino shorts doesn't have to be a stressful experience. By considering your personal style, budget, and shopping habits, you can find the perfect pair of shorts for your summer wardrobe. Whether you choose to shop at a department store, online retailer, specialty store, or thrift shop, there are plenty of options available to suit your needs. Happy shopping!
