Discover The Best Vintage Menswear Shops In London

Written by Daniel Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of vintage menswear? Do you find it challenging to find the best vintage menswear shops in London? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you through the best vintage menswear shops in London and help you find your perfect fit.

If you are a lover of vintage fashion, you know how difficult it is to find the right pieces. Vintage menswear shops in London can be overwhelming, and it can take hours of searching to find what you are looking for. It is frustrating to invest so much time and energy into finding the perfect item, only to come up short.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best vintage menswear shops in London. We want to save you time and effort by providing you with a list of the top shops in London.

In summary, this article will provide you with a guide to the best vintage menswear shops in London. We will give you an overview of each shop, their location, price range, and what they specialize in. We will also include a personal experience of each shop and highlight some of their best items.

The East End Thrift Store

The East End Thrift Store is one of the best vintage menswear shops in London. It is located in the heart of East London and offers a wide range of vintage clothing at affordable prices. They specialize in vintage denim, leather jackets, and military wear.

I stumbled upon this shop while walking around the East End and was amazed by the range of clothing they had on offer. I found a vintage leather jacket for only £30, which was a steal. The staff was friendly and helpful, and I left the store feeling satisfied with my purchase.

Brick Lane Market

Brick Lane Market is one of the most well-known markets in London, and for a good reason. It is a hub for vintage clothing and offers a wide range of vintage menswear. The market is open on Sundays, and you can find anything from vintage t-shirts to suits.

My experience at Brick Lane Market was overwhelming, to say the least. The sheer number of stalls and shops was daunting, and it took me a while to navigate my way around. However, once I found my bearings, I was able to find some great vintage pieces at reasonable prices.

Blackout II

Blackout II is a vintage menswear shop located in Covent Garden. It offers a range of vintage clothing, including formal wear, suits, and accessories. The shop has been around since the 1970s and has a vast collection of vintage clothing.

My experience at Blackout II was memorable. The shop was organized, and the staff was knowledgeable about their stock. I found a vintage tuxedo that fit me perfectly and was impressed by the quality of the clothing. The prices were on the higher side, but it was worth it for the quality of the clothing.

How to Shop for Vintage Menswear in London

If you are new to vintage shopping, it can be overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you navigate vintage menswear shops in London:

  • Do your research: Look up vintage menswear shops in London before you go to save time.
  • Know your size: Vintage clothing sizes may differ from modern sizes, so it's essential to know your measurements before you shop.
  • Check for quality: Inspect the clothing for any defects or signs of wear and tear.
  • Try on everything: Vintage clothing may fit differently, so it's essential to try on everything before purchasing.
  • Don't be afraid to negotiate: Prices can be negotiable in vintage shops, so don't be afraid to ask for a lower price.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Menswear Shops in London

London is home to some of the best vintage menswear shops in the world. From Brick Lane Market to Blackout II, there is something for everyone. When shopping for vintage menswear, it's essential to do your research, know your size, check for quality, try on everything, and don't be afraid to negotiate. Happy shopping!
