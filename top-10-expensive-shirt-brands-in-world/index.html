1. Brioni

Brioni is an Italian luxury fashion brand that specializes in menswear. It was founded in Rome in 1945 and is known for its handmade suits and shirts. The brand's shirts are made from the finest cotton and silk fabrics, and the attention to detail is evident in every stitch. Brioni's shirts are priced at an average of $1,000.

Why are these shirt brands so expensive?
