How to Determine the Right Fit?

If you're unsure about whether to buy standard or wide fit shoes Clarks, you can measure your foot size at home. To do this, you'll need a piece of paper, a pen, and a ruler. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Trace Your Foot

Place a piece of paper on the floor and stand on it. Trace the outline of your foot using a pen or pencil.

Step 2: Measure Your Foot

Using a ruler, measure the width of the ball of your foot and the heel. Compare the measurements with the size chart provided by the shoe manufacturer to determine the right fit.

Step 3: Try Them On

Once you've determined the right fit, try on the shoes before making a purchase. Walk around in them for a few minutes to ensure that they're comfortable and don't cause any discomfort.
