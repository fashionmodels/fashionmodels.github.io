Best Men's Swimsuits for Lap Swimming: Jammer Style

One of the most popular swimwear styles for lap swimming is the jammer. Jammers are similar to cycling shorts in design, and they offer a lot of benefits for serious swimmers. They are designed to be form-fitting, which helps to reduce drag in the water and improve performance. Additionally, they are made from durable materials that can withstand the wear and tear of regular use.

Personally, I have been using the Speedo Jammer for years and have found it to be the best suit for lap swimming. It provides excellent support and is comfortable to wear for long periods of time. The Speedo Jammer is also designed to be chlorine-resistant, which helps to extend its lifespan.

Best Men's Swimsuits for Lap Swimming: Brief Style

Another popular swimwear style for lap swimming is the brief. Briefs are similar in design to traditional underwear and are known for their minimal coverage. They are popular with competitive swimmers who want to reduce drag in the water and improve their performance.

Personally, I have found the TYR Durafast Elite Solid Racer to be the best brief for lap swimming. It is made from a blend of polyester and spandex, which provides the right level of support and comfort. Additionally, it is designed to be both chlorine and fade-resistant, which helps to extend its lifespan.

Choosing the Right Swimsuit for Your Needs

When choosing a swimsuit for lap swimming, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you want to make sure that the suit is comfortable and fits well. Look for a suit that is designed specifically for lap swimming, as this will ensure that it provides the right level of support and performance.

Additionally, you want to consider the material that the suit is made from. Look for a suit that is made from durable materials that can withstand the wear and tear of regular use. Lastly, consider your personal style and preferences. There are a wide variety of swimwear styles available, so choose the one that makes you feel the most confident and comfortable.

Tips for Maintaining Your Swimsuit

Once you have found the perfect swimsuit for lap swimming, it is important to take care of it properly. To extend the lifespan of your suit, rinse it thoroughly after each use and hang it to dry in a cool, dry place. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, as these can damage the fabric. Additionally, avoid leaving your suit in direct sunlight for extended periods of time, as this can cause fading and damage to the fabric.

Best Men's Swimsuits for Lap Swimming: Square Leg Style

The square leg style is another popular option for lap swimming. These suits are similar in design to traditional shorts, but with a shorter inseam. They offer more coverage than briefs or jammers, but still provide a streamlined fit that reduces drag in the water.

Personally, I have found the Nike Men's Swim Performance Square Leg to be the best square leg suit for lap swimming.
