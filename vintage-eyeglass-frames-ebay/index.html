How to Care for Vintage Eyeglass Frames

When caring for vintage eyeglass frames, it is important to handle them with care. Avoid dropping or scratching the frames, and store them in a case when not in use. Additionally, clean the frames with a soft cloth and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.
