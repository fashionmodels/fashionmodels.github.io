Mens Eastern Clothing Brands In Pakistan A Guide To The Best

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to mens eastern clothing brands in Pakistan, there are a lot of options available. However, finding the right brand that offers high-quality clothing, great designs, and affordable prices can be a daunting task. If you're looking to upgrade your wardrobe with some stylish eastern wear, then this guide is for you.

The target audience of this article is men who are interested in buying eastern clothing from Pakistan but are not sure where to start. It will provide them with a list of the best brands and a brief overview of each one.

In this article, we will be discussing the top mens eastern clothing brands in Pakistan, their specialties, and why they are worth checking out.

Mens Eastern Clothing Brands in Pakistan: Khaadi

When it comes to eastern clothing brands in Pakistan, Khaadi is a name that needs no introduction. Founded in 1998, Khaadi is known for its unique prints, high-quality fabrics, and affordable prices. Whether you're looking for a casual kurta or a formal sherwani, Khaadi has got you covered. They also offer a wide range of accessories, including shoes, bags, and jewelry.

Personally, I have always been a fan of Khaadi's designs. Their clothes are stylish and comfortable, and they never compromise on quality. I recently bought a kurta from Khaadi, and I must say, I was impressed with the fit and the feel of the fabric.

Mens Eastern Clothing Brands in Pakistan: Junaid Jamshed

Another popular eastern clothing brand in Pakistan is Junaid Jamshed. Founded by the late singer and religious scholar Junaid Jamshed, this brand is known for its traditional designs and high-quality fabrics. They offer a range of eastern wear, including kurtas, shalwar kameez, and waistcoats.

I have personally bought several kurtas from Junaid Jamshed, and I must say, I have never been disappointed. Their clothes are well-designed, comfortable, and durable. However, their prices can be a bit on the higher side, so it's always a good idea to wait for their sales.

Mens Eastern Clothing Brands in Pakistan: Gul Ahmed

Gul Ahmed is another popular eastern clothing brand in Pakistan. Founded in 1953, this brand is known for its high-quality fabrics and elegant designs. They offer a wide range of eastern wear, including kurtas, shalwar kameez, and sherwanis.

What I love about Gul Ahmed is the attention to detail they put into their designs. Their clothes are not only stylish but also comfortable to wear. I recently bought a sherwani from Gul Ahmed for a wedding, and I must say, it was the highlight of my wardrobe. However, their prices can be a bit steep, so it's always a good idea to wait for their sales.

Mens Eastern Clothing Brands in Pakistan: Alkaram Studio

Alkaram Studio is a popular eastern clothing brand in Pakistan that offers a wide range of designs and styles. Founded in 1986, this brand is known for its unique prints and affordable prices. They offer a range of eastern wear, including kurtas, shalwar kameez, and waistcoats.

What I like about Alkaram Studio is the variety of designs they offer. Whether you're looking for a simple kurta or a fancy sherwani, they have got you covered. Their clothes are also affordable, which makes them a popular choice among the masses.

Question and Answer

Q: Which is the best mens eastern clothing brand in Pakistan?

A: There is no one best brand when it comes to mens eastern clothing in Pakistan. However, some of the most popular and well-known brands include Khaadi, Junaid Jamshed, Gul Ahmed, and Alkaram Studio.

Q: What should I look for when buying mens eastern clothing in Pakistan?

A: When buying mens eastern clothing in Pakistan, you should look for high-quality fabrics, good designs, and affordable prices. It's also important to consider the occasion you're buying the clothes for and the weather conditions.

Q: Where can I buy mens eastern clothing in Pakistan?

A: You can buy mens eastern clothing in Pakistan from a variety of places, including brand outlets, local markets, and online stores. It's always a good idea to do some research before making a purchase to ensure you're getting the best deal.

Q: How do I take care of my mens eastern clothing?

A: To take care of your mens eastern clothing, it's important to follow the care instructions on the label. In general, you should hand wash your clothes in cold water and hang them to dry. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach as they can damage the fabric.

Conclusion of Mens Eastern Clothing Brands in Pakistan

When it comes to mens eastern clothing brands in Pakistan, there are a lot of options available. However, by doing some research and shopping around, you can find the right brand that offers high-quality clothing, great designs, and affordable prices. Whether you're looking for a casual kurta or a formal sherwani, the brands mentioned in this article are definitely worth checking out.
