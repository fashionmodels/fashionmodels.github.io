Top 10 Leather Bag Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you in search of the perfect leather bag but not sure where to start? With so many options available, choosing the right brand can be a daunting task. In this guide, we will break down the top 10 leather bag brands in India, highlighting their unique features and benefits to help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to leather bags, quality is key. Investing in a high-quality leather bag can last a lifetime and elevate any outfit. Whether you are looking for a classic handbag, a functional backpack, or a stylish tote, these top 10 leather bag brands in India offer a wide range of options to suit any style and occasion.

In this guide, we will cover the top 10 leather bag brands in India, their history, craftsmanship, and signature styles. We will also highlight their unique features, such as eco-friendly materials, ethical production practices, and customization options.

1. Hidesign

Founded in 1978 by Dilip Kapur, Hidesign is one of India's most iconic leather bag brands. Known for its rustic, vintage-inspired designs, Hidesign offers a wide range of bags, wallets, and accessories made from high-quality leather and eco-friendly materials.

My personal experience with Hidesign has been exceptional. I purchased a leather backpack for my travels, and it has held up beautifully over the years. The leather has developed a beautiful patina, and the bag has plenty of pockets and compartments to keep everything organized.

2. Da Milano

Da Milano is a luxury leather goods brand that was founded in 1989. Known for its sleek, minimalist designs, Da Milano offers a wide range of bags, wallets, and accessories made from the finest leather and crafted by skilled artisans.

I recently purchased a Da Milano leather tote for work, and it has quickly become my go-to bag. The leather is buttery soft, and the design is both functional and stylish. I love the attention to detail, such as the gold hardware and the embossed logo.

3. Baggit

Baggit is a vegan leather bag brand that was founded in 1990. Known for its affordable, trendy designs, Baggit offers a wide range of bags, wallets, and accessories made from high-quality faux leather and eco-friendly materials.

As someone who is conscious about animal welfare and the environment, I love Baggit's commitment to sustainability. I purchased a Baggit shoulder bag for everyday use, and it has held up beautifully. The faux leather looks and feels like the real thing, and the bag has plenty of pockets and compartments to keep everything organized.

4. Caprese

Caprese is a women's handbag brand that was launched in 2012. Known for its chic, feminine designs, Caprese offers a wide range of handbags, totes, satchels, and clutches made from high-quality synthetic leather and crafted with attention to detail.

I recently purchased a Caprese handbag for a wedding, and it was the perfect accessory to my outfit. The bag was lightweight and spacious enough to hold all my essentials, and the gold hardware added a touch of glamour.

5. Esbeda

Esbeda is a luxury handbag brand that was founded in 2006. Known for its glamorous, high-fashion designs, Esbeda offers a wide range of bags, clutches, and wallets made from premium materials and crafted with precision.

I purchased an Esbeda clutch for a special occasion, and it was the perfect complement to my outfit. The clutch was beautifully crafted, with intricate beading and embroidery, and the materials felt luxurious to the touch.

6. Satya Paul

Satya Paul is a designer brand that was founded in 1985. Known for its unique, artistic designs, Satya Paul offers a wide range of handbags, totes, and clutches made from high-quality materials and crafted with creativity.

I recently purchased a Satya Paul tote for a creative project, and it was the perfect canvas to showcase my artwork. The tote was spacious enough to hold all my supplies, and the design was eye-catching and unique.

7. Holii

Holii is a women's handbag brand that was launched in 2009. Known for its vibrant, colorful designs, Holii offers a wide range of bags, totes, and clutches made from high-quality leather and crafted with a fusion of traditional and modern techniques.

My personal experience with Holii has been delightful. I purchased a Holii crossbody bag for a trip, and it was the perfect accessory to my outfit. The bag was lightweight and easy to carry, and the design was playful and fun.

8. Lino Perros

Lino Perros is a fashion accessories brand that was founded in 1999. Known for its affordable, trendy designs, Lino Perros offers a wide range of bags, wallets, and accessories made from high-quality materials and crafted with attention to detail.

I recently purchased a Lino Perros wallet for everyday use, and it has held up beautifully. The materials feel sturdy and durable, and the design is both functional and stylish.

9. Lavie

Lavie is a women's handbag brand that was launched in 2010. Known for its chic, trendy designs, Lavie offers a wide range of handbags, totes, satchels, and clutches made from high-quality materials and crafted with a fusion of style and comfort.

I purchased a Lavie handbag for work, and it has become my go-to bag. The bag is spacious enough to hold all my essentials, and the design is both professional and stylish. I love the attention to detail, such as the gold hardware and the embossed logo.

10. The House of Tara

The House of Tara is a backpack brand that was founded in 2012. Known for its bohemian, eclectic designs, The House of Tara offers a wide range of backpacks, duffels, and totes made from high-quality materials and crafted with attention to detail.

I recently purchased a House of Tara backpack for a weekend getaway, and it was the perfect companion. The backpack was spacious enough to hold all my essentials, and the design was colorful and playful.

Conclusion of Top 10 Leather Bag Brands in India

Choosing the right leather bag brand can be a challenge, but with this guide to the top 10 leather bag brands in India, you can make an informed decision. Whether you prefer classic, vintage-inspired designs or trendy, colorful styles, these top brands offer something for everyone. With a focus on quality, craftsmanship, and sustainability, these brands are sure to elevate any outfit and stand the test of time.
