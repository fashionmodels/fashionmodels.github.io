Mens Ethnic Wear Brands In India A Guide For Fashion Forward Men

Written by Oliver Aug 23, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to dressing up for special occasions or cultural events, men often struggle to find the right outfit that is both stylish and comfortable. Finding the perfect mens ethnic wear can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available in the market. From sherwanis to kurtas, there are countless styles to choose from, which can make the decision-making process overwhelming.

If you're someone who is looking to up their ethnic wear game, then look no further. In this post, we will be discussing the top mens ethnic wear brands in India, and what makes them stand out in the market.

Our goal is to help you find the right brand that caters to your preferences, style, and budget. So, let's get started!

Mens Ethnic Wear Brands in India: Fabindia

As one of the most popular mens ethnic wear brands in India, Fabindia offers a wide range of traditional Indian wear for men. From kurtas to jackets, their collection is a fusion of traditional and modern styles that cater to all age groups. What makes Fabindia stand out is their use of natural and sustainable fabrics, which adds a unique touch to their outfits.

Personally, I have always been a big fan of Fabindia's kurtas. They are comfortable, stylish, and perfect for any occasion. Whenever I wear one of their kurtas, I always receive compliments from my friends and family.

Mens Ethnic Wear Brands in India: Manyavar

Manyavar is another popular brand that offers a wide range of mens ethnic wear. Their collection includes sherwanis, kurtas, jackets, and accessories that are perfect for weddings, festivals, and other special occasions. What makes Manyavar stand out is their attention to detail and quality, which is reflected in their outfits.

Personally, I have worn Manyavar's sherwanis to multiple weddings, and they never fail to impress. They are comfortable, stylish, and perfect for dancing the night away.

What to Look for in a Mens Ethnic Wear Brand in India

When looking for the perfect mens ethnic wear brand in India, there are a few factors to consider. Firstly, the brand should offer a wide range of styles that cater to different occasions and preferences. Secondly, the quality of the fabric and attention to detail should be top-notch, as this can make or break the outfit. Finally, the brand should offer outfits that are comfortable and easy to wear, as this can make a huge difference in how you feel throughout the day.

How to Style Mens Ethnic Wear

Styling mens ethnic wear can be a bit tricky, especially if you are new to it. However, there are a few tips that can help you create a stylish and comfortable outfit. Firstly, invest in a good pair of shoes that complement your outfit. Secondly, accessorize your outfit with a watch or bracelet to add a touch of personality. Finally, experiment with different styles and colors to find what suits you best.

Mens Ethnic Wear Brands in India: Raymond

Raymond is another popular brand that offers a wide range of mens ethnic wear. Their collection includes kurtas, sherwanis, and suits that are perfect for formal occasions. What makes Raymond stand out is their attention to detail and quality, which is reflected in their outfits.

Personally, I have worn Raymond's suits to multiple formal events, and they always make me feel confident and stylish.
