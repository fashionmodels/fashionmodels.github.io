What To Wear To A Christening 2021 Uk The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Christenings are special occasions that require us to dress appropriately. Finding the perfect outfit can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we will give you some tips on what to wear to a christening in 2021 in the UK.

Attending a christening can be a daunting task, especially if it's your first time. You want to dress appropriately without being too formal or too casual. Finding the right balance can be a challenge, but with our guide, you'll be able to find the perfect outfit without breaking a sweat.

What to Wear to a Christening 2021 UK: The Basics

When it comes to what to wear to a christening 2021 UK, there are a few basics to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to avoid wearing white as it's traditionally reserved for the baby being christened or the godparents. You also want to avoid anything too revealing or flashy. A christening is a religious event, and you don't want to draw attention away from the ceremony.

For women, a knee-length dress or skirt paired with a blouse is a safe bet. You can also wear a trouser suit or jumpsuit if you prefer. Just make sure that it's not too tight or revealing. For men, a suit and tie or smart trousers and a blazer are appropriate.

Accessorizing for a Christening

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so it's important to choose them wisely. For women, a small clutch bag and simple jewelry are ideal. You can also add a fascinator or hat if you want to dress up your outfit. For men, a watch and leather shoes are perfect. You can also add a pocket square or tie clip to complete your look.

What Shoes to Wear to a Christening

Choosing the right shoes is essential when it comes to what to wear to a christening 2021 UK. For women, closed-toe heels or flats are ideal. Avoid anything too high or strappy. For men, leather shoes are a must. Make sure they're clean and polished before the event.

What Not to Wear to a Christening

When it comes to what not to wear to a christening, there are a few things to keep in mind. Avoid anything too revealing or flashy, as we mentioned earlier. You also want to avoid anything too casual, such as jeans or trainers. Lastly, avoid anything that could potentially offend, such as t-shirts with slogans or offensive graphics.

Personal Experience

When I attended my first christening, I was unsure of what to wear. I ended up wearing a knee-length dress with a cardigan and closed-toe heels. I accessorized with a small clutch bag and simple jewelry. I felt comfortable and confident in my outfit, and it was appropriate for the occasion.

Dressing for Different Types of Christenings

There are different types of christenings, and each one requires a different dress code. For a traditional christening, you'll want to dress more formally than you would for a modern christening. For a baptism, you'll want to dress more conservatively than you would for a dedication. It's important to research the type of christening you'll be attending and dress accordingly.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a christening?

A: Black is generally not recommended for christenings as it's traditionally associated with funerals. However, if you must wear black, make sure to add some color with your accessories.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a christening?

A: No, jeans are too casual for a christening. Stick to smart trousers or a knee-length dress or skirt.

Q: Can I wear a fascinator to a christening?

A: Yes, you can wear a fascinator to a christening. Just make sure it's not too large or distracting.

Q: Can I wear sandals to a christening?

A: Sandals are generally not recommended for a christening. Stick to closed-toe shoes such as heels or flats.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Christening 2021 UK

Attending a christening is a special occasion that requires us to dress appropriately. Remember to avoid wearing white, anything too revealing or flashy, and anything too casual. Stick to smart trousers, knee-length dresses or skirts, and closed-toe shoes. Accessorize with small clutch bags, simple jewelry, and hats or fascinators if you wish. With our guide, you'll be able to find the perfect outfit for any type of christening in 2021 in the UK.
