How Much Does Wedding Suit Hire Cost

Written by Eveline Oct 12, 2023 · 4 min read

On your wedding day, you want to look your best. One key element of your appearance is the suit that you wear. However, buying a suit can be expensive, and you may not want to wear the same suit again. This is where suit hire comes in. But how much does wedding suit hire cost? It can be a pain point for many couples, especially those on a tight budget.

The cost of wedding suit hire varies depending on several factors, such as the style of the suit, the number of suits needed, and the location of the hire company. On average, you can expect to pay between £70 and £150 per suit.

To save money, it’s a good idea to shop around and compare prices from different hire companies. You may also be able to get a discount if you hire multiple suits or if you book well in advance.

In summary, wedding suit hire can cost between £70 and £150 per suit depending on the style, number of suits, and location of the hire company. To save money, shop around and consider booking in advance or hiring multiple suits.

How Much Does Wedding Suit Hire Cost? A Personal Experience

When my husband and I were planning our wedding, we knew we wanted to look our best. However, we didn’t want to spend a fortune on new suits that we might never wear again. We decided to look into suit hire instead.

We found a local hire company that offered a range of styles at reasonable prices. We decided on a classic navy suit for my husband and a grey suit for his groomsmen. The total cost for five suits was £550, which worked out at £110 per suit.

We were pleased with the quality of the suits and the service we received from the hire company. The suits looked great on the day, and we felt confident and comfortable in them.

Factors That Affect the Cost of Wedding Suit Hire

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect the cost of wedding suit hire. These include:

Style of the Suit

The style of the suit can affect the cost of hire. For example, a classic three-piece suit may be more expensive than a simple two-piece suit.

Number of Suits

The more suits you need to hire, the more expensive it will be. Some hire companies offer discounts for multiple hires.

Location

The location of the hire company can also affect the cost. Companies in larger cities may charge more than those in smaller towns.

Tips for Saving Money on Wedding Suit Hire

Here are some tips for saving money on wedding suit hire:

  • Shop around and compare prices from different hire companies
  • Consider booking well in advance
  • Hire multiple suits to get a discount
  • Choose a simple style of suit to keep costs down

Is It Cheaper to Buy or Hire a Wedding Suit?

Buying a suit can be more expensive than hiring one, especially if you want a high-quality suit. However, if you plan to wear the suit again, buying may be the better option in the long run. It’s important to weigh up the costs and benefits of each option before making a decision.

Question and Answer

Q: How many suits do I need to hire for my wedding?

A: The number of suits you need to hire will depend on the size of your wedding party. You will need a suit for the groom, best man, ushers, and any fathers or grandfathers who are attending the wedding.

Q: Can I get a discount for hiring multiple suits?

A: Yes, some hire companies offer discounts for multiple hires. It’s worth asking when you are getting a quote.

Q: How far in advance should I book wedding suit hire?

A: It’s a good idea to book wedding suit hire as early as possible, especially if you are getting married during peak wedding season. Six months to a year in advance is recommended.

Q: Are there any hidden costs with wedding suit hire?

A: It’s important to check the terms and conditions of your hire agreement to ensure there are no hidden costs, such as cleaning fees or late return fees.

Conclusion of How Much Does Wedding Suit Hire Cost

Wedding suit hire can cost between £70 and £150 per suit, depending on the style, number of suits, and location of the hire company. To save money, it’s a good idea to shop around, book in advance, and consider hiring multiple suits. By doing so, you can look your best on your wedding day without breaking the bank.
