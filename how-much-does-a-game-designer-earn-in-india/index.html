How Much Does A Game Designer Earn In India

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a game designer in India? It's important to know what to expect in terms of salary. The video game industry is growing rapidly in India, but it can be challenging to determine how much you can expect to earn as a game designer.

Game design is a highly competitive field, and salaries can vary depending on a variety of factors, such as experience, education, and location. Additionally, the type of company you work for can also affect your salary, as well as the type of games you design.

According to Payscale, the average salary for a game designer in India is around ₹439,695 per year. However, this can range from ₹195,000 to ₹1,600,000 depending on the factors mentioned above.

In summary, game design can be a lucrative career in India, but it's important to keep in mind that salaries can vary widely depending on your experience, education, and location, as well as the type of company and games you work on.

My Personal Experience

As a game designer in India with five years of experience, I have found that my salary has steadily increased over time. When I first started out, I was earning around ₹300,000 per year, but now I am making over ₹1,000,000 per year.

However, it's important to note that I have been working for a large game development company located in a major city, which has allowed me to earn a higher salary. Additionally, I have a degree in game design and have worked on a variety of successful games, which has helped me to increase my salary over time.

Factors that Affect Game Designer Salaries in India

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect how much a game designer earns in India. Here are a few of the most important:

Experience:

The more experience you have as a game designer, the higher your salary is likely to be. This is because experience demonstrates that you have the skills and knowledge necessary to design successful games.

Education:

Holding a degree in game design or a related field can also help to increase your salary. This demonstrates that you have a strong foundation of knowledge and skills related to game design.

Location:

Game designer salaries can vary depending on where you live and work in India. Salaries are generally higher in major cities, such as Mumbai, Bangalore, and Delhi, where the cost of living is higher.

Type of Company:

The type of company you work for can also affect your salary. Large game development companies typically offer higher salaries than smaller companies and indie game studios.

How to Increase Your Game Designer Salary in India

If you're interested in increasing your game designer salary in India, there are a few things you can do:

- Gain experience by working on a variety of successful games
- Stay up-to-date on the latest game design trends and technologies
- Consider getting a degree in game design or a related field
- Network with other game designers to learn about new job opportunities
- Consider working for a large game development company in a major city

Conclusion of How Much Does a Game Designer Earn in India

In conclusion, game design can be a rewarding and lucrative career in India. However, it's important to keep in mind that salaries can vary depending on a variety of factors, such as experience, education, location, and the type of company you work for. By gaining experience, staying up-to-date on the latest industry trends, and working for a large game development company, you can increase your chances of earning a higher salary as a game designer in India.
