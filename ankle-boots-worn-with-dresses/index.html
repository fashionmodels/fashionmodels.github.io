Ankle Boots Worn With Dresses A Complete Guide

Written by William Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Looking for a way to add some edge to your dress game? Look no further than ankle boots! This versatile shoe style can take your outfit from sweet to sophisticated in a snap. But finding the right way to style ankle boots with dresses can be tricky. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to rock this trend with confidence.

Have you ever struggled with deciding what shoes to wear with a dress? It can be especially challenging when it comes to ankle boots. You want to show off your stylish boots, but you don't want them to clash with the dress. And then there's the question of what kind of dress works best with ankle boots. It's enough to make you want to stick to flats or heels. But don't worry! With the right tips, you can make ankle boots work with any dress style.

The target of ankle boots worn with dresses is to create a stylish and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going for a casual daytime look or dressing up for a night out, ankle boots can take your outfit to the next level. Plus, they're a great way to add some variety to your shoe collection.

So, what are the key things to keep in mind when styling ankle boots with dresses? First, consider the length and style of the dress. Knee-length or midi dresses are a great choice for ankle boots, as they show off just the right amount of ankle. Maxi dresses can be tricky, as they can overwhelm the boots. Stick to a simple, streamlined ankle boot style to avoid looking too busy. Second, think about the occasion. For a casual daytime look, pair ankle boots with a flowy sundress or denim dress. For a dressier event, opt for a fitted dress and heeled ankle boots.

Pairing Ankle Boots with a Maxi Dress

When it comes to styling ankle boots with a maxi dress, it's all about balance. You want to show off your boots without overwhelming the dress. Stick to a simple, streamlined ankle boot style in a neutral color. Black or brown leather ankle boots are a great choice. Avoid boots with too much embellishment or hardware, as they can clash with the dress. A maxi dress with a high-low hemline is a great choice, as it will show off just the right amount of ankle. You can also try a side-slit maxi dress for a more daring look.

Pairing Ankle Boots with a Midi Dress

Midi dresses are the perfect length for ankle boots. They show off just the right amount of ankle while still providing coverage. For a casual daytime look, pair ankle boots with a flowy midi dress in a floral print or pastel color. For a dressier occasion, opt for a fitted midi dress and heeled ankle boots. A midi dress with a slit or asymmetrical hemline is a great choice, as it will show off your boots while still maintaining a sophisticated look.

Choosing the Right Ankle Boot Style

When it comes to choosing the right ankle boot style, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, consider the occasion. For a casual daytime look, opt for a flat or low-heeled ankle boot in a neutral color. For a dressier occasion, choose a heeled ankle boot in a sleek black or brown leather. Second, think about the dress style. A flowy sundress pairs well with a boho-inspired ankle boot with fringe or embroidery. A fitted dress calls for a more streamlined ankle boot style.

Tips for Wearing Ankle Boots with Dresses

Here are a few additional tips for wearing ankle boots with dresses:

  • Choose a boot that hits at or just above the ankle for the most flattering look.
  • Stick to a neutral color like black, brown, or grey for the most versatility.
  • Avoid ankle boots with too much embellishment or hardware, as they can clash with the dress.
  • Play with textures by pairing suede ankle boots with a flowy dress or a leather ankle boot with a fitted dress.

Personal Experience with Ankle Boots Worn with Dresses

When I first started experimenting with ankle boots and dresses, I was hesitant. I worried that the boots would make my legs look shorter or that they would clash with the dress. But once I found the right pair of ankle boots, I was hooked. I love how they add a bit of edge to even the most feminine dress. My go-to look is a midi dress with a slit paired with heeled ankle boots. It's the perfect mix of sophisticated and edgy.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a mini dress?

A: Absolutely! Ankle boots and mini dresses are a match made in heaven. Just be sure to choose a boot style that complements the dress. For a casual look, opt for a flat ankle boot in a neutral color. For a night out, choose a heeled ankle boot in a sleek black or brown leather.

Q: What kind of tights should I wear with ankle boots and dresses?

A: It depends on the dress style and the occasion. For a casual daytime look, try sheer black tights with ankle boots and a flowy dress. For a dressier event, opt for opaque black tights with heeled ankle boots and a fitted dress.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a cocktail dress?

A: Yes! Ankle boots can add an unexpected edge to a cocktail dress. Choose a sleek black or brown leather ankle boot with a high heel for a dressier occasion. For a more casual look, try ankle boots with a flowy cocktail dress in a floral print or pastel color.

Q: How do I know if my ankle boots and dress are clashing?

A: To avoid clashing, stick to a neutral color palette and avoid boots with too much embellishment or hardware. If you're unsure, try pairing the dress and boots together and taking a photo. Sometimes seeing the outfit from a different perspective can help you determine if it works or not.

Conclusion of Ankle Boots Worn with Dresses

Ankle boots are a stylish and versatile shoe style that can take any dress to the next level. By following these tips and tricks, you can rock this trend with confidence. Whether you're going for a casual daytime look or dressing up for a night out, ankle boots are the perfect way to add some edge to your outfit.
