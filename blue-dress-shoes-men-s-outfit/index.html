How To Style Blue Dress Shoes For Men

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old black dress shoes to every formal event? Do you feel like your shoe game is lacking a certain pop of color? If you're looking for a way to spice up your formal outfits, blue dress shoes might be just what you need.

Blue dress shoes can be a tricky item to pull off, especially if you're not used to wearing colorful footwear. Finding the right outfit to pair them with and making sure they don't clash with the rest of your ensemble can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered with this guide on how to style blue dress shoes for men.

The target of this guide is to help men who want to add a bit of color to their formal outfits and stand out from the crowd. Whether you're attending a wedding, a business meeting, or a fancy dinner party, blue dress shoes can give your outfit a unique and stylish touch.

In summary, this guide will cover the following:

  • Why blue dress shoes are a great addition to your wardrobe
  • How to pair blue dress shoes with different outfits
  • The dos and don'ts of wearing blue dress shoes
  • Tips for buying the perfect pair of blue dress shoes

Blue Dress Shoes for Formal Events

I remember the first time I wore blue dress shoes to a formal event. I was nervous about standing out too much and looking like a clown, but as soon as I arrived at the venue, I received compliments left and right. The blue shoes not only looked great, but they also made me feel more confident and stylish.

So, how can you pull off the same look? When it comes to formal events, you want to keep your outfit classy and elegant. A navy blue suit paired with light blue dress shoes can create a monochromatic look that is both sophisticated and eye-catching. If you don't want to go all out with a blue suit, you can also pair blue dress shoes with a grey suit or a black suit with a blue tie. Just make sure to keep the rest of your accessories simple and understated.

Blue Dress Shoes for Casual Outfits

Blue dress shoes aren't just for formal events, they can also be incorporated into your casual outfits. A pair of blue suede dress shoes can add a pop of color to a simple denim and t-shirt combo. You can also pair blue leather dress shoes with khaki pants and a white button-up shirt for a dressier casual look. Just remember to keep the rest of your outfit casual and relaxed to balance out the boldness of the shoes.

Choosing the Right Shade of Blue

When it comes to blue dress shoes, there are a variety of shades to choose from. Navy blue is a safe and versatile option that can be paired with almost any outfit. Light blue is a bolder choice that can add a unique touch to your outfit. Cobalt blue is a more daring option that can make a statement. When choosing the right shade of blue, consider the occasion and the rest of your outfit. If you're wearing a neutral-colored suit, you can go for a brighter shade of blue. If you're wearing a colorful outfit, you might want to stick to a more muted shade of blue.

Caring for Your Blue Dress Shoes

Once you've found the perfect pair of blue dress shoes, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last for years to come. Make sure to clean them regularly with a soft cloth and shoe polish. If they get wet, let them dry naturally and avoid exposing them to direct sunlight. You can also use a waterproof spray to protect them from the elements.

Conclusion of Blue Dress Shoes Men's Outfit

Blue dress shoes can be a stylish and unique addition to your wardrobe, whether you're dressing up for a formal event or dressing down for a casual outing. Just remember to choose the right shade of blue, pair them with the right outfit, and take good care of them. With these tips, you'll be rocking blue dress shoes like a pro in no time.

Question and Answer

Q: Can blue dress shoes be worn with a black suit?

A: Yes, blue dress shoes can be worn with a black suit, but make sure to keep the rest of your accessories simple and understated.

Q: What color socks should I wear with blue dress shoes?

A: You can either wear socks that match the color of your pants or go for a bold contrast with a pair of patterned or brightly colored socks.

Q: Can blue dress shoes be worn with jeans?

A: Yes, blue dress shoes can be worn with jeans, but make sure to keep the rest of your outfit casual and relaxed.

Q: Are blue dress shoes appropriate for a job interview?

A: It depends on the company and the dress code. If you're unsure, it's best to stick to more traditional black or brown dress shoes.
