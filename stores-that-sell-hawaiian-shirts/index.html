FAQs

Q: How do I know what size Hawaiian shirt to buy?
A: It's important to check the sizing chart for the specific store you're buying from, as sizes can vary between brands. Additionally, you may want to consider trying on a few different sizes to see which one fits you best.

Q: Can I wear a Hawaiian shirt to a formal event?
A: While Hawaiian shirts are generally considered to be more casual, there are some dressier options available that can be worn to more formal events. However, it's always a good idea to check the dress code before wearing a Hawaiian shirt to a formal event.

Q: How do I care for my Hawaiian shirt?
A: It's important to follow the care instructions on the label of your Hawaiian shirt, as different fabrics may require different care. In general, you'll want to wash your shirt in cold water and hang it up to dry to avoid shrinking or damaging the fabric.

Q: What accessories should I wear with my Hawaiian shirt?
A: Hawaiian shirts are generally worn with more casual accessories, such as sunglasses, sandals, and shorts. However, you can also dress up your Hawaiian shirt with more formal accessories, such as dress shoes and a blazer.
