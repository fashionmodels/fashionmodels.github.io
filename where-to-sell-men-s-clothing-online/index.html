Where To Sell Men s Clothing Online A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to declutter your closet or make some extra cash, selling your gently used men's clothing online can be a great option. However, with so many platforms to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to sell your items. In this guide, we'll explore the best places to sell men's clothing online and help you determine which platform is right for you.

When it comes to selling men's clothing online, there are several factors to consider. You'll want to think about the type of items you're selling, your target audience, and the fees and commission each platform charges. Additionally, you'll want to consider the ease of use and convenience of each platform.

There are several popular platforms to sell men's clothing online, including Poshmark, eBay, Grailed, and Depop. Each platform has its own unique features and target audience, so it's important to understand the differences between them before choosing where to sell your items.

Poshmark

Poshmark is a popular platform for selling men's and women's clothing, shoes, and accessories. With over 70 million users, Poshmark is a great option if you're looking to reach a large audience. The platform is easy to use, and sellers can create listings in just a few minutes.

One of the unique features of Poshmark is the ability to bundle items. This means that buyers can purchase multiple items from the same seller and receive a discount on their total purchase. Additionally, Poshmark offers a flat shipping rate of $7.11 for items under 5 pounds, which makes shipping easy and affordable for both sellers and buyers.

eBay

eBay is a well-known platform for buying and selling a wide variety of items, including men's clothing. With over 185 million active buyers, eBay offers a massive audience for sellers. eBay is also known for its bidding system, which allows buyers to bid on items and potentially score a great deal.

However, eBay does charge fees for both listing items and selling them. Additionally, eBay can be a bit more complicated to use than some other platforms, so it may not be the best option for beginners.

Grailed

Grailed is a platform specifically designed for buying and selling men's clothing. The platform is known for its high-end and designer items, so it's a great option if you're selling luxury men's clothing. Grailed also offers a "wardrobe consultation" service, where sellers can receive personalized advice on pricing and selling their items.

However, Grailed does charge a 6% commission fee on all sales, which is higher than some other platforms. Additionally, because the platform is focused on high-end items, it may not be the best option if you're selling more affordable men's clothing.

Depop

Depop is a platform that combines elements of social media and e-commerce. With a focus on vintage and unique items, Depop is a great option if you're selling one-of-a-kind men's clothing. The platform is very user-friendly, and sellers can easily create listings and engage with potential buyers.

However, Depop does charge a 10% commission fee on all sales, which is higher than some other platforms. Additionally, because the platform is focused on vintage and unique items, it may not be the best option if you're selling more mainstream men's clothing.

Additional Options

In addition to the platforms listed above, there are several other options for selling men's clothing online. Some of these include Mercari, ThreadUp, and Vinted. Each platform has its own unique features and target audience, so it's important to do your research before choosing where to sell your items.

Tips for Selling Men's Clothing Online

Now that you know where to sell your men's clothing online, here are a few tips to help you make the most of your selling experience:

  • Take high-quality photos: Make sure your photos are well-lit and show the item from multiple angles.
  • Write a detailed description: Be sure to include the brand, size, and any flaws or imperfections.
  • Price competitively: Do some research to see what similar items are selling for and price your items accordingly.
  • Ship quickly: Buyers appreciate fast shipping, so try to ship your items within 1-2 business days.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I determine the best platform to sell my men's clothing?

A: Consider factors such as the type of items you're selling, your target audience, and the fees and commission each platform charges.

Q: Can I sell all types of men's clothing on these platforms?

A: Each platform has its own guidelines on what types of items can be sold. Be sure to read the guidelines carefully before creating your listings.

Q: How do I price my items competitively?

A: Do some research to see what similar items are selling for and price your items accordingly. You can also offer discounts or bundle items to encourage buyers to make a purchase.

Q: How do I get paid for my sales?

A: Each platform has its own payment system, but most will either deposit funds directly into your bank account or send you a check or PayPal payment.

Conclusion of Where to Sell Men's Clothing Online

Now that you know where to sell men's clothing online, it's time to start decluttering your closet and making some extra cash. Consider the factors we've discussed and choose the platform that's best for your needs. With a little effort and some great photos, you'll be on your way to a successful selling experience.
