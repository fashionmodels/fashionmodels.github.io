Best Sustainable Jewelry Brands A Guide To Ethical And Eco Friendly Jewelry

Written by Luffy Oct 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for jewelry that is not only beautiful but also sustainable? Do you want to support brands that use ethical and eco-friendly practices in their production? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best sustainable jewelry brands that you can feel good about wearing.

What are Sustainable Jewelry Brands?

Sustainable jewelry brands are those that prioritize ethical and eco-friendly practices in their production process. This includes using recycled materials, sourcing from responsible suppliers, and ensuring fair labor practices. These brands are committed to reducing their environmental impact and promoting social responsibility.

When you purchase jewelry from sustainable brands, you can feel good about your purchase knowing that it was made with care for people and the planet.

Our Top Picks for Best Sustainable Jewelry Brands

1. Mejuri - This Canadian brand uses recycled silver and gold in their designs and sources their gemstones from ethical suppliers. They also have a focus on fair labor practices and transparency in their production process.

2. Clean Origin - If you're looking for sustainable engagement or wedding rings, Clean Origin is a great option. They use lab-grown diamonds and recycled metals in their designs, ensuring a conflict-free and eco-friendly process.

3. Pandora - This popular brand has committed to using 100% recycled silver and gold by 2025. They also prioritize responsible sourcing of their gemstones and have a focus on reducing their carbon footprint.

4. Article22 - Article22 creates beautiful jewelry from materials sourced from bombs dropped in Laos during the Vietnam War. Not only do they repurpose these materials into stunning designs, but they also support local artisans and communities in Laos.

Why Choose Sustainable Jewelry?

Choosing sustainable jewelry is a great way to reduce your environmental impact and promote ethical practices in the fashion industry. By supporting brands that prioritize sustainability, you can help shift the industry towards more responsible practices.

Additionally, sustainable jewelry is often made with higher quality materials and production processes, resulting in pieces that are not only beautiful but also durable and long-lasting.

How to Shop for Sustainable Jewelry

When shopping for sustainable jewelry, look for brands that prioritize eco-friendly and ethical practices. This can include using recycled materials, sourcing from responsible suppliers, and ensuring fair labor practices.

Additionally, do your research and read reviews from other customers to ensure that the brand you are purchasing from is truly committed to sustainability and ethical practices.

FAQs About Sustainable Jewelry Brands

Q: What materials are commonly used in sustainable jewelry?
A: Sustainable jewelry can be made from a variety of materials, including recycled metals and gemstones, ethically sourced materials, and even repurposed materials like recycled glass or bombs. Q: How can I ensure that a brand is truly sustainable?
A: Do your research and read reviews from other customers to ensure that the brand you are purchasing from is truly committed to sustainability and ethical practices. Q: Is sustainable jewelry more expensive than traditional jewelry?
A: Sustainable jewelry can be more expensive than traditional jewelry due to the higher quality materials and production processes used. However, investing in sustainable jewelry can be a great way to promote responsible practices and reduce your environmental impact. Q: How can I recycle my old jewelry?
A: Many sustainable jewelry brands offer recycling programs for old or unwanted jewelry. Additionally, you can look into local recycling programs or donate your old jewelry to organizations that repurpose materials for new designs.

Conclusion of Best Sustainable Jewelry Brands

Investing in sustainable jewelry is a great way to promote ethical and eco-friendly practices in the fashion industry. By choosing brands that prioritize sustainability, you can make a positive impact on the environment and support responsible production processes.
