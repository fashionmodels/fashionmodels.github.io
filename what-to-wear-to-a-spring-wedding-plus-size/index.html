What To Wear To A Spring Wedding Plus Size The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Spring is here, and wedding season is upon us! But for plus-size women, finding the perfect outfit for a spring wedding can be a daunting task. You want to look stylish and chic, but also comfortable and confident. Don't worry - we've got you covered. In this post, we'll show you how to choose the perfect outfit for a spring wedding, no matter your size or shape.

When it comes to what to wear to a spring wedding plus size, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose an outfit that is appropriate for the occasion. This means avoiding anything too casual or too formal. Second, you want to choose an outfit that flatters your figure and makes you feel confident and beautiful. And third, you want to choose an outfit that is comfortable enough to wear all day and dance the night away.

So, what should you wear to a spring wedding plus size? Here are a few tips:

First, consider the dress code. If the wedding invitation specifies a dress code, be sure to follow it. For a spring wedding, the dress code is usually semi-formal or cocktail attire. This means you should wear a dress that falls just above or below the knee, or a dressy pantsuit or jumpsuit. Avoid anything too short or too revealing.

Second, choose the right fabric. For a spring wedding, you want to choose a lightweight and breathable fabric, such as cotton or chiffon. Avoid anything too heavy or too clingy.

Third, accessorize wisely. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and flatter your figure. For example, if you're wearing a dress, choose a pair of heels that elongate your legs. If you're wearing pants, choose a statement necklace or earrings that draw attention to your face.

Finally, don't forget about the details. A great outfit is all about the details, so pay attention to the little things. Make sure your hair and makeup are on point, and don't forget to bring a clutch or small purse to hold your essentials.

Choosing the Perfect Dress for a Spring Wedding Plus Size

Choosing the perfect dress for a spring wedding plus size can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. Here are a few tips:

First, choose a dress that flatters your figure. Look for a dress that nips in at the waist and flares out at the hips, creating an hourglass shape. Avoid anything too tight or too loose.

Second, choose a dress in a flattering color. For spring, pastels and bright colors are always a good choice. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Third, choose a dress with the right neckline. For a spring wedding, a V-neck or scoop neck is always a flattering choice. Avoid anything too low-cut or too high-necked.

Choosing the Perfect Pantsuit or Jumpsuit for a Spring Wedding Plus Size

If you're not a fan of dresses, don't worry - there are plenty of other options for a spring wedding plus size. A pantsuit or jumpsuit can be just as stylish and chic. Here are a few tips:

First, choose a pantsuit or jumpsuit that flatters your figure. Look for a style that nips in at the waist and flares out at the hips, creating an hourglass shape. Avoid anything too boxy or too tight.

Second, choose a pantsuit or jumpsuit in a flattering color. For spring, pastels and bright colors are always a good choice. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Third, accessorize wisely. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and flatter your figure. For example, if you're wearing a pantsuit, choose a statement necklace or earrings that draw attention to your face.

Styling Tips for a Spring Wedding Plus Size

Here are a few additional styling tips to keep in mind when choosing what to wear to a spring wedding plus size:

First, choose comfortable shoes. You'll be on your feet all day and night, so choose shoes that you can wear comfortably for hours.

Second, choose the right undergarments. Choose undergarments that flatter your figure and don't show through your outfit.

Third, don't be afraid to layer. Spring weather can be unpredictable, so bring a light jacket or wrap just in case.

Where to Shop for a Spring Wedding Plus Size

If you're wondering where to shop for a spring wedding plus size, here are a few options:

First, check out plus-size retailers like Lane Bryant, Torrid, and Ashley Stewart. They offer a great selection of dresses, pantsuits, and jumpsuits in a range of sizes and styles.

Second, check out department stores like Nordstrom and Macy's. They offer a great selection of plus-size formalwear, including dresses, pantsuits, and jumpsuits.

Third, check out online retailers like ASOS and Eloquii. They offer a great selection of plus-size formalwear that you can order from the comfort of your own home.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a floral dress to a spring wedding?

A: Yes, floral dresses are a great choice for a spring wedding. Just be sure to choose a dress in a flattering silhouette and a color that complements your skin tone.

Q: Can I wear a maxi dress to a spring wedding?

A: Yes, maxi dresses can be a great choice for a spring wedding. Just be sure to choose a dress in a lightweight and breathable fabric, and avoid anything too casual or too revealing.

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to a spring wedding?

A: Yes, jumpsuits can be a great choice for a spring wedding. Just be sure to choose a jumpsuit in a flattering silhouette and a color that complements your skin tone.

Q: Can I wear white to a spring wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing white to a wedding, as it's traditionally the color of the bride's dress. Instead, opt for a pastel or bright-colored dress or jumpsuit.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Spring Wedding Plus Size

Choosing the perfect outfit for a spring wedding plus size can be a challenge, but with these tips, you'll be able to find a stylish and chic outfit that flatters your figure and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to choose an outfit that is appropriate for the occasion, choose the right fabric and accessories, and pay attention to the little details. Happy shopping!
