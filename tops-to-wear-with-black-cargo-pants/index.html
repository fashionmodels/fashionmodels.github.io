Tops To Wear With Black Cargo Pants A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Black cargo pants are a versatile piece of clothing that can be styled in numerous ways. However, choosing the right top to wear with black cargo pants can be tricky, especially if you want to look stylish and put-together. In this guide, we’ll share some tips on how to choose the perfect top to wear with black cargo pants and create a chic and trendy outfit.

The Pain Points of Choosing a Top for Black Cargo Pants

One of the main pain points of choosing a top to wear with black cargo pants is finding the right balance between comfort and style. Cargo pants are usually associated with a relaxed and casual look, but you don’t want to look too frumpy or unkempt. Another challenge is finding a top that complements the utilitarian style of cargo pants without making your outfit look too busy or cluttered.

Choosing the Perfect Top for Black Cargo Pants

The key to choosing the perfect top for black cargo pants is to find a balance between comfort, style, and functionality. Here are some tips on how to achieve that:

Opt for a Fitted Top

If you want to balance out the loose and baggy silhouette of cargo pants, opt for a fitted top. A fitted t-shirt, crop top, or tank top can create a sleek and streamlined look that complements the utilitarian style of cargo pants. Plus, a fitted top can accentuate your curves and create a feminine touch to your outfit.

Try a Oversized Sweater or Hoodie

On the other hand, if you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, you can pair your black cargo pants with an oversized sweater or hoodie. This can create a cozy and comfortable outfit that is perfect for running errands, lounging at home, or going for a walk in a chilly day.

Layer with a Denim Jacket or Leather Jacket

If you want to add some edge and sophistication to your outfit, you can layer your black cargo pants with a denim jacket or a leather jacket. A denim jacket can create a vintage and retro look that complements the utilitarian style of cargo pants, while a leather jacket can add some toughness and attitude to your outfit.

Add Some Pop of Color or Pattern

If you want to add some interest and personality to your outfit, you can add some pop of color or pattern to your top. A bright-colored t-shirt, a printed blouse, or a graphic tee can create a fun and playful look that contrasts with the understated and neutral style of black cargo pants.

A Personal Experience with Tops to Wear with Black Cargo Pants

As a fashion blogger, I’ve experimented with various tops to wear with black cargo pants and found that a fitted crop top and a denim jacket are my go-to options. The fitted crop top creates a flattering and feminine silhouette that balances out the baggy and utilitarian style of cargo pants, while the denim jacket adds some texture and interest to the outfit. I also like to accessorize with some gold jewelry and a pair of sneakers to create a chic and trendy look that is perfect for a casual day out.

FAQs About Tops to Wear with Black Cargo Pants

Q: Can I wear a blazer with black cargo pants?

A: Yes, you can wear a blazer with black cargo pants for a more formal and polished look. Opt for a fitted blazer that complements the utilitarian style of cargo pants, and pair it with a simple top and some heels for a chic and sophisticated outfit.

Q: What shoes should I wear with black cargo pants?

A: You can wear various shoes with black cargo pants, depending on the occasion and your personal style. Sneakers, ankle boots, combat boots, and sandals are some of the options that can create a stylish and comfortable look. Avoid wearing high heels or stilettos, as they can clash with the casual and relaxed style of cargo pants.

Q: Can I wear a crop top with black cargo pants?

A: Yes, you can wear a crop top with black cargo pants for a trendy and playful look. Opt for a high-waisted cargo pants that covers your belly button, and pair it with a fitted or loose crop top that shows a bit of skin. You can also layer a denim jacket or a cardigan over the crop top for some coverage and warmth.

Q: What accessories can I wear with black cargo pants?

A: You can wear various accessories with black cargo pants, depending on your personal style and the occasion. Some options include a statement necklace, a pair of sunglasses, a cross-body bag, a scarf, or a hat. Make sure to choose accessories that complement the style and color of your black cargo pants, and avoid wearing too much jewelry or accessories that can overwhelm your outfit.

Conclusion of Tops to Wear with Black Cargo Pants

Choosing the perfect top to wear with black cargo pants can be challenging, but with these tips and suggestions, you can create a stylish and comfortable outfit that suits your personal style and occasion. Whether you prefer a fitted top, a cozy sweater, a layered look, or some pop of color, there are endless ways to style black cargo pants and create a chic and trendy outfit.
