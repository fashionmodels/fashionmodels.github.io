Levi's

Levi's is a popular brand of jeans that has been around since the 1800s. The brand is known for its high-quality denim and excellent craftsmanship. Levi's jeans are available in a variety of styles and fits, such as skinny, straight, and bootcut. The price range for Levi's jeans in India is between Rs. 2,000 to Rs. 8,000.

I have been a fan of Levi's jeans for a long time. They are comfortable, durable, and look great. I usually go for the classic 501 style, which is a straight fit. Levi's jeans are worth the investment as they last for a long time and do not lose their shape after multiple washes.

Wrangler is another popular brand of jeans that is known for its durability and style. Wrangler jeans are available in a variety of fits, such as slim, regular, and relaxed. Wrangler jeans are an excellent investment for anyone looking for high-quality jeans that last long and look great.
