Discover The Best Of All Time Netflix Series

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you feeling overwhelmed by the sheer amount of content on Netflix? Are you having trouble deciding what to watch next? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the best of all time Netflix series that are sure to keep you entertained for hours on end.

With so many options available, it can be difficult to know where to start. You may find yourself scrolling endlessly through the selection, unable to make a decision. It's a common problem, and one that we've all experienced. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best of all time Netflix series to watch.

So, what are the best of all time Netflix series? The answer to that question is subjective, as everyone has different tastes and preferences. However, there are some shows that have captured the hearts of audiences around the world and have become instant classics. These shows have earned critical acclaim and have become cultural touchstones.

In this article, we'll be exploring some of the best of all time Netflix series and what makes them so special. We'll be taking a closer look at the characters, storylines, and themes that have made these shows so popular with audiences. So, sit back, relax, and get ready to discover your next binge-worthy show.

Stranger Things

One of the most popular Netflix series of all time is Stranger Things. This science fiction horror show is set in the 1980s and follows a group of friends as they try to save their town from supernatural forces. The show has been praised for its nostalgic feel, strong characters, and thrilling plot.

As a huge fan of the show, I can attest to its greatness. The characters are all well-developed and have their own unique personalities. The storyline is engaging and keeps you on the edge of your seat throughout the entire series. The show also has a great soundtrack that perfectly captures the feel of the 80s.

The Crown

The Crown is a historical drama that follows the life of Queen Elizabeth II from her early reign to the present day. The show has been praised for its attention to detail, strong performances, and stunning visuals.

As someone who is not typically a fan of historical dramas, I was hesitant to watch The Crown. However, I was quickly drawn in by the characters and their stories. The show does an excellent job of humanizing the royal family and showing the struggles they face both personally and politically.

Narcos

Narcos is a crime drama that tells the story of the rise and fall of the Medellin drug cartel in Colombia. The show has been praised for its gritty realism, strong performances, and gripping storyline.

As a fan of true crime, I was immediately drawn to Narcos. The show does an excellent job of balancing the action and suspense with the emotional depth of the characters. It also provides a fascinating look into the world of drug trafficking and the impact it has on society.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a crime drama that follows the journey of a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer. The show has been praised for its complex characters, engaging storyline, and stunning cinematography.

As someone who was late to the Breaking Bad party, I was blown away by the show. The characters are all flawed and complex, which makes them feel real and relatable. The show also has some of the most memorable scenes in television history, including the famous "I am the one who knocks" speech.

Conclusion of Best of All Time Netflix Series

There you have it, some of the best of all time Netflix series that are sure to keep you entertained for hours on end. Whether you're a fan of science fiction, historical dramas, crime thrillers, or anything in between, there's something for everyone on Netflix. So, grab some popcorn, settle in, and get ready to discover your new favorite show.
