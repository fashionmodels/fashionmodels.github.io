Materials

When it comes to materials, there are a few options to consider. Cotton is a classic choice that is breathable and comfortable, but it can sometimes lose its shape over time. Choosing the right size of underwear is crucial for comfort and fit. The best way to determine your size is to measure your waist and hips, and then consult the brand's size chart. It's also important to consider the style of underwear you're buying, as different styles may fit differently.
