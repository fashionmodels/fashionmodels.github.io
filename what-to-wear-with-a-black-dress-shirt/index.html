What To Wear With A Black Dress Shirt A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Black dress shirts are a staple in any wardrobe, but finding the right outfit to pair with them can be a daunting task. You want to look stylish and put-together, but not overdo it. In this post, we will guide you on what to wear with a black dress shirt to make you look your best.

Many people struggle with what to wear with a black dress shirt. It can be challenging to find the right combination of colors and patterns to complement the shirt without it looking too somber.

The best way to pair a black dress shirt is with light-colored pants such as beige, khaki, or white. This combination creates a striking contrast that is both stylish and sophisticated. You can also opt for dark-colored pants such as navy or black, but this combination is best suited for formal events.

What to Wear with a Black Dress Shirt: Casual Look

For a casual look, pair your black dress shirt with light-colored jeans and sneakers. This combination is perfect for a day out with friends or a casual date. You can also add a denim jacket or a bomber jacket to complete the look.

What to Wear with a Black Dress Shirt: Formal Look

For a formal look, pair your black dress shirt with a black or navy suit and dress shoes. This combination is perfect for weddings, business meetings, and other formal events. You can also add a tie or a pocket square to complete the look.

Accessories to Pair with a Black Dress Shirt

When it comes to accessories, it's best to keep it simple. A black dress shirt pairs well with a classic watch, a leather belt, and a pair of sunglasses. You can also add a simple necklace or bracelet to add a touch of personality to your outfit.

How to Choose the Right Shoes to Pair with a Black Dress Shirt

Choosing the right shoes to pair with a black dress shirt can be tricky. For a casual look, sneakers or loafers are a great option. For a formal look, dress shoes such as oxfords or brogues are a better choice. Make sure that the shoes match the color of your pants for a cohesive look.

What to Wear with a Black Dress Shirt: Night Out Look

For a night out look, pair your black dress shirt with dark-colored pants such as black or navy and dress shoes. You can also add a blazer or a leather jacket to complete the look. Accessorize with a watch and a pair of sunglasses.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a black dress shirt with jeans?

A: Yes, you can pair a black dress shirt with light-colored jeans for a casual look.

Q: What color pants should I wear with a black dress shirt?

A: Light-colored pants such as beige, khaki, or white create a striking contrast with a black dress shirt. For a formal look, you can opt for dark-colored pants such as navy or black.

Q: What shoes should I wear with a black dress shirt?

A: For a casual look, sneakers or loafers are a great option. For a formal look, dress shoes such as oxfords or brogues are a better choice.

Q: Can I wear a tie with a black dress shirt?

A: Yes, you can add a tie or a pocket square to your formal look when pairing with a black dress shirt.

Conclusion of What to Wear with a Black Dress Shirt

Pairing a black dress shirt can be a challenge, but with the right combination of colors and patterns, you can create a stylish and sophisticated look. Remember to keep it simple and choose accessories that complement your outfit. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, a black dress shirt is a versatile piece that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.
