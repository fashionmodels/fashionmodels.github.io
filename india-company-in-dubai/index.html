Setting Up An India Company In Dubai A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you an entrepreneur looking to expand your business to Dubai? Do you want to set up an India company in Dubai but don't know where to start? Setting up a company in a foreign country can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a smooth and hassle-free process.

The process of setting up an India company in Dubai can be complex and overwhelming. You may face challenges such as understanding the legal requirements, navigating the local laws, and finding the right business partners. However, with the right support and guidance, you can overcome these challenges and successfully set up your business in Dubai.

Setting up an India company in Dubai is a strategic decision for many entrepreneurs who want to take advantage of the city's booming economy and strategic location. Dubai is an attractive destination for foreign investors due to its business-friendly environment, tax incentives, and strategic location.

Why Set Up an India Company in Dubai?

As a global business hub, Dubai offers numerous benefits to foreign investors. Some of the key benefits of setting up an India company in Dubai include:

1. Tax Incentives: Dubai offers tax incentives to foreign investors, making it an attractive destination for business owners. The city has no corporate or personal income taxes, no value-added tax (VAT), and no withholding tax.

2. Strategic Location: Dubai is strategically located between Europe, Africa, and Asia, making it an ideal location for businesses looking to expand their global reach. The city has a world-class infrastructure, including modern airports, seaports, and road networks, making it easy to transport goods and services.

3. Business-Friendly Environment: Dubai has a business-friendly environment that encourages entrepreneurship and innovation. The city has a transparent legal system, a low crime rate, and a stable political environment, making it a safe and secure place to do business.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up an India Company in Dubai

Setting up an India company in Dubai involves several steps. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you navigate the process:

Step 1: Choose Your Business Activity and Legal Structure

Before setting up your India company in Dubai, you need to choose your business activity and legal structure. The legal structure you choose will depend on the type of business you want to set up and your long-term goals. You can choose from several legal structures, including:

- Free Zone Company

- Limited Liability Company (LLC)

- Branch Office

How to Choose Your Business Activity and Legal Structure?

Choosing your business activity and legal structure requires careful consideration. You need to research the market, your competitors, and the legal requirements for each legal structure. You should also consult with a legal expert to help you make an informed decision.

Step 2: Register Your Business

Once you have chosen your business activity and legal structure, you need to register your business with the Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED). You will need to submit the necessary documents, including your business plan, passport copies, and other legal documents.

How to Register Your Business?

You can register your business online or in person at the DED. The registration process can take up to two weeks, depending on the legal structure you choose.

Step 3: Obtain Your Business License

After registering your business, you need to obtain your business license. The type of license you need will depend on your business activity and legal structure. You can choose from several types of licenses, including:

- Commercial License

- Professional License

- Industrial License

How to Obtain Your Business License?

You can obtain your business license from the DED. The process can take up to two weeks, depending on the type of license you choose.

Step 4: Open a Corporate Bank Account

After obtaining your business license, you need to open a corporate bank account in Dubai. This will allow you to conduct transactions, receive payments, and pay bills.

How to Open a Corporate Bank Account?

You can open a corporate bank account in Dubai by contacting a local bank. You will need to submit the necessary documents, including your business license, passport copies, and other legal documents.

Conclusion of India Company in Dubai

Setting up an India company in Dubai can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a smooth and hassle-free process. Dubai offers numerous benefits to foreign investors, including tax incentives, a strategic location, and a business-friendly environment. By following the step-by-step guide outlined above, you can successfully set up your India company in Dubai and take advantage of the city's booming economy.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the minimum capital required to set up an India company in Dubai?
A. The minimum capital required to set up an India company in Dubai depends on the legal structure you choose. For example, to set up an LLC, you need a minimum capital of AED 300,000.

Q. What are the benefits of setting up a company in a Dubai Free Zone?
A. Setting up a company in a Dubai Free Zone offers numerous benefits, including 100% ownership, tax exemptions, and no customs duties.

Q. Do I need a local sponsor to set up an India company in Dubai?
A. If you choose to set up an LLC or a branch office, you will need a local sponsor. However, if you choose to set up a Free Zone company, you do not need a local sponsor.

Q. How long does it take to set up an India company in Dubai?
A. The time it takes to set up an India company in Dubai depends on the legal structure you choose and the complexity of your business. However, the process can take anywhere from two weeks to several months.
