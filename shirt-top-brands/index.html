1. Levi's

Levi's is a popular brand that offers high-quality denim shirts for both men and women. They are known for their classic designs and durable fabrics that can last for years. I've personally owned a denim shirt from Levi's for over three years, and it still looks new.

Levi's offers a variety of denim shirt styles, from classic button-downs to oversized shirts. They also offer different denim washes, so you can choose the one that matches your style. If you're looking for a comfortable and stylish denim shirt, Levi's is the brand to go for.

2. Uniqlo

Uniqlo is a Japanese brand that offers affordable and comfortable clothing for both men and women. They are known for their simple and minimalist designs that can match any style. Uniqlo offers a variety of shirt styles, from basic t-shirts to dress shirts.

One of my favorite shirts from Uniqlo is their Supima Cotton Crew Neck t-shirt. It's made from high-quality cotton that feels soft and comfortable on the skin. The shirt also has a slim fit design that flatters the body shape. If you're looking for affordable and comfortable shirts, Uniqlo is the brand to check out.

J.Crew is a well-known brand that offers classic and stylish clothing for both men and women. They are known for their preppy and timeless designs that can match any occasion. J.Crew offers a variety of shirt styles, from button-downs to polos.

The shirt also has a classic fit design that looks flattering on any body shape. If you're looking for classic and stylish shirts, J.Crew is the brand to go for.

4. Patagonia

Patagonia is a brand that offers high-quality outdoor clothing for both men and women. They are known for their sustainable and eco-friendly practices that prioritize the environment. Patagonia offers a variety of shirt styles, from t-shirts to flannels.

Ralph Lauren is a luxury brand that offers high-end clothing for both men and women. They are known for their iconic polo shirts and classic designs that can match any occasion. Ralph Lauren offers a variety of shirt styles, from dress shirts to t-shirts.
