What to Wear to a Funeral 2021 Summer: A Guide to Dressing Appropriately

Funerals are a time for mourning and showing respect to the deceased and their loved ones. It can be difficult to know what to wear to a funeral, especially in the summer when the weather is hot and humid. You want to look respectful and appropriate, but you also want to be comfortable.

When it comes to what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer, the most important thing is to dress modestly and professionally. You want to show respect for the deceased and their family, and dressing appropriately is one way to do that.

Here are some guidelines on what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer:

Wear Dark Colors

When it comes to what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer, black is the most traditional color to wear. However, if you don't have anything black in your wardrobe, you can wear other dark colors such as navy blue, dark grey, or deep purple. Avoid wearing bright colors or patterns, as they can be seen as disrespectful.

Avoid Casual Clothing

When attending a funeral, you should avoid wearing casual clothing such as jeans or t-shirts. Opt for more formal attire such as a suit, dress pants and a button-up shirt, or a dress or skirt and blouse. Make sure your clothing is clean and well-fitted.

Choose Comfortable Fabric

Summer weather can be hot and humid, so you want to choose clothing made of lightweight, breathable fabric such as cotton or linen. Avoid heavy fabrics like wool or polyester. Dress shoes should be comfortable and broken in, as you may be standing or walking for a long period of time.

Accessorize Appropriately

When it comes to what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer, it's important to accessorize appropriately. Avoid wearing flashy jewelry or anything that might distract from the solemnity of the occasion. Wear simple, understated pieces such as a watch or a pair of earrings. If you wear a hat, make sure it is subdued and matches your outfit.

Personal Experience

When my grandfather passed away last summer, I struggled with what to wear to his funeral. I wanted to look respectful and appropriate, but I also wanted to be comfortable in the heat. I ended up wearing a black knee-length dress made of lightweight cotton, paired with simple black flats and pearl earrings. I felt comfortable and professional, and I received many compliments on my outfit.

Another Personal Experience

My friend recently lost her mother and I attended the funeral. I wore a navy blue dress pantsuit with a white blouse and black flats. It was comfortable and respectful. My friend appreciated the effort I put into dressing appropriately for the occasion.

What Should You Not Wear to a Funeral?

When considering what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer, there are some things you should avoid wearing, such as:

  • Avoid wearing anything too revealing or provocative
  • Avoid wearing anything with a bright color or loud pattern
  • Avoid wearing anything too casual, such as jeans, shorts, or flip flops
  • Avoid wearing anything too formal, such as a ball gown or tuxedo

How to Dress Children for a Funeral?

When it comes to children, it's important to dress them in modest, respectful clothing. Avoid anything too casual or too formal. For boys, dress pants and a button-up shirt are appropriate. For girls, a dress or skirt and blouse are appropriate. Make sure their shoes are comfortable and clean.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a hat to a funeral?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat to a funeral, but make sure it is subdued and matches your outfit.

Q: Is it okay to wear open-toed shoes to a funeral?

A: It's best to avoid open-toed shoes at a funeral, as they can be seen as too casual. Opt for closed-toe shoes instead.

Q: Can I wear a bright color if it was the deceased's favorite color?

A: While it may seem like a nice gesture to wear a bright color in honor of the deceased, it's still best to avoid anything too bright or loud. Stick to more subdued colors such as black, navy blue, or dark grey.

Q: Can I wear a suit to a funeral if I'm a woman?

A: Yes, a suit is a perfectly appropriate option for women to wear to a funeral. Pair it with a blouse or dress shirt and dress shoes.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Funeral 2021 Summer

When it comes to what to wear to a funeral 2021 summer, the most important thing is to dress modestly and professionally. Choose dark colors, avoid casual clothing, opt for comfortable fabric, and accessorize appropriately. Remember, the focus should be on showing respect for the deceased and their loved ones.
