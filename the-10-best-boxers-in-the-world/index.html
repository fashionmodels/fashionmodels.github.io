Q: Who is the greatest boxer of all time?

Q: How do boxers train for fights?

A: Boxers train for fights by doing a combination of cardio exercise, strength training, and sparring. They also watch video footage of their opponents to study their fighting style and weaknesses.

Q: Why do boxers wear gloves?

A: Boxers wear gloves to protect their hands and to make the sport safer. Gloves also help to spread out the impact of punches and reduce the risk of serious injury.

Q: How do boxers cut weight before a fight?

A: Boxers cut weight before a fight by reducing their calorie intake, increasing their water intake, and doing intense cardio exercise to burn fat. They may also use saunas or hot baths to sweat out excess water weight.
