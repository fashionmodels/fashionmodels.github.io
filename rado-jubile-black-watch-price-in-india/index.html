The Rado Jubile Black Watch Price in India: What You Need to Know

When it comes to luxury watches, the Rado Jubile Black is a popular choice for many. Its sleek design, high-quality materials, and Swiss-made movement make it a reliable and stylish timepiece.

The Rado Jubile Black watch price in India ranges from INR 50,000 to INR 2,00,000, depending on the model and seller. The price can vary due to several factors such as the warranty, availability, and the seller's reputation.

Personally, I purchased my Rado Jubile Black watch from an authorized dealer in Mumbai for INR 1,20,000. It came with a two-year warranty, and I was able to verify its authenticity by checking the watch's serial number and certificate of authenticity.

Where to Buy the Rado Jubile Black Watch in India

There are several authorized dealers of Rado watches in India, including Ethos Watch Boutiques, Kapoor Watch Co., and The Prime Luxury Watch Boutique. You can also purchase the Rado Jubile Black watch online from websites such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Tata CLiQ.

It's essential to ensure that you purchase the watch from a reputable seller to avoid any fraud or counterfeit products. Always verify the seller's reputation and check for the watch's authenticity before making a purchase.

How to Ensure the Authenticity of the Rado Jubile Black Watch

One of the most critical factors to consider when purchasing a luxury watch is its authenticity. To ensure that the Rado Jubile Black watch you are purchasing is genuine, there are several steps you can take:

  • Verify the seller's reputation
  • Check for the watch's serial number and certificate of authenticity
  • Examine the watch's details, such as the logo, font, and weight
  • Compare the watch to photos of the authentic product from the Rado website or authorized dealer

By taking these steps, you can ensure that the Rado Jubile Black watch you are purchasing is authentic and worth the investment.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between the Rado Jubile Black and the Rado Centrix?

A: The Rado Jubile Black and the Rado Centrix are both popular Rado watch models. The main difference between the two is their design. The Jubile Black has a black ceramic bracelet and case, while the Centrix has a stainless steel bracelet and case. The Jubile Black also has diamonds on the dial, while the Centrix does not.

Q: What is the warranty period for the Rado Jubile Black watch in India?

A: The warranty period for the Rado Jubile Black watch in India varies depending on the seller. Typically, it ranges from two to three years.

Q: Is the Rado Jubile Black watch water-resistant?

A: Yes, the Rado Jubile Black watch is water-resistant up to 30 meters.

Q: Can I customize my Rado Jubile Black watch?

A: Yes, you can customize your Rado Jubile Black watch by changing the bracelet or adding diamonds to the dial. However, it's essential to ensure that you purchase authentic Rado parts to maintain the watch's value and quality.

Conclusion of Rado Jubile Black Watch Price in India
