Discover The Best Clothing Instagram Accounts In India

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through Instagram to find the perfect clothing accounts to follow? Do you want to stay up-to-date with the latest fashion trends in India? Look no further! In this tutorial blog post, we will guide you through the best clothing Instagram accounts in India that you should be following right now.

As a fashion enthusiast, you know the pain of not being able to find the right clothing inspiration on Instagram. With so many accounts to choose from, it can be overwhelming to determine which ones are worth your time. That's why we've compiled a list of the best clothing Instagram accounts in India that cater to different styles and preferences.

Our target is to help you find the best fashion inspiration on Instagram. Whether you're into traditional Indian clothing, western wear, or a fusion of both, these accounts are sure to provide you with the latest fashion trends and styling tips.

To summarize, we will be sharing with you the top clothing Instagram accounts in India that you should be following right now. We'll also explain why these accounts are worth your time and provide you with a personal experience of why we love them so much.

The Label Life (@thelabellife)

The Label Life is a women's lifestyle brand that offers timeless and versatile clothing pieces for everyday wear. Founded by three Bollywood celebrities - Malaika Arora, Bipasha Basu, and Sussanne Khan, The Label Life's Instagram account is a true reflection of their brand's aesthetic. The account is a mix of clean and minimalistic visuals with pops of color, making it a visual treat for the eyes.

As a personal experience, I have been following The Label Life for over a year now, and I absolutely love their clothing pieces. Their account not only provides fashion inspiration but also shares styling tips and tricks that are practical and easy to follow. From classic white shirts to statement-making dresses, The Label Life has something for everyone.

Nush (@nushbrand)

Nush is a fashion brand founded by Bollywood actor Anushka Sharma. Nush's Instagram account is a perfect blend of western and traditional Indian wear. Their account is full of bold and statement-making pieces that are perfect for those who want to make a fashion statement.

As a personal experience, I recently purchased a traditional Indian outfit from Nush, and I was blown away by the quality of the fabric and the attention to detail. Their Instagram account provides a sneak peek into their latest collection, making it easier for you to shop their pieces.

Forever 21 India (@forever21_in)

Forever 21 India's Instagram account is a go-to for those who love affordable and trendy clothing pieces. Their account is full of bright and colorful visuals, making it a fun and exciting account to follow.

As a personal experience, I love Forever 21 India's Instagram account as it provides me with the latest fashion trends without breaking the bank. Their account is perfect for those who are looking for trendy clothing pieces but don't want to spend a lot of money.

How to Shop from these Accounts?

Shopping from these Instagram accounts is easy and convenient. Most of these accounts have a link to their website in their bio, making it easier for you to shop their pieces. Some accounts also have the option of shopping directly from Instagram using Instagram's shopping feature. All you have to do is click on the product you like, and it will take you to the product's page on their website.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these accounts only for women's clothing?

A: No, while some of these accounts cater specifically to women's clothing, others have a mix of men's and women's clothing.

Q: Are these accounts only for traditional Indian wear?

A: No, these accounts cater to different styles and preferences, including western wear, fusion wear, and traditional Indian wear.

Q: Do these accounts ship internationally?

A: It depends on the brand. Some of these brands do ship internationally, while others only ship within India. It's best to check their website for more information on shipping.

Q: Are these accounts verified?

A: Yes, all of the accounts mentioned in this post are verified by Instagram.

Conclusion of Best Clothing Instagram Accounts India

In conclusion, these clothing Instagram accounts in India are perfect for those who love fashion and want to stay up-to-date with the latest fashion trends. From western wear to traditional Indian wear, these accounts cater to different styles and preferences. So, what are you waiting for? Start following these accounts today and get inspired!
