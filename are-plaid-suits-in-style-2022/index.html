Why Plaid Suits Are in Style 2022

How to Style Plaid Suits in 2022

Styling a plaid suit can seem daunting, but it doesn't have to be. The key is to keep it simple and let the suit do the talking. Here are some tips to help you style your plaid suit in 2022:

Choose the Right Shirt

When it comes to styling a plaid suit, the right shirt can make all the difference. A plain white shirt is always a safe option, but you can also experiment with different colors and patterns. Just make sure that the shirt complements the suit and doesn't clash with it.

Accessorize Wisely

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so it's essential to choose them wisely. A tie can add a touch of sophistication to your plaid suit, but make sure that it matches the suit and shirt. You can also add a pocket square or cufflinks for some extra flair.

Where to Buy Plaid Suits in 2022

If you're looking to buy a plaid suit in 2022, there are plenty of options available. Many high-street retailers offer plaid suits in a range of colors and styles. You can also check out online retailers like ASOS or Amazon for a wider selection. If you're looking for a more high-end option, designer brands like Gucci and Prada offer plaid suits that are sure to turn heads.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a plaid suit to a wedding?

A: Yes! Plaid suits are a great choice for weddings, especially if you want to make a statement. Just make sure that the suit matches the formality of the event and that you pair it with the right accessories.

Q: What shoes should I wear with a plaid suit?

A: The shoes you wear with a plaid suit will depend on the occasion and the suit itself. For a formal event, a pair of dress shoes like oxfords or loafers is a safe choice. For a more casual look, you can wear sneakers or even boots.

Q: Can I wear a plaid suit to the office?

A: Yes! Plaid suits can be worn to the office, but it's essential to choose the right style and color. Stick to classic colors like navy or gray, and make sure that the suit fits well. Pair it with a plain shirt and dress shoes for a sophisticated look.

Q: How do I know if a plaid suit fits well?

A: The key to a well-fitting plaid suit is to make sure that it fits you correctly. The shoulders should fit snugly, and the sleeves should end at your wrist bone. The pants should be hemmed to the right length and fit comfortably around the waist.
