Top Wedding Card Designers In India A Guide To Choosing The Best

Written by Christine Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning your dream wedding and looking for the perfect wedding invitation? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. From elegant and traditional to modern and trendy, finding the right wedding card designer in India can be a daunting task. Here’s a guide to help you choose the best.

When it comes to selecting a wedding card designer in India, there are a few things you should consider. First, decide on the style and theme of your wedding. This will help you narrow down your options and choose a designer that specializes in your desired style. Second, consider your budget. Wedding card designers in India offer a wide range of pricing options, so it’s important to choose a designer that fits within your budget.

In this article, we’ll take a closer look at the top wedding card designers in India and what sets them apart. We’ll explore their design styles, pricing options, and customer reviews to help you make the best decision for your special day.

1. Ravish Kapoor

If you’re looking for a wedding card designer in India that specializes in luxury invitations, look no further than Ravish Kapoor. Known for his opulent and extravagant designs, Kapoor has become a favorite among celebrities and high-profile clients. His designs feature intricate details and luxurious materials, such as silk and velvet. Expect to pay a premium for Kapoor’s designs, as his invitations can cost upwards of Rs. 1,000 per card.

2. Parekh Cards

Parekh Cards has been a leading wedding card designer in India for over 50 years. They offer a wide range of designs, from traditional to modern, and are known for their high-quality printing and affordable pricing. Parekh Cards also offers customization options, allowing you to add your own personal touch to your wedding invitations. Prices start at just Rs. 15 per card, making Parekh Cards a great option for budget-conscious couples.

3. Indian Wedding Card

Indian Wedding Card is a popular choice among couples looking for traditional and elegant wedding invitations. They offer a range of designs, including Hindu, Muslim, and Sikh wedding invitations. Indian Wedding Card also offers customization options, such as adding your own text and choosing your preferred font. Prices start at around Rs. 30 per card, making Indian Wedding Card an affordable option for couples on a budget.

4. Turmeric Ink

Turmeric Ink is a wedding card designer in India that specializes in modern and trendy invitations. Their designs feature bold colors and unique patterns, making them a great choice for couples looking for something different. Turmeric Ink also offers customization options, allowing you to add your own personal touch to your wedding invitations. Prices start at around Rs. 100 per card, making Turmeric Ink a mid-range option for couples.

Question and Answer:

Q: How do I choose the best wedding card designer in India?

A: Consider your wedding style and budget, and research different designers to find one that specializes in your desired style and fits within your budget.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for wedding invitations in India?

A: Prices can vary widely depending on the designer and the level of customization. Expect to pay anywhere from Rs. 15 to Rs. 1,000 per card.

Q: Can I customize my wedding invitations?

A: Yes, many wedding card designers in India offer customization options, allowing you to add your own personal touch to your invitations.

Q: What is the turnaround time for wedding invitations in India?

A: Turnaround times can vary depending on the designer and the level of customization. It’s best to contact the designer directly to get an estimate of how long it will take to receive your wedding invitations.

Conclusion of Top Wedding Card Designers in India:

Choosing the right wedding card designer in India can make all the difference in creating the perfect wedding invitation. By considering your wedding style and budget, you can find a designer that specializes in your desired style and fits within your budget. Whether you’re looking for luxury and opulence or modern and trendy, the top wedding card designers in India have something for everyone.
