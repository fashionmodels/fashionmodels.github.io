Discover The Top 10 Highest Paid Boxers In The World

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Boxing is one of the most exciting and dangerous sports in the world. The thrill of the fight, the adrenaline rush, and the sound of the crowd cheering all add to the excitement. But for the boxers, it's not just about the thrill, but also about the money they make. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the top 10 highest paid boxers in the world and how they earned their fortunes.

Boxing is a tough sport, and boxers put their bodies on the line every time they step into the ring. They endure grueling training regimens, face the risk of serious injury, and often struggle to make a living. For the top 10 highest paid boxers in the world, however, the rewards are well worth the risks.

So, who are the top 10 highest paid boxers in the world? The answer to this question is constantly changing as boxers rise and fall in the ranks. However, we've compiled a list of the top 10 highest paid boxers based on their earnings over the past year.

Here are the top 10 highest paid boxers in the world:

Floyd Mayweather Jr.

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is one of the most successful boxers of all time, with an undefeated record of 50-0. He's also the highest paid boxer in the world, earning a whopping $285 million in 2018. Mayweather has made a name for himself not only for his skills in the ring but also for his extravagant lifestyle and outspoken personality.

Canelo Alvarez

Canelo Alvarez is a Mexican boxer who has quickly risen to the top of the sport. In 2018, he earned an impressive $94 million, making him the second-highest paid boxer in the world. Alvarez has a record of 52-1-2 and has won titles in multiple weight classes.

Anthony Joshua

Anthony Joshua is a British boxer who has become a household name in the sport. He earned $45 million in 2018, making him the third-highest paid boxer in the world. Joshua has a record of 22-1 and has won multiple heavyweight titles.

Gennady Golovkin

Gennady Golovkin is a Kazakh boxer who has made a name for himself with his impressive knockout record. He earned $25 million in 2018, putting him in fourth place on our list of the top 10 highest paid boxers. Golovkin has a record of 38-1-1 and has won multiple middleweight titles.

Tyson Fury

Tyson Fury is a British boxer who has made a comeback to the sport after a hiatus. He earned $23 million in 2018, making him the fifth-highest paid boxer in the world. Fury has a record of 28-0-1 and has won multiple heavyweight titles.

Deontay Wilder

Deontay Wilder is an American boxer known for his powerful punches. He earned $22 million in 2018, putting him in sixth place on our list of the top 10 highest paid boxers. Wilder has a record of 41-0-1 and has won multiple heavyweight titles.

Manny Pacquiao

Manny Pacquiao is a Filipino boxer who has been in the sport for over 20 years. He earned $20 million in 2018, making him the seventh-highest paid boxer in the world. Pacquiao has a record of 62-7-2 and has won titles in multiple weight classes.

Josh Warrington

Josh Warrington is a British boxer who has made a name for himself in recent years with his impressive performances. He earned $16 million in 2018, putting him in eighth place on our list of the top 10 highest paid boxers. Warrington has a record of 30-0 and has won multiple featherweight titles.

Errol Spence Jr.

Errol Spence Jr. is an American boxer who has quickly risen to the top of the sport. He earned $15 million in 2018, making him the ninth-highest paid boxer in the world. Spence Jr. has a record of 25-0 and has won multiple welterweight titles.

Leo Santa Cruz

Leo Santa Cruz is a Mexican boxer who has been in the sport for over a decade. He earned $14 million in 2018, putting him in tenth place on our list of the top 10 highest paid boxers. Santa Cruz has a record of 36-1-1 and has won titles in multiple weight classes.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, the top 10 highest paid boxers in the world have earned their fortunes through hard work, dedication, and skill. Boxing is a tough sport, but for these boxers, the rewards are well worth the risks. Whether you're a fan of the sport or not, it's hard to deny the excitement and drama that comes with every fight.
