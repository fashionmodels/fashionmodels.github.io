Best Men s Clothing Subscription Boxes 2021 A Guide To Upgrade Your Wardrobe

Written by Christine Oct 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you struggle to find the time or motivation to shop for new clothes? Are you tired of wearing the same outfits over and over again? Men's clothing subscription boxes may be the solution to your fashion woes. In this guide, we'll explore the best men's clothing subscription boxes of 2021 and how they can help you upgrade your wardrobe without the hassle.

Many men find shopping for clothes to be a chore. From navigating crowded stores to trying on endless outfits, the process can be overwhelming and time-consuming. Additionally, some men may not have a strong sense of style or simply lack the desire to keep up with the latest fashion trends. This is where men's clothing subscription boxes can come in handy.

A men's clothing subscription box is a service that delivers a curated selection of clothes to your doorstep on a regular basis. These boxes can include everything from basic t-shirts to stylish jackets, and are tailored to your personal style preferences. By outsourcing the shopping process, you can save time and energy while still looking your best.

In this article, we'll highlight some of the best men's clothing subscription boxes of 2021 and what makes them stand out. We'll also provide tips on how to choose the right subscription box for your needs, and answer some common questions about the process.

1. Stitch Fix

Stitch Fix is one of the most popular men's clothing subscription boxes on the market. When you sign up for the service, you'll fill out a style profile that includes your size, fit preferences, and style goals. A personal stylist will then curate a box of five items that are tailored to your preferences.

One of the unique features of Stitch Fix is that you can try on the clothes at home before deciding what to keep. You'll only be charged for the items you choose to keep, and you can send back the rest for free. This allows you to take risks with your fashion choices without the commitment.

2. Menlo Club

Menlo Club (formerly Five Four Club) is another popular men's clothing subscription box. Each month, you'll receive a curated box of two to three items, which can include everything from shirts to pants to accessories. Menlo Club also has its own clothing line, which means you'll get access to exclusive styles.

One of the benefits of Menlo Club is its affordability. The subscription starts at just $29 per month, which makes it accessible to men on a budget. Additionally, the company offers a satisfaction guarantee, so you can be sure you'll love what you receive.

3. Frank And Oak

Frank And Oak is a sustainable men's clothing brand that offers a subscription box service. When you sign up, you'll fill out a style quiz that helps the company understand your preferences. Each month, you'll receive a box of three to five items that are curated to your style profile.

One of the benefits of Frank And Oak is that the company is committed to sustainability. All of its clothes are made from eco-friendly materials, and it has a focus on reducing waste. If you're looking for a subscription box that aligns with your values, Frank And Oak may be a good option.

4. Trunk Club

Trunk Club is a men's clothing subscription box that is owned by Nordstrom. When you sign up for the service, you'll be matched with a personal stylist who will curate a box of clothes based on your preferences. You'll have the opportunity to provide feedback on the items before deciding what to keep.

One of the benefits of Trunk Club is that it offers a wide range of clothing options, from casual to formal wear. Additionally, the company has a focus on quality, so you can expect to receive clothes that are made to last. Keep in mind that Trunk Club is one of the more expensive subscription boxes on the market, with prices starting at $25 per month.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I choose the right men's clothing subscription box?

A: Consider factors such as price, style options, and company values when choosing a subscription box. It can also be helpful to read reviews from other customers to get an idea of what to expect.

Q: How often will I receive a men's clothing subscription box?

A: It depends on the service you choose. Some subscription boxes come monthly, while others come quarterly or on a less frequent basis.

Q: How much does a men's clothing subscription box cost?

A: Prices vary depending on the service and the number of items included in each box. Some subscription boxes start as low as $29 per month, while others can cost upwards of $100 per box.

Q: Can I return items I don't like from a men's clothing subscription box?

A: Most subscription boxes have a return policy that allows you to send back items you don't want. However, the specifics of the policy may vary, so be sure to read the fine print before signing up.

Conclusion of Best Men's Clothing Subscription Boxes 2021

Men's clothing subscription boxes can be a game-changer for anyone who wants to upgrade their wardrobe without the hassle of traditional shopping. By choosing the right service for your needs and preferences, you can receive a curated selection of clothes that fit your style and budget. Whether you're looking for sustainable options, exclusive styles, or the ability to try on clothes at home, there's a subscription box out there for you.
