Step by Step Guide to Convert Men's Medium Shirts to Women's Size

Step 1: Measure Your Body Size

The first step in converting men's medium shirts to women's size is to measure your body size. It is essential to get accurate measurements to ensure the perfect fit.

The measurements to take are:

- Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest
- Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your waist
- Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips

Tip:

It is best to measure yourself in the undergarments you plan to wear with the shirt.

Step 2: Understand the Differences Between Men's and Women's Shirts

The second step in converting men's medium shirts to women's size is to understand the differences between men's and women's shirts. Men's shirts are designed to fit a straighter body shape, whereas women's shirts are designed to fit a more curvy body shape.

The differences between men's and women's shirts are:

- Men's shirts have broader shoulders
- Men's shirts have a longer torso
- Men's shirts have a looser fit around the waist
- Men's shirts have a straighter cut through the hips

Tip:

It is essential to keep these differences in mind while making the necessary adjustments to the shirt.

Step 3: Make the Necessary Adjustments

The third and final step in converting men's medium shirts to women's size is to make the necessary adjustments to the shirt. The adjustments to make are:

- Shorten the sleeves: Women's arms are generally shorter than men's arms.
- Take in the sides: Women have a narrower waist than men.
- Adjust the shoulders: Women's shoulders are generally narrower than men's shoulders.
- Add darts: Darts help create a more fitted look around the bust and waist.

Tip:

It is best to take the shirt to a tailor to make the necessary adjustments. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, it is essential to take it slow and make small adjustments at a time.
