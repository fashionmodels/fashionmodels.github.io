Dubai Indian Wedding Shopping A Guide To Finding The Perfect Outfit

Written by Oliver Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you getting married soon and feeling overwhelmed with the task of finding the perfect outfit? Look no further than Dubai, a city known for its extravagant shopping experiences. However, shopping for an Indian wedding in Dubai can be a daunting task, especially if you are unfamiliar with the city or its customs.

The target of Dubai Indian wedding shopping is to find the perfect outfit for your wedding day. This can be a challenging task as Indian weddings are known for their elaborate ceremonies and multiple outfit changes. Additionally, Dubai has a vast array of shopping options, from high-end boutiques to traditional markets, which can make it difficult to know where to start.

In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks to make your Dubai Indian wedding shopping experience stress-free and enjoyable. We will cover everything from where to shop to what to look for in an outfit.

Dubai Indian Wedding Shopping: Where to Start

When it comes to shopping for an Indian wedding in Dubai, it is important to start early. Many brides begin shopping for their wedding outfit 6-8 months in advance to ensure they have enough time to find the perfect dress. It is also important to do your research ahead of time and familiarize yourself with the different shopping options available in Dubai.

Personally, I found that the best place to start was at a high-end boutique. These stores usually have a wide selection of designer Indian wedding outfits and knowledgeable staff who can help you find the perfect dress. Some of my favorite high-end boutiques in Dubai include Sabyasachi, Manish Malhotra, and Tarun Tahiliani.

What to Look for in an Outfit

When shopping for an Indian wedding outfit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the theme of your wedding and choose an outfit that complements it. For example, if you are having a traditional Indian wedding, you may want to choose a red or gold outfit with intricate embroidery.

Second, consider your comfort level. Indian weddings are known for their long ceremonies, so it is important to choose an outfit that you can comfortably wear for several hours. Additionally, consider the climate in Dubai and choose an outfit that is appropriate for the weather.

Where to Shop for Accessories

Once you have found your perfect outfit, it is time to start shopping for accessories. Dubai has a vast array of shopping options for accessories, including traditional markets and high-end boutiques.

Personally, I found that the best place to shop for accessories was at a traditional market like the Gold Souk or Spice Souk. These markets have a wide selection of jewelry, bangles, and other accessories at affordable prices. However, if you are looking for high-end accessories, there are also plenty of boutiques to choose from, including Amrapali and Gehna.

Tips for Shopping in Dubai

Finally, here are a few tips to keep in mind when shopping for your Indian wedding outfit in Dubai:

  • Start shopping early to ensure you have enough time to find the perfect outfit.
  • Familiarize yourself with the different shopping options available in Dubai.
  • Choose an outfit that complements the theme of your wedding and is comfortable to wear.
  • Shop for accessories at a traditional market or high-end boutique.
  • Be prepared to negotiate prices at traditional markets.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time of year to shop for an Indian wedding outfit in Dubai?

A: The best time to shop for an Indian wedding outfit in Dubai is during the Dubai Shopping Festival, which usually takes place in January and February.

Q: Are there any traditional markets in Dubai that specialize in Indian wedding outfits?

A: Yes, the Meena Bazaar and Karama Market are both known for their selection of Indian wedding outfits.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on an Indian wedding outfit in Dubai?

A: Prices can vary widely depending on the designer and quality of the outfit. However, expect to spend anywhere from AED 3,000 to AED 20,000 for a high-end Indian wedding outfit.

Q: Do I need to make an appointment to shop at a high-end boutique in Dubai?

A: It is recommended to make an appointment at high-end boutiques to ensure that you receive personalized attention from the staff.

Conclusion of Dubai Indian Wedding Shopping

Shopping for an Indian wedding outfit in Dubai can be a fun and exciting experience if you know where to start. By familiarizing yourself with the different shopping options available and keeping a few tips in mind, you can find the perfect outfit for your special day. Happy shopping!
