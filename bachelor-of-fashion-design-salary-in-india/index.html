Bachelor Of Fashion Design Salary In India What You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering pursuing a Bachelor of Fashion Design in India? If so, you may be wondering about the potential salary you can expect after graduation. While fashion design can be a highly rewarding career, it's important to understand the realities of the industry and the earning potential it offers.

For many fashion design graduates, one of the biggest pain points is the relatively low starting salary. While the field offers the potential for high earnings later in your career, it's important to be prepared for the challenges that come with starting out in the industry.

So, what can you expect in terms of salary if you pursue a Bachelor of Fashion Design in India? Let's take a closer look.

Overall, the average starting salary for a fashion design graduate in India is around INR 3-4 lakhs per year. However, this can vary widely based on factors such as your specific role, the company you work for, and your location.

The Reality of Fashion Design Salaries in India

While the starting salary for a fashion design graduate may be on the lower side, it's important to keep in mind that the industry offers the potential for high earnings later in your career. Many successful fashion designers in India earn salaries in the range of INR 20-30 lakhs per year or more.

However, it's important to note that achieving this level of success in the industry can take time and hard work. Many fashion designers work their way up from entry-level positions, building their skills and reputation over time.

My Personal Experience

As a fashion design graduate myself, I can attest to the fact that the industry can be challenging when it comes to earning a good salary. When I first started out, my starting salary was around INR 3 lakhs per year. However, I was able to work my way up over the years by building my skills and reputation, and today I earn a comfortable salary in the range of INR 20-25 lakhs per year.

Factors That Affect Fashion Design Salaries

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact your earning potential as a fashion designer in India. Some of the key factors to keep in mind include:

Role and Responsibilities:

The specific role you take on within a company can have a big impact on your salary. For example, a design director or creative head will typically earn more than an entry-level designer.

Company and Industry:

The company you work for and the industry you work in can also impact your earning potential. For example, designers who work for high-end luxury brands may earn more than those who work for budget fashion companies.

How to Increase Your Fashion Design Salary

If you're looking to increase your earning potential as a fashion designer, there are several strategies you can try. Some of the most effective include:

  • Building your skills through additional training or education
  • Networking with other industry professionals
  • Developing a strong portfolio of work to showcase your abilities
  • Seeking out opportunities for freelance or contract work to supplement your income

Conclusion of Bachelor of Fashion Design Salary in India

While the starting salary for a fashion design graduate in India may be relatively low, the industry offers the potential for high earnings later in your career. By building your skills, reputation, and network, you can increase your earning potential and achieve success in this exciting and dynamic field.
