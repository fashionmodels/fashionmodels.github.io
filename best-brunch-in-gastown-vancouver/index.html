Best Cafe for Brunch in Gastown Vancouver: Nelson the Seagull

One of Gastown's most popular cafes is Nelson the Seagull, and it's not hard to see why. The atmosphere is cozy and laid-back, with an industrial-chic decor that's perfect for Instagram photos. Their menu features a range of delicious brunch dishes, including a classic avocado toast, a breakfast sandwich, and a variety of baked goods. The coffee is also top-notch, with a range of espresso-based drinks and drip coffee available.

One of the standout dishes at Nelson the Seagull is their sourdough pancakes. These fluffy pancakes are made with sourdough starter, giving them a unique flavor and texture. They come topped with maple syrup and whipped cream, making them the perfect sweet start to your day.

Best Brunch Dish in Gastown Vancouver: Jam Cafe's Chicken and Waffles

If you're looking for a brunch dish that's both hearty and delicious, look no further than Jam Cafe's Chicken and Waffles. This dish features a crispy fried chicken breast on top of a fluffy waffle, drizzled with maple syrup and served with a side of gravy. It's the perfect combination of sweet and savory, and it's sure to leave you feeling satisfied.
