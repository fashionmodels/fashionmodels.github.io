The Benefits of Down Insulation

One of the reasons why warm winter coats made in Canada are so effective is that they use down insulation. Down is a natural insulator that is found under the feathers of ducks and geese. It traps air and keeps it close to your body, which helps to keep you warm.

When it comes to down insulation, the higher the fill power, the better. Fill power refers to the amount of space one ounce of down takes up when it’s fully lofted. The higher the fill power, the more air the down can trap, which means more warmth for you.

How to Care for Your Warm Winter Coat

If you want your warm winter coat to last for years to come, then it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

1. Always follow the care instructions on the label.

2. Store your coat in a cool, dry place.

3. Avoid washing your coat too often. Spot clean it instead.

4. If you do need to wash your coat, use a gentle detergent and cold water.
