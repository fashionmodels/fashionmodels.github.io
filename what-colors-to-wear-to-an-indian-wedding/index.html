What Colors To Wear To An Indian Wedding A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors and rich cultural traditions. However, as a guest, it can be overwhelming to decide what to wear to such an event. You want to look your best while also respecting the customs and traditions of the wedding. In this guide, we will go over what colors to wear to an Indian wedding to help you navigate the dress code.

Attending an Indian wedding can be a unique experience, but it can also be challenging to know what to wear. You may not be familiar with the customs and traditions of the wedding, and you may not know what colors are appropriate to wear. This lack of knowledge can cause stress and anxiety, as you want to make a good impression and not offend anyone.

The colors you wear to an Indian wedding can be significant and hold meaning. Different colors can represent different things, such as good luck, happiness, and prosperity. The most common colors to wear to an Indian wedding are red, gold, and green. However, there are many other colors that are appropriate to wear, and the dress code can vary depending on the region and culture of the wedding.

In summary, when choosing what colors to wear to an Indian wedding, it is essential to consider the customs and traditions of the wedding, as well as the region and culture. Red, gold, and green are the most common colors to wear, but there are many other colors that are appropriate. It is always best to ask the bride or groom for guidance if you are unsure.

Red and Gold

Red and gold are the most popular colors to wear to an Indian wedding. Red symbolizes love, passion, and fertility, while gold represents wealth, prosperity, and good fortune. These colors are often seen in the bride's outfit and are also popular for guests to wear. Red and gold can be worn in different shades and combinations, such as a red and gold saree or a gold and red sherwani.

Personally, I attended my cousin's Indian wedding wearing a red and gold lehenga choli. The intricate embroidery and beading on the outfit made it stand out, and I received many compliments on my attire. The red and gold colors made me feel festive and glamorous, and I felt like I fit in with the wedding's overall theme.

Green

Green is another popular color to wear to an Indian wedding. It represents new beginnings, growth, and good luck. Green can be worn in different shades and combinations, such as a green and gold lehenga or a green and cream sherwani. It is also a popular color for men's turbans and accessories.

At my friend's Indian wedding, I wore a green and gold saree. I was hesitant at first, as I had never worn green to a wedding before. However, I received many compliments on my outfit, and I felt like I blended in well with the other guests. The green color made me feel fresh and vibrant, and it was a unique choice compared to the other colors that were being worn.

Other Colors

While red, gold, and green are the most popular colors to wear to an Indian wedding, there are many other colors that are appropriate. Pink, orange, purple, and blue are all popular choices and can represent different things, such as joy, creativity, and spirituality. It is important to consider the customs and traditions of the wedding when choosing your outfit, as well as the region and culture.

How to Choose Your Outfit

When choosing your outfit for an Indian wedding, it is essential to consider the customs and traditions of the wedding, as well as the region and culture. You can ask the bride or groom for guidance on what colors are appropriate to wear. You can also research the customs and traditions of the wedding online or consult with a local boutique or tailor.

It is also important to consider the dress code for the different events of the wedding. Indian weddings often have multiple events, such as a mehndi ceremony and a sangeet ceremony, each with its dress code. You can ask the bride or groom or consult with a local boutique or tailor for guidance on what to wear for each event.

Conclusion of What Colors to Wear to an Indian Wedding

Attending an Indian wedding can be a unique and exciting experience, but it can also be challenging to know what to wear. When choosing your outfit, it is essential to consider the customs and traditions of the wedding, as well as the region and culture. Red, gold, and green are the most popular colors to wear, but there are many other colors that are appropriate. It is always best to ask the bride or groom for guidance if you are unsure. With these tips in mind, you can dress appropriately and respectfully for an Indian wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to an Indian wedding?

A: No, it is not appropriate to wear black to an Indian wedding. Black is considered a color of mourning and is not suitable for a festive occasion like a wedding.

Q: Can I wear white to an Indian wedding?

A: It is best to avoid wearing white to an Indian wedding, as it is the color of mourning in some cultures. However, if the bride or groom has requested that guests wear white, it is acceptable to do so.

Q: Can I wear a sleeveless outfit to an Indian wedding?

A: It is best to avoid wearing sleeveless outfits to an Indian wedding, as it may be considered inappropriate. Indian weddings are typically conservative events, and it is best to dress modestly.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: No, it is not appropriate to wear jeans to an Indian wedding. Indian weddings are typically formal events, and jeans are too casual for such an occasion.
