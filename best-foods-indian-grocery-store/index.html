The Best Foods To Buy At An Indian Grocery Store

Written by Frank Oct 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to explore Indian cuisine or simply want to stock up on some essential ingredients, visiting an Indian grocery store can be overwhelming. With so many unfamiliar products and brands, it can be difficult to know where to start. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll guide you through the best foods to buy at an Indian grocery store.

Indian cuisine is known for its bold flavors and diverse ingredients. From fragrant spices to lentils and rice, the cuisine is a reflection of the country's rich cultural heritage. However, for those who are not familiar with Indian cooking, navigating an Indian grocery store can be a daunting task. But worry not, we have compiled a list of the best foods to buy at an Indian grocery store.

When it comes to Indian grocery stores, the options are endless. From lentils and spices to snacks and sweets, there is something for everyone. However, the key to shopping at an Indian grocery store is to know what you're looking for. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a beginner, it's important to have a plan before you shop.

In summary, the best foods to buy at an Indian grocery store include lentils, spices, rice, ghee, naan bread, and snacks such as samosas and bhajis. These ingredients form the backbone of Indian cuisine and will allow you to create a wide variety of dishes in your own kitchen.

The Best Lentils to Buy

One of the staples of Indian cuisine is lentils. They are a great source of protein and can be used in a variety of dishes. The most commonly used lentils in Indian cooking include chana dal, moong dal, and toor dal. Chana dal is a split chickpea lentil that is commonly used in dals and curries. Moong dal is a split mung bean lentil that is used in soups, stews, and curries. Toor dal is a split pigeon pea lentil that is used in dals and sambars.

When I first started cooking Indian food, I was overwhelmed by the different types of lentils available. However, I found that starting with the three types mentioned above was a good place to start.

The Best Spices to Buy

Spices are an integral part of Indian cuisine. They are used to add flavor and depth to dishes. Some of the most commonly used spices in Indian cooking include cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. These spices can be used in a variety of dishes, from curries to rice dishes.

When it comes to buying spices at an Indian grocery store, I recommend buying them whole rather than ground. Whole spices have a longer shelf life and can be ground as needed. Additionally, whole spices have a more intense flavor than pre-ground spices.

The Best Rice to Buy

Rice is a staple in Indian cuisine. It is used in a variety of dishes, from biryanis to pilafs. The most commonly used rice in Indian cooking is basmati rice. Basmati rice is a long-grain rice that has a nutty flavor and aroma. It is also known for its fluffy texture.

When buying basmati rice at an Indian grocery store, look for aged rice. Aged rice has a better flavor and texture than new rice. Additionally, look for rice that has been packaged in a clear bag. This will allow you to inspect the rice for any impurities.

The Best Snacks to Buy

If you're looking for a quick snack, an Indian grocery store is the place to be. From samosas to bhajis, there is a wide variety of snacks to choose from. Samosas are a pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas. Bhajis are fritters made from vegetables such as onions, spinach, and potatoes.

When it comes to buying snacks at an Indian grocery store, look for freshly made snacks. Many stores will have a snack counter where you can purchase freshly made snacks. Additionally, look for snacks that are made with high-quality ingredients.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I buy pre-made spice mixes at an Indian grocery store?

A: Yes, you can buy pre-made spice mixes at an Indian grocery store. However, I recommend buying whole spices and grinding them yourself for the best flavor.

Q: What is the best way to store lentils?

A: Lentils should be stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. They will keep for up to a year if stored properly.

Q: Can I buy fresh produce at an Indian grocery store?

A: Yes, many Indian grocery stores will have a selection of fresh produce, including fruits and vegetables.

Q: What is ghee?

A: Ghee is a type of clarified butter that is commonly used in Indian cooking. It has a nutty flavor and can be used in a variety of dishes.

Conclusion of Best Foods Indian Grocery Store

Shopping at an Indian grocery store can be a fun and exciting experience. However, with so many unfamiliar products, it can also be overwhelming. By knowing what to look for, you can navigate the store with ease and confidence. The best foods to buy at an Indian grocery store include lentils, spices, rice, ghee, naan bread, and snacks such as samosas and bhajis. Happy shopping!
