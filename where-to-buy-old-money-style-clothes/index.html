Where To Buy Old Money Style Clothes A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to dress like a member of the old money elite, you may be wondering where to buy old money style clothes. It can be difficult to find pieces that fit the aesthetic and quality of the upper class, but fear not! We've put together a list of the best places to find old money style clothes.

The first thing to consider when looking for old money style clothes is the quality of the materials. The elite of old money society did not skimp on quality, so you'll want to find pieces made from high-quality materials like silk, cashmere, and wool. Additionally, old money style clothes tend to be classic and timeless, rather than trendy and of-the-moment.

Now, let's get into the best places to buy old money style clothes:

Department Stores

Believe it or not, many department stores carry old money style clothes. Look for brands like Brooks Brothers, Ralph Lauren, and J. Crew, which offer classic pieces that are stylish and timeless. Be sure to check out the men's section, as many old money style clothes are unisex.

Thrift Stores and Consignment Shops

Thrift stores and consignment shops can be a goldmine for old money style clothes. Look for vintage pieces made from high-quality materials, like tweed blazers, cashmere sweaters, and silk blouses. You may have to do some digging, but the reward is worth it.

Online Retailers

Online retailers like Etsy, eBay, and Poshmark offer a wide selection of old money style clothes. These sites allow you to search for specific pieces, so you can find exactly what you're looking for. Be sure to read the seller's reviews and check their return policy before making a purchase.

Conclusion

When it comes to where to buy old money style clothes, it's all about quality and timelessness. Look for pieces made from high-quality materials and brands that offer classic, stylish pieces. Check out department stores, thrift stores, consignment shops, and online retailers for the best selection. Happy shopping!

Where to Buy Old Money Style Clothes Online

If you're looking to buy old money style clothes online, there are several options to choose from. Etsy offers a wide selection of vintage and handmade pieces, while eBay and Poshmark allow you to search for specific brands and items. Just be sure to check the seller's reviews and return policy before making a purchase.

Tips for Buying Old Money Style Clothes Online

When buying old money style clothes online, it's important to do your research. Look for sellers with high ratings and positive reviews, and be sure to carefully read the item description and measurements. If you're unsure about the fit, don't be afraid to contact the seller and ask for more information. And always check the return policy before making a purchase, just in case the item doesn't fit or isn't as described.
