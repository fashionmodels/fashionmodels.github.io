Are You Supposed To Wear Underwear Under Compression Pants

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Compression pants have become increasingly popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts. They are designed to improve performance, reduce muscle soreness, and prevent injuries. However, one question that many people ask is whether they should wear underwear under compression pants or not. This article aims to answer this question and provide some useful insights.

Many people find compression pants uncomfortable to wear without underwear. The tight-fitting fabric can cause chafing, irritation, and even skin infections. Moreover, some people are concerned about hygiene and prefer to wear underwear for extra protection. On the other hand, some athletes argue that wearing underwear under compression pants defeats the purpose of compression, as it can interfere with the blood flow and reduce the benefits of compression.

The short answer is that it depends on personal preference and the type of compression pants you are wearing. Some compression pants are designed with a built-in underwear or liner that provides extra support and comfort. In this case, you do not need to wear underwear. However, if your compression pants do not have a built-in liner or if you find it uncomfortable, you can wear your regular underwear underneath.

In summary, whether you should wear underwear under compression pants depends on your personal preference and the design of the compression pants. If you feel comfortable wearing your regular underwear, go for it. If you prefer not to wear underwear, make sure your compression pants have a built-in liner for extra support and comfort.

My Personal Experience

As someone who regularly wears compression pants for running and weightlifting, I have tried both wearing underwear and going commando. I found that wearing underwear underneath my compression pants caused more discomfort and chafing, especially during long runs. However, I always make sure to wear compression pants with a built-in liner for extra support and comfort.

Benefits of Wearing Compression Pants Without Underwear

If you decide to go commando with your compression pants, there are some benefits you can enjoy. Firstly, you will have a more streamlined silhouette without any visible panty lines or bulges. Secondly, you will have better ventilation and less risk of sweat build-up, which can cause skin irritation and infections. Finally, you will have a more comfortable and unrestricted range of motion, which can improve your performance and reduce the risk of injuries.

How to Choose Compression Pants with Built-in Liner

If you prefer to wear compression pants with a built-in liner, there are some things you should consider when choosing your pants. Firstly, make sure the liner is made of a soft and breathable fabric that feels comfortable against your skin. Secondly, make sure the liner is snug enough to provide support but not too tight to cause chafing or irritation. Finally, make sure the pants fit well and do not bunch or ride up during exercise.

Tips for Choosing Underwear to Wear Under Compression Pants

If you decide to wear your regular underwear under compression pants, there are some tips you can follow to minimize discomfort and chafing. Firstly, choose underwear made of a soft and breathable fabric that wicks away moisture and sweat. Secondly, make sure the underwear fits well and does not bunch or ride up during exercise. Finally, avoid wearing thongs or G-strings, as they can cause more friction and irritation.

My Final Thoughts

Whether you should wear underwear under compression pants is a personal choice, and there is no right or wrong answer. It depends on your comfort level and the design of the compression pants. However, if you choose to wear underwear, make sure it is made of a soft and breathable fabric and fits well. Also, make sure your compression pants have a built-in liner for extra support and comfort.

Question and Answer

Q: Can wearing underwear under compression pants reduce the benefits of compression?

A: It depends on the type of underwear and the compression pants you are wearing. If the underwear is too tight or restrictive, it can interfere with the blood flow and reduce the benefits of compression. However, if the underwear is made of a soft and breathable fabric and fits well, it should not affect the compression.

Q: Can wearing compression pants without underwear cause skin infections?

A: It is possible if you do not maintain proper hygiene and clean your compression pants regularly. Sweat and bacteria can accumulate in the fabric and cause skin irritation, rashes, or infections. Therefore, it is important to wash your compression pants after each use and avoid wearing them for too long without washing.

Q: Can men wear compression pants without underwear?

A: Yes, men can wear compression pants without underwear, as long as they feel comfortable and do not experience any discomfort or chafing. However, men should make sure to clean their genital area thoroughly and wear compression pants with a built-in liner or support for extra protection.

Q: Can women wear compression pants without underwear?

A: Yes, women can wear compression pants without underwear, as long as they feel comfortable and do not experience any discomfort or chafing. However, women should make sure to wear compression pants with a built-in liner or a seamless design to avoid visible panty lines.

Conclusion of Are You Supposed to Wear Underwear Under Compression Pants

Whether you should wear underwear under compression pants depends on personal preference and the design of the pants. Some compression pants have a built-in liner, while others do not. If you feel comfortable wearing your regular underwear, go for it. If you prefer not to wear underwear, make sure your compression pants have a built-in liner for extra support and comfort. Remember to choose soft and breathable fabrics and maintain proper hygiene to avoid skin irritation and infections.
