Types Of Indian Formal Wear A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you attending an Indian wedding or a formal event and wondering what to wear? Indian formal wear can be confusing, with so many options and styles to choose from. In this guide, we will break down the different types of Indian formal wear and help you choose the perfect outfit for your next occasion.

When it comes to Indian formal wear, there are several pain points that people often struggle with. Some of the common questions include:

  • What is the difference between a saree and a lehenga?
  • What kind of jewelry should I wear with my outfit?
  • How do I choose the right colors for my outfit?

If you have these questions or other concerns about Indian formal wear, you're not alone. Many people struggle with navigating the world of Indian fashion.

So, what exactly is Indian formal wear? In essence, it is clothing that is appropriate for formal occasions in India, such as weddings, religious ceremonies, and other formal events. Indian formal wear is known for its intricate designs, vibrant colors, and luxurious fabrics like silk, chiffon, and velvet.

In this guide, we will cover the most popular types of Indian formal wear and provide tips on how to style them. Here are the main types of Indian formal wear:

Saree

A saree is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body and over the shoulder. It is one of the most popular types of Indian formal wear and is worn by women of all ages. Sarees come in a wide range of designs, colors, and fabrics, including silk, cotton, and chiffon. Some of the most popular styles include the Banarasi saree, the Kanjeevaram saree, and the Chanderi saree.

My personal experience with wearing a saree was for my cousin's wedding. I chose a bright pink chiffon saree with gold embroidery, which I paired with heavy gold jewelry and a bold lip color. I received so many compliments and felt confident and beautiful all night long.

Lehenga

A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a blouse and a scarf. It is a popular choice for formal events like weddings and is often heavily embroidered or embellished. Lehengas come in a variety of styles and fabrics, such as silk, velvet, and net. Some of the most popular styles include the A-line lehenga, the mermaid lehenga, and the circular lehenga.

I wore a lehenga to my best friend's wedding and it was one of the most beautiful outfits I've ever worn. My lehenga was a deep burgundy color with gold embroidery and a matching dupatta. I paired it with gold jewelry and a bold smokey eye for a dramatic look.

Anarkali Suit

An Anarkali suit is a type of dress that features a long flowing skirt and a fitted bodice. It is a popular choice for formal events because it is comfortable, stylish, and easy to wear. Anarkali suits come in a variety of styles, such as floor-length, knee-length, and asymmetrical. They are often made of lightweight fabrics like chiffon and georgette and can be heavily embellished with embroidery or sequins.

Sherwani

A sherwani is a traditional Indian men's outfit that is worn for formal occasions like weddings and religious ceremonies. It is a long coat that is paired with trousers and is often made of luxurious fabrics like silk, velvet, and brocade. Sherwanis come in a variety of styles and colors, from classic black and white to bold jewel tones.

When it comes to choosing the right Indian formal wear, it's important to consider the occasion, the time of day, and your personal style. Whether you choose a saree, lehenga, Anarkali suit, or sherwani, make sure you feel comfortable and confident in your outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What kind of jewelry should I wear with my Indian formal wear?

A: When it comes to jewelry, it's best to keep it simple and let your outfit shine. For sarees and lehengas, go for statement earrings and a simple necklace. For Anarkali suits, a pair of chandelier earrings and a bracelet will complete your look. For men's outfits like the sherwani, a simple watch and a pair of cufflinks will add a touch of sophistication.

Q: How do I choose the right color for my Indian formal wear?

A: Indian formal wear is known for its vibrant colors, so don't be afraid to go bold. However, it's also important to consider the occasion and the time of day. For daytime events, pastel colors like peach, mint, and lavender are a great choice. For evening events, jewel tones like emerald, sapphire, and ruby will make you stand out.

Q: Can men wear sarees or lehengas?

A: While sarees and lehengas are traditionally worn by women, there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to fashion. If you're a man who wants to wear a saree or lehenga, go for it! Just make sure you choose an outfit that fits your body type and personal style.

Q: How do I style my hair for Indian formal wear?

A: For women, a simple bun or braid is a classic and elegant hairstyle that will complement any Indian formal wear. You can also add a touch of glamour with a hair accessory like a maang tikka or a flower crown. For men, a sleek and polished hairstyle like a side part or a slicked-back look is a great choice.

Conclusion of Types of Indian Formal Wear

Indian formal wear is a beautiful and vibrant world of fashion that is steeped in tradition and history. Whether you choose a saree, lehenga, Anarkali suit, or sherwani, make sure you choose an outfit that makes you feel confident and comfortable. With the right accessories and styling, you'll be sure to turn heads at your next formal event.
