Types of Suits

When shopping for a suit, it's important to know the different types available so you can choose the right one for your needs. Here are some of the most common types of suits:

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying a suit?

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a suit?

Q: How do I know if a suit fits me properly?

A: A suit should fit snugly but not be too tight. Make sure the sleeves and pants are the right length and that the jacket buttons comfortably.
