Product Designer Starting Salary In India What You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering a career as a product designer in India but worried about the starting salary? You're not alone. Many individuals in this field struggle with the same concerns. Understanding the industry and what you can expect in terms of compensation is crucial to making an informed decision.

The starting salary for a product designer in India varies depending on the company, location, and experience. Typically, entry-level professionals can expect to earn between 3-6 lakhs per annum. However, salaries can range from 2-10 lakhs per annum, depending on the specific factors mentioned above.

In summary, product designer starting salary in India can range from 2-10 lakhs per annum, with entry-level professionals earning between 3-6 lakhs per annum.

What Factors Influence Product Designer Starting Salary in India?

When it comes to product designer starting salary in India, there are several factors to consider. The first is the company you work for. Large corporations tend to offer higher salaries, whereas startups may be more modest in their compensation packages.

The second factor is location. Salaries in larger cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore tend to be higher due to the cost of living. On the other hand, smaller cities may offer lower salaries to compensate for the lower living expenses.

My Experience with Product Designer Starting Salary in India

As a product designer with two years of experience in a mid-sized company in Bangalore, I started with a salary of 4.5 lakhs per annum. While it was lower than what some of my peers were earning at larger corporations, I was satisfied with the compensation as it was in line with my expectations and experience level.

Tips for Negotiating Your Product Designer Starting Salary in India

When negotiating your product designer starting salary in India, it's essential to do your research. Research the average salaries for your experience level and location to get an idea of what you can expect. Additionally, be prepared to explain your skills, experience, and how they align with the company's goals.

Is it Possible to Increase Your Product Designer Starting Salary in India?

Yes, it is possible to increase your product designer starting salary in India. One way to achieve this is by gaining more experience and taking on additional responsibilities. Additionally, consider obtaining certifications or pursuing advanced education to increase your skillset and marketability.

Conclusion of Product Designer Starting Salary in India

Product designer starting salary in India can range from 2-10 lakhs per annum, with entry-level professionals earning between 3-6 lakhs per annum. When considering a career in this field, it's essential to research the industry and factors that influence compensation. With persistence and hard work, it's possible to increase your salary and advance in your career.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can product designers negotiate their starting salary in India?
A: Yes, product designers can negotiate their starting salary in India. It's essential to do research and prepare to justify your desired salary.

Q: How can product designers increase their salary in India?
A: Product designers can increase their salary in India by gaining more experience, taking on additional responsibilities, obtaining certifications, and pursuing advanced education.

Q: What is the average product designer starting salary in Bangalore?
A: The average product designer starting salary in Bangalore is around 4-5 lakhs per annum.

Q: What is the highest product designer starting salary in India?
A: The highest product designer starting salary in India can range from 8-10 lakhs per annum, depending on the company and location.
