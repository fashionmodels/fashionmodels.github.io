Discovering Original Surf Brands A Guide To Authenticity

Written by Aprilia Nov 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Surfing is more than just a sport or a hobby. It's a way of life that embodies freedom, adventure, and a deep connection to nature. But with the rise of mainstream surf culture, it can be hard to find original surf brands that truly capture the essence of the sport. Many surf brands today prioritize commercial success over authenticity, leaving surfers feeling disconnected from the culture they love.

If you're a surfer looking for original surf brands that embody the true spirit of the sport, you're not alone. Many surfers are searching for brands that prioritize quality, craftsmanship, and a genuine love for surfing. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best original surf brands out there and what makes them special.

So what exactly is the target of original surf brands? Simply put, these brands prioritize authenticity over commercial success. They're run by surfers, for surfers, and they focus on creating high-quality products that reflect the true essence of surfing. Whether it's boards, apparel, or accessories, original surf brands are committed to creating products that enhance the surfing experience and connect surfers to the ocean.

In this guide, we'll explore some of the best original surf brands and what makes them unique. From established brands to up-and-coming labels, we'll dive into what sets these companies apart and why they're worth checking out.

Patagonia Surf: A Commitment to Sustainability

Patagonia is a well-known outdoor brand that has made a name for itself in the surfing world. But what sets Patagonia Surf apart from other surf brands is their unwavering commitment to sustainability. From using recycled materials to supporting environmental causes, Patagonia Surf is dedicated to minimizing their impact on the planet while still creating high-quality surf products.

Personally, I've been a fan of Patagonia Surf for years. I first discovered the brand when I was looking for a new wetsuit, and I was immediately drawn to their commitment to sustainability. Since then, I've tried out several of their surf products, and I've been consistently impressed with the quality and durability of their gear.

Banks Journal: A Fusion of Surf and Style

Banks Journal is a relatively new brand that has quickly gained a following among surfers who appreciate a more fashion-forward approach to surf apparel. Their designs are inspired by both traditional surf culture and modern streetwear, creating a unique fusion of surf and style.

I first discovered Banks Journal when I was looking for a new pair of boardshorts. I was drawn to the unique patterns and prints, and I appreciated that the brand was doing something different in the surf world. Since then, I've tried out several of their products, and I've been consistently impressed with the quality and attention to detail.

The Importance of Authenticity in Surf Brands

One of the key factors that sets original surf brands apart from mainstream brands is their commitment to authenticity. Surfing is more than just a sport or a fashion statement - it's a way of life that's deeply rooted in culture and tradition. Original surf brands recognize this, and they prioritize creating products that reflect that culture and connect surfers to the ocean.

Authenticity is important for surfers because it allows us to stay connected to the sport in a meaningful way. When we wear or use products from original surf brands, we're not just buying into a trend - we're participating in a culture that we love and respect. This connection to the sport and the culture is what keeps surfers coming back year after year, regardless of trends or mainstream popularity.

How to Support Original Surf Brands

If you're a surfer looking to support original surf brands, there are a few things you can do. First and foremost, do your research. Look for brands that prioritize authenticity, quality, and a genuine love for surfing. Seek out brands that are run by surfers, and that have a strong connection to the ocean and the culture.

Secondly, invest in high-quality products that will last. Original surf brands prioritize craftsmanship and durability, so you can trust that their products will stand up to the elements and last for years to come. By investing in high-quality surf gear, you're not only supporting original surf brands - you're also investing in your own surfing experience.
