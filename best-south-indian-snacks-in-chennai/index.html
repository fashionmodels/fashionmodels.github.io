Discover The Best South Indian Snacks In Chennai

Written by William Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a foodie looking for the best South Indian snacks in Chennai? Do you want to indulge in traditional and mouth-watering delicacies that will leave your taste buds craving for more? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a culinary journey and showcase the best South Indian snacks you must try in Chennai.

South Indian snacks are known for their spicy and tangy flavors. They are a perfect blend of taste and nutrition, making them a popular choice for people of all ages. From crispy dosas to spicy vadas, Chennai has a wide range of South Indian snacks to offer.

If you are looking for the best South Indian snacks in Chennai, then you have come to the right place. We have curated a list of the must-try snacks that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Dosa

Dosa is a staple South Indian snack that is loved by people all over the world. It is a thin and crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter. The dosa is usually served with a side of coconut chutney, sambar, and tomato chutney. Some of the best places to try dosa in Chennai are Murugan Idli Shop, Sangeetha Restaurant, and Saravana Bhavan.

Vada

Vada is another popular South Indian snack that is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. It is a deep-fried doughnut-shaped snack made with urad dal and spices. It is usually served with sambar and coconut chutney. Some of the best places to try vada in Chennai are Ratna Café, Sangeetha Restaurant, and Murugan Idli Shop.

Idli

Idli is a soft and fluffy steamed cake made with fermented rice and lentil batter. It is a healthy and nutritious snack that is easy on the stomach. It is usually served with sambar and coconut chutney. Some of the best places to try idli in Chennai are Murugan Idli Shop, Sangeetha Restaurant, and Saravana Bhavan.

How to Make South Indian Snacks at Home

If you want to try making some of these South Indian snacks at home, here is a simple recipe for dosa:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Method:

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds and soak them in water for at least 4 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients to a fine batter.
  3. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  4. Cover the batter and let it ferment for at least 8 hours.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladleful of batter on it.
  6. Spread the batter in a circular motion to make a thin pancake.
  7. Add a few drops of oil around the edges and cook till it turns golden brown.
  8. Flip the dosa and cook for a few seconds on the other side.
  9. Remove from the pan and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
