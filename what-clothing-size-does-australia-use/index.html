What Clothing Size Does Australia Use?

Australia uses a unique sizing system that is different from the rest of the world. Australian clothing sizes are based on the metric system, which means that the measurements are in centimeters rather than inches. In general, Australian clothing sizes are smaller than US or UK sizes.

The sizes range from 4 to 32 for women and XS to 6XL for men. However, it's important to note that different brands may have varying sizes, so it's always recommended to check the size chart before making a purchase.
