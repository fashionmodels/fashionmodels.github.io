What To Wear With A Black Dress A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Black dresses are a staple in many women's wardrobes, but the question of what to wear with them can be a bit daunting. Whether you're dressing up for a formal event or keeping it casual on a night out, there are plenty of options to choose from to complement your little black dress.

Many women struggle with finding the right accessories and shoes to pair with their black dress. They want to look stylish and put-together, but don't want to overdo it or clash with the dress itself. It can be a tricky balance to strike, but with a few key tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect outfit to go with your black dress.

The key to what to wear with a black dress is to keep it simple and classic. Black is a versatile color that goes with just about anything, so you can have fun with your accessories and shoes without worrying about clashing. Here are some of the best options to consider:

Statement Jewelry

One of the easiest ways to dress up a black dress is to add some statement jewelry. A bold necklace or a pair of chandelier earrings can add some sparkle and shine to your outfit, making it perfect for a night out or a formal event. Just be sure to keep the rest of your accessories simple, so as not to overwhelm your look.

Colorful Shoes

Another way to add some interest to your black dress is to wear colorful shoes. This could mean anything from a bright red pump to a pair of funky printed flats. Just be sure to choose a color or pattern that complements your dress, rather than clashes with it.

Belts and Scarves

If you're looking for a way to cinch in your waist or add some texture to your outfit, consider adding a belt or scarf. A skinny belt can add some definition to your waistline, while a chunky scarf can add some warmth and texture to your look.

Jackets or Blazers

If you're heading to a more formal event, or just want to add some structure to your outfit, consider adding a jacket or blazer. A fitted blazer can add some sophistication to your look, while a leather jacket can add some edge.

Q&A

Q: Can I wear black shoes with a black dress?

A: Yes, black shoes are a classic option that will always look chic with a black dress. Just be sure to choose a style that complements the dress, such as a strappy sandal or a pointed-toe pump.

Q: What kind of jewelry should I wear with a black dress?

A: It depends on the event and your personal style, but statement jewelry is always a great option. A bold necklace or a pair of chandelier earrings can add some sparkle and shine to your outfit.

Q: What kind of jacket should I wear with a black dress?

A: Again, it depends on the event and your personal style. A fitted blazer can add some sophistication to your look, while a leather jacket can add some edge.

Q: Can I wear tights with a black dress?

A: Yes, tights are a great way to add some warmth and texture to your outfit. Just be sure to choose a color and pattern that complements your dress.

Conclusion of What to Wear With a Black Dress

When it comes to what to wear with a black dress, the options are endless. Whether you opt for statement jewelry, colorful shoes, or a structured jacket, the key is to keep it simple and classic. With these tips in mind, you can easily find the perfect outfit to complement your little black dress, no matter the occasion.
