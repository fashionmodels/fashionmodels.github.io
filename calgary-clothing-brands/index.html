Discovering The Best Calgary Clothing Brands

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Calgary is a city that's always on the move, and the fashion scene is no exception. Whether you're looking for casual comfort or high-end couture, there's a Calgary clothing brand that's perfect for you. So, why stick to the same old brands when you can explore the local fashion scene and discover something new and exciting?

Shopping for clothes can be a challenge, especially if you're not sure where to look or what to expect. You might have trouble finding clothes that fit well, or you might feel overwhelmed by the sheer number of options available. Plus, with so many fast fashion brands out there, it can be difficult to find clothes that are ethically made and environmentally friendly.

If you're looking for high-quality clothes that are made by local designers, then Calgary clothing brands are the perfect solution. These brands offer unique styles and designs that you won't find anywhere else, and they're committed to using sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the best Calgary clothing brands and what makes them so special. We'll also explore the local fashion scene and offer some tips on how to find the perfect clothes for your style and budget.

Local Favorites: The Best Calgary Clothing Brands

When it comes to Calgary clothing brands, there are plenty of options to choose from. Here are just a few of our favorite local brands:

1. Camp Brand Goods

Founded in 2011, Camp Brand Goods is a Calgary-based clothing brand that specializes in casual wear that's perfect for outdoor adventures. From cozy sweatshirts to durable backpacks, Camp Brand Goods offers a wide range of products that are designed to last. Plus, all of their products are made in Canada using sustainable materials.

2. Adorn Boutique

Adorn Boutique is a women's clothing boutique that offers a carefully curated selection of clothing, accessories, and beauty products. With a focus on sustainable and ethically made products, Adorn Boutique is committed to providing customers with high-quality products that are both stylish and responsible.

3. Local Laundry

Local Laundry is a clothing brand that's all about celebrating local communities. Their products feature unique designs that pay tribute to different neighborhoods and landmarks in Calgary, and they donate 10% of their profits to local charities. Plus, all of their products are made using sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices.

4. CoutuKitsch

CoutuKitsch is a jewelry brand that offers unique and stylish pieces that are perfect for any occasion. From delicate necklaces to bold statement earrings, CoutuKitsch has something for everyone. Plus, all of their jewelry is handmade in Calgary using ethically sourced materials.

My Personal Experience with Calgary Clothing Brands

As someone who's always on the lookout for unique and sustainable fashion options, I love exploring the local Calgary clothing scene. One of my favorite brands is Camp Brand Goods, which offers cozy and stylish sweatshirts that are perfect for chilly Calgary evenings. I also love shopping at Adorn Boutique, which offers a great selection of sustainable and ethically made clothing and accessories.

But perhaps my favorite thing about shopping for Calgary clothing brands is the sense of community that comes with it. When you buy from local designers and shops, you're supporting small businesses and contributing to the local economy. Plus, it's a great way to meet new people and discover new and exciting fashion options.

How to Find the Best Calgary Clothing Brands

If you're interested in exploring the local Calgary clothing scene, there are a few things you can do to get started:

1. Look for local markets and events

Calgary is home to a number of markets and events that showcase local designers and artisans. Check out events like Market Collective or the Calgary Farmers' Market to discover new and exciting fashion options.

2. Do your research

Research local Calgary clothing brands online to get a sense of their style and design aesthetic. You can also read reviews from other customers to get a sense of the quality of the products and customer service.

3. Shop at local boutiques

If you're looking for a more personalized shopping experience, consider shopping at local boutiques. Many of these shops offer a carefully curated selection of clothing and accessories, and they can offer personalized styling advice and recommendations.

Question and Answer

Q: Are Calgary clothing brands expensive?

A: Not necessarily. While some local designers and boutiques offer high-end, luxury clothing, there are also plenty of affordable options available. Many brands are committed to offering sustainable and ethically made products at an affordable price point.

Q: Are Calgary clothing brands only for women?

A: No! While some brands may specialize in women's clothing, there are also plenty of options available for men and children as well.

Q: Are Calgary clothing brands eco-friendly?

A: Many Calgary clothing brands are committed to using sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices. However, it's always a good idea to do your research and read reviews to ensure that you're buying from a brand that aligns with your values.

Q: Can I buy Calgary clothing brands online?

A: Yes! Many local designers and boutiques offer online shopping options, so you can shop from the comfort of your own home.

Conclusion of Calgary Clothing Brands

Whether you're a longtime resident or a first-time visitor to Calgary, exploring the local clothing scene is a great way to discover unique and sustainable fashion options. From cozy sweatshirts to stylish jewelry, there's something for everyone in the world of Calgary clothing brands. So why not take a break from the fast fashion brands and discover something new and exciting today?
