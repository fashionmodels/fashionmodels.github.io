Are you looking to invest in stocks that align with your values and also offer a good return on investment? Look no further than green stocks. In this guide, we will dive into the world of green stocks to invest in 2021 UK and explore the top options for investors.

What are Green Stocks?

Green stocks are stocks of companies that are environmentally friendly and sustainable. These companies prioritize reducing their carbon footprint, conserving natural resources, and promoting sustainable practices. Investing in green stocks not only allows you to contribute to a greener planet but also offers a great return on investment.

Green stocks are also referred to as eco-friendly stocks or ethical stocks. These stocks are typically from companies in the renewable energy, electric vehicle, and sustainable agriculture industries, among others.

Why Invest in Green Stocks?

Investing in green stocks is an excellent way to make a positive impact on the planet while also earning a return on investment. Green companies are at the forefront of environmentally sustainable practices and are poised for significant growth in the coming years. As the world becomes more environmentally conscious, demand for green products and services will only increase.

Additionally, investing in green stocks can also offer diversification benefits to your portfolio. As the global economy shifts towards sustainability, green stocks are becoming increasingly mainstream, and investing in them can provide a hedge against volatility in other sectors.

Top Green Stocks to Invest in 2021 UK

Here are the top green stocks to invest in 2021 UK:

1. Tesla

Tesla is a leading electric vehicle and clean energy company. The company is at the forefront of developing sustainable energy solutions and is committed to reducing carbon emissions. Tesla has a market capitalization of over $800 billion, making it one of the most valuable companies in the world.

2. Orsted

Orsted is a Danish renewable energy company that specializes in offshore wind farms. The company is committed to reducing carbon emissions and is a leading player in the global shift towards renewable energy. Orsted has a market capitalization of over $60 billion.

3. Vestas Wind Systems

Vestas Wind Systems is a Danish wind turbine manufacturer that specializes in the design, manufacture, installation, and maintenance of wind turbines. The company has a market capitalization of over $38 billion and is committed to reducing carbon emissions through the use of renewable energy sources.

4. NextEra Energy

NextEra Energy is a leading renewable energy company based in the United States. The company specializes in wind and solar energy, and its market capitalization is over $170 billion. NextEra Energy is committed to reducing carbon emissions and is a leader in the shift towards renewable energy.

5. Brookfield Renewable Partners

Brookfield Renewable Partners is a global renewable energy company that specializes in hydroelectric, wind, and solar power. The company has a market capitalization of over $15 billion and is committed to reducing carbon emissions through sustainable energy solutions.

How to Invest in Green Stocks?

Investing in green stocks is relatively simple. You can invest in green stocks through a stockbroker, robo-advisor, or an online trading platform. Before investing, it is essential to research the companies you are interested in and their environmental policies and practices.

It is also important to consider your investment goals and risk tolerance when investing in green stocks. Green stocks, like any other investment, come with risks and uncertainties, and it is crucial to invest only what you can afford to lose.
