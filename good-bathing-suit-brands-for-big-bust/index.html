Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for in a bathing suit for big busts?

A: Look for bathing suits that provide underwire support, adjustable straps, and a wide band around the back. These features will ensure that your bathing suit fits properly and provides the support you need.

Q: What types of bathing suits are best for big busts?

A: One-piece bathing suits and bikinis with underwire support are the best options for big busts. Look for bathing suits that have adjustable straps and a wide band around the back for added support.

Q: What should I avoid when looking for a bathing suit for big busts?

A: Avoid bathing suits that don't have underwire support or adjustable straps. These features are essential for providing the support you need. Also, avoid bathing suits with thin straps, as they can dig into your shoulders and cause discomfort.
