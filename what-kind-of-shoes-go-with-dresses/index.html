What Kind Of Shoes Go With Dresses A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling with finding the perfect pair of shoes to wear with your favorite dress? Do you feel overwhelmed with the countless options available in the market? Fear not, as we have got you covered with this comprehensive guide on what kind of shoes go with dresses.

It can be frustrating to put together an outfit that looks great from head to toe, only to realize that the shoes you chose don't match the dress. This can happen due to a lack of knowledge about what kind of shoes to wear with dresses or simply because of the wide variety of shoes available in the market.

When it comes to pairing shoes with dresses, the possibilities are endless. However, there are some general guidelines that you can follow to ensure you always look your best.

In this article, we will discuss the different types of shoes that work best with dresses, as well as some tips and tricks to help you make the right choice every time.

What Kind of Shoes Go with Dresses?

Before we dive into the specifics, let's start with the basics. When choosing shoes to wear with a dress, the most important thing to consider is the occasion. The shoes you wear to a formal event will be different from the shoes you wear to a casual party.

Another important factor to consider is the style of the dress. Different dress styles require different types of shoes to complement them. For instance, a maxi dress will look great with sandals, while a pencil dress will look better with pumps.

Here are some common shoe styles that go well with different types of dresses:

1. Sandals

Sandals are a great choice for dresses that are casual or have a bohemian vibe. They work well with maxi dresses, sundresses, and even midi dresses. Look for sandals with straps that wrap around your ankles for a more refined look.

2. Pumps

Pumps are a classic shoe style that look great with almost any dress. They work particularly well with pencil dresses, midi dresses, and sheath dresses. For a more casual look, opt for pumps with a low heel.

3. Flats

Flats are a comfortable and stylish option that work well with most types of dresses. They are particularly well-suited for casual dresses, such as sundresses and shirt dresses. For a more formal look, opt for flats with embellishments or metallic accents.

4. Boots

Boots are a great option for dresses that are worn in the fall or winter. They work well with sweater dresses, maxi dresses, and even midi dresses. Look for boots that hit just above the knee for a chic look.

Personal Experience

When it comes to pairing shoes with dresses, my go-to option is always pumps. They are versatile, elegant, and can be worn with almost any dress style. I particularly love wearing nude pumps, as they go with everything and make my legs look longer.

However, I recently tried wearing ankle boots with a maxi dress, and I was pleasantly surprised by how well it turned out. The boots added a touch of edge to the feminine dress, and I received many compliments on the outfit.

Tips for Choosing Shoes to Wear with Dresses

Here are some tips and tricks to help you choose the perfect shoes to wear with your dress:

1. Consider the occasion

As mentioned earlier, the occasion plays a significant role in determining the type of shoes you should wear with your dress. For a formal event, opt for dressy shoes, such as pumps or strappy heels. For a casual event, sandals or flats would be a better option.

2. Match the colors

When in doubt, match the color of your shoes to your dress. This creates a cohesive look that is visually appealing. If you are wearing a printed dress, choose a color from the print to match your shoes.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to try different shoe styles with your dresses. You may be surprised by how well unconventional pairings work together. For instance, try wearing sneakers with a midi dress or ankle boots with a maxi dress.

4. Pay attention to the details

The details of your shoes can make all the difference in your outfit. Look for shoes with interesting textures, embellishments, or prints to add a pop of personality to your dress.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a dress?

A: Yes, you can! Sneakers look great with casual dresses, such as t-shirt dresses or midi dresses. Opt for sneakers in a neutral color, such as white or black, to keep the look simple and chic.

Q: Can I wear flip flops with a dress?

A: While flip flops may be comfortable, they are not the best option to wear with a dress. They do not provide enough support or structure to complement the dress, and can make the outfit look sloppy.

Q: Can I wear boots with a short dress?

A: Yes, you can! Boots look great with short dresses, such as mini dresses or A-line dresses. Look for boots that hit just above the knee for a balanced look.

Q: Can I wear flats with a formal dress?

A: Yes, you can! Flats can be a great option for a formal dress, especially if you are attending an event where you will be on your feet for a long time. Look for flats with embellishments or metallic accents to add a touch of glamour to your outfit.

Conclusion of What Kind of Shoes Go with Dresses

Choosing the right shoes to wear with a dress can be a daunting task, but with the right knowledge and a little experimentation, you can create outfits that look amazing. Remember to consider the occasion, dress style, and colors when choosing shoes, and don't be afraid to try different styles to find what works best for you.
