The Best Menswear Designers In London A Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

London is known for its fashion and style, particularly in menswear. However, with so many designers and brands to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this blog post, we'll be exploring the best menswear designers in London and why they're worth checking out.

If you're looking for quality, style, and innovation in menswear, you can't go wrong with the designers in London. From high-end luxury brands to more affordable options, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a classic suit, a casual outfit, or something in between, the designers in London have got you covered.

So, without further ado, let's dive into the best menswear designers in London and what makes them stand out.

The Savile Row Tailors

When it comes to classic British tailoring, Savile Row is the place to go. This street in Mayfair is home to some of the most renowned tailors in the world, including Gieves & Hawkes, Henry Poole, and Huntsman. These tailors have been around for centuries, and their expertise and attention to detail are unparalleled. If you're looking for a bespoke suit that will fit you like a glove, Savile Row is the place to go.

Burberry

Burberry is a British luxury brand that's been around since 1856. While the brand is known for its iconic trench coats, they also offer a wide range of menswear that's both stylish and timeless. From tailored suits to casual pieces, Burberry is a great option for those looking for high-quality menswear with a British twist.

Paul Smith

Paul Smith is a British fashion designer who's been in the industry for over 50 years. His menswear collections are known for their playful twists on classic styles, as well as their use of bold colors and patterns. If you're looking for something unique and eye-catching, Paul Smith is the designer for you.

Alexander McQueen

Alexander McQueen was a British fashion designer who was known for his avant-garde designs and theatrical runway shows. While he passed away in 2010, his brand continues to be a major player in the fashion industry. If you're looking for something edgy and unconventional, Alexander McQueen is the designer for you.

Question and Answer

Q: What's the difference between Savile Row tailors and other tailors?

A: Savile Row tailors are known for their bespoke services, which means that every garment is made specifically for the individual. This means that the garment will fit perfectly and be tailored to the individual's specific measurements and preferences. Other tailors may offer made-to-measure services, which are not as personalized as bespoke.

Q: Is Burberry only known for its trench coats?

A: While Burberry is known for its trench coats, the brand offers a wide range of menswear, including suits, jackets, and casual pieces. Burberry is a great option for those looking for high-quality menswear that's both stylish and timeless.

Q: What sets Paul Smith apart from other designers?

A: Paul Smith is known for his playful twists on classic styles, as well as his use of bold colors and patterns. His designs are unique and eye-catching, making them a great option for those looking for something different.

Q: What kind of customer would be interested in Alexander McQueen?

A: Alexander McQueen is known for his avant-garde designs and theatrical runway shows. His designs are edgy and unconventional, making them a great option for those who want to make a statement with their clothing.

Conclusion of Best Menswear Designers in London

London is home to some of the best menswear designers in the world, and it's worth exploring what they have to offer. From classic tailors to high-end luxury brands, there's something for every style and budget. So if you're looking to up your fashion game, be sure to check out the best menswear designers in London.
