Ralph Lauren Men's Shirts Size Chart Explained

When it comes to choosing the right size for your Ralph Lauren shirt, the first step is to take your body measurements. You will need to measure your chest, neck, and sleeve length to find the right size. Once you have your measurements, you can use the Ralph Lauren Men's Shirts Size Chart to find the perfect size for you.

It's important to note that Ralph Lauren offers different fits for its shirts. The classic fit is a more relaxed fit, while the slim fit is a more fitted option. The custom fit is a combination of the classic and slim fits. When using the size chart, make sure to select the appropriate fit for your body type.

Tips for Choosing the Right Size

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using the Ralph Lauren Men's Shirts Size Chart:

  • Measure yourself accurately to ensure the best fit.
  • Consider the fit of the shirt when choosing your size.
  • If you are between sizes, size up for a more relaxed fit or size down for a slimmer fit.
