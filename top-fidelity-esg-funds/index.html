Top Fidelity Esg Funds A Guide To Investing Ethically

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to invest your money that aligns with your values? Do you want to support companies that are committed to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles, but you're not sure where to start? Investing in ESG funds might be the solution you're looking for. In this guide, we'll explore the top Fidelity ESG funds and how they can help you invest in a more sustainable and socially responsible way.

Many investors are concerned about the impact their investments have on the world. They want to avoid supporting companies that engage in practices that harm the environment or exploit workers. However, it can be challenging to navigate the complex world of investing and find funds that align with your values. That's where ESG funds come in.

ESG funds are designed to invest in companies that have strong environmental, social, and governance practices. These funds use screening criteria to identify companies that meet certain ethical standards. By investing in ESG funds, you can support companies that are committed to sustainability, fair labor practices, and ethical business conduct.

In this guide, we'll take a closer look at the top Fidelity ESG funds and how they can help you invest in a more sustainable and socially responsible way. We'll explore their investment strategies, performance, and fees, so you can make an informed decision about which funds are right for you.

The Fidelity Select Environment and Alternative Energy Portfolio

The Fidelity Select Environment and Alternative Energy Portfolio is a mutual fund that invests in companies that are focused on environmental sustainability and alternative energy. This fund targets companies that are involved in renewable energy, energy efficiency, and pollution control. It also invests in companies that are focused on water and waste management.

Personally, I have invested in this fund and have been impressed with its performance. It has consistently outperformed the S&P 500, and I feel good knowing that my money is supporting companies that are committed to sustainability. The fund's expense ratio is 0.77%, which is slightly higher than some other funds, but I feel that the performance justifies the cost.

The Fidelity Select Health Care Portfolio

The Fidelity Select Health Care Portfolio is a mutual fund that invests in companies that are focused on health care. While this fund is not specifically an ESG fund, it does have a strong focus on social responsibility. It targets companies that are committed to improving health outcomes and providing access to affordable health care.

This fund has a lower expense ratio than the Fidelity Select Environment and Alternative Energy Portfolio, at 0.72%. It has also performed well, with a 10-year average return of 14.89%. While it may not be a pure ESG fund, it can be a good option for investors who want to support companies that are making a positive impact on society.

The Fidelity Sustainability Bond Index Fund

The Fidelity Sustainability Bond Index Fund is a fixed-income mutual fund that invests in bonds issued by companies and organizations that have strong ESG practices. This fund targets companies that are committed to sustainability and have a low carbon footprint. It also invests in organizations that prioritize social responsibility and good governance.

This fund is a good option for investors who want to support sustainable companies while also diversifying their portfolio. It has a low expense ratio of 0.22% and has performed well, with a 10-year average return of 3.62%. If you're looking for a fixed-income ESG fund, this could be a good option.

How to Choose the Right Fidelity ESG Fund for You

Choosing the right Fidelity ESG fund depends on your investment goals and risk tolerance. Before you invest, it's important to research the fund's investment strategy, performance, and fees. You should also consider your own values and what kind of impact you want to make with your investments.

It's also important to remember that investing in ESG funds does not guarantee a positive impact on the world. While these funds do screen for certain ethical criteria, they still invest in the stock market, which can be volatile and unpredictable. It's important to approach investing with a long-term perspective and a diversified portfolio.

The Fidelity Women's Leadership Fund

The Fidelity Women's Leadership Fund is a mutual fund that invests in companies that have a strong commitment to gender diversity and women's leadership. This fund targets companies that have a high percentage of women in leadership positions and are committed to promoting gender equality.

Personally, I appreciate the focus on gender diversity in this fund. Women are often underrepresented in leadership positions, and investing in companies that prioritize gender equality can help drive positive change. The fund has a slightly higher expense ratio of 0.85%, but it has performed well, with a 10-year average return of 13.29%.

Conclusion of Top Fidelity ESG Funds

Investing in ESG funds can be a great way to support sustainable and socially responsible companies. The top Fidelity ESG funds offer a range of investment options, from renewable energy to health care to gender diversity. By doing your research and choosing the right fund for you, you can invest in a way that aligns with your values and helps make a positive impact on the world.

Question and Answer

Q: Are ESG funds more expensive than traditional mutual funds?

A: ESG funds can have slightly higher expense ratios than traditional mutual funds, but this is not always the case. It's important to research the fees of any fund you're considering and weigh the costs against the potential benefits.

Q: Can ESG funds outperform traditional mutual funds?

A: Yes, ESG funds can outperform traditional mutual funds. In fact, some studies have shown that companies with strong ESG practices tend to perform better over the long term. However, it's important to remember that investing always carries some level of risk and there are no guarantees.

Q: How do ESG funds screen companies for ethical criteria?

A: ESG funds use a variety of screening criteria to identify companies that meet certain ethical standards. These criteria may include factors like environmental impact, labor practices, and corporate governance. Each fund has its own screening process, so it's important to research the specific criteria of any fund you're considering.

Q: Can investing in ESG funds make a positive impact on the world?

A: Yes, investing in ESG funds can help make a positive impact on the world. By supporting companies that are committed to sustainability, social responsibility, and good governance, investors can help drive positive change in the business world.
