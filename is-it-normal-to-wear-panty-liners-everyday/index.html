The Benefits and Risks of Wearing Panty Liners Every Day

There are several benefits to wearing panty liners every day. They can help manage vaginal discharge, reduce odor, and provide a sense of cleanliness. However, there are also some risks to consider. Q: Can wearing panty liners every day cause a yeast infection?

Q: Can I wear panty liners during exercise?

A: Yes, you can wear panty liners during exercise to manage sweat and discharge. However, it's important to change your liner after your workout to maintain hygiene.
