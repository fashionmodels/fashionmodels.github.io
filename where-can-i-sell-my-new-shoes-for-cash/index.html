Where Can I Sell My New Shoes For Cash

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to sell your new shoes for some quick cash? Perhaps you bought a pair of shoes that don't fit quite right or just aren't your style. Or maybe you received a gift that you don't want to keep. Whatever the reason, selling your shoes can be a great way to make some extra money. However, figuring out where to sell them can be a challenge.

It can be frustrating to have brand new shoes that you don't want, but not know where to sell them. You might be worried about scams or not getting a fair price for your shoes. Additionally, you might not want to deal with the hassle of shipping your shoes if you sell them online.

One option for selling your new shoes is to sell them online through platforms like eBay, Poshmark, or Mercari. These platforms allow you to create listings and sell your shoes to buyers all over the country. However, you'll need to factor in shipping costs and potential fees from the platform.

You can also sell your shoes locally through apps like OfferUp or Facebook Marketplace. This can be a good option if you want to avoid shipping costs and meet the buyer in person. However, you may have to deal with lowball offers or no-shows from potential buyers.

If you have a high-end pair of shoes or sneakers, you might consider selling them through a consignment store or website. These places specialize in selling luxury goods and can often get you a better price for your shoes. However, they may take a commission on the sale.

Selling Shoes to a Resale Shop

I recently sold a pair of brand new shoes to a local resale shop. I had purchased the shoes online and they didn't fit me quite right. Instead of dealing with shipping them back, I decided to try to sell them locally. I found a resale shop near me that specializes in shoes and brought them in. The staff looked over the shoes to make sure they were in good condition and offered me a fair price. I was able to walk out of the store with cash in hand, which was a great feeling.

Selling Shoes through Social Media

A friend of mine recently sold a pair of new shoes through Facebook Marketplace. She took some pictures of the shoes and posted them on Facebook, along with a description and asking price. Within a few hours, she had several interested buyers. She agreed to meet up with one of them in a public place and completed the sale. She was happy to have sold the shoes so quickly and without having to deal with shipping.

Consignment Stores for Selling Shoes

If you have a high-end pair of shoes or sneakers, you might consider selling them through a consignment store. These stores specialize in selling luxury goods and can often get you a better price for your shoes than you would get through a resale shop or online platform. However, they may take a commission on the sale, so be sure to read the terms carefully.

Selling Shoes to Friends and Family

If you're not comfortable selling your shoes to strangers, you might consider selling them to friends or family members. You could post on social media or send a message to your contacts to see if anyone is interested in buying your shoes. This can be a good option if you don't need to sell the shoes quickly and want to avoid dealing with shipping or fees.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I sell my shoes if they've been worn?

A: Yes, you can still sell your shoes if they've been worn. However, they may not sell for as much as a brand new pair.

Q: How do I know if I'm getting a fair price for my shoes?

A: Do some research on the brand and style of your shoes to get an idea of how much they are worth. You can also check prices on similar shoes on resale platforms to see what they are selling for.

Q: Do I have to pay to list my shoes on resale platforms?

A: It depends on the platform. Some platforms charge a fee to list your items, while others take a commission on the sale.

Q: What should I do if I receive a lowball offer on my shoes?

A: You can either counteroffer with a higher price or decline the offer and wait for a better one.

Conclusion of Where Can I Sell My New Shoes for Cash

Selling your new shoes for cash can be a great way to make some extra money. There are a variety of platforms and methods you can use to sell your shoes, including online resale platforms, local apps, consignment stores, and selling to friends or family. Consider the pros and cons of each option to decide which one is right for you. By following these tips, you can sell your shoes quickly and safely, and get a fair price for them.
