What Jeans To Wear With Ankle Boots A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of jeans to wear with your ankle boots? Do you find yourself feeling frustrated and lost when trying to create the perfect outfit? Look no further! We've got you covered in this comprehensive guide on what jeans to wear with ankle boots.

One of the most common pain points when it comes to putting together an outfit with ankle boots is figuring out which style of jeans will complement them best. With so many different cuts, lengths, and washes available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

First and foremost, it's important to consider the style of ankle boot you'll be wearing. Are they sleek and streamlined or chunky and bohemian? This will affect the style of jeans you choose to pair with them.

To make things easier, we've broken down the different styles of jeans and ankle boots and what combinations work best.

Skinny Jeans and Ankle Boots

Personally, I love pairing skinny jeans with ankle boots. It's a classic look that never goes out of style. The key is to make sure the jeans are fitted enough to tuck into the boots without bunching up, but not so tight that they're uncomfortable. Choose a high-waisted pair for a chic, elongating effect.

Straight-Leg Jeans and Ankle Boots

Straight-leg jeans are a great option for those who want a more relaxed look. They provide a looser fit that complements chunky ankle boots well. Roll up the cuffs for a laid-back vibe. This style also works well with heeled ankle boots for a more polished look.

Boyfriend Jeans and Ankle Boots

Boyfriend jeans are a great option for those who want to add a touch of masculinity to their outfit. The relaxed fit of the jeans contrasts nicely with the sleekness of ankle boots. Choose a cuffed pair for a more polished look.

Flared Jeans and Ankle Boots

Flared jeans and ankle boots may seem like an unlikely combination, but they can actually work really well together. The key is to make sure the jeans are long enough to cover the top of the boots, but not so long that they're dragging on the ground. This pairing works particularly well with heeled ankle boots.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with bootcut jeans?

A: It's not recommended to wear ankle boots with bootcut jeans, as the combination can create an awkward silhouette. Instead, opt for a longer boot or a cropped flare jean.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with white jeans?

A: Yes! Ankle boots look great with white jeans, particularly when paired with a cozy sweater or blazer.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with wide-leg jeans?

A: It's not recommended to wear ankle boots with wide-leg jeans, as the combination can make your legs look shorter than they are. Instead, opt for a longer boot or a cropped straight-leg jean.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with cropped jeans?

A: Absolutely! Cropped jeans are a great way to show off your ankle boots. Just make sure the hem of the jeans hits at the right spot on your leg to avoid any awkward gaps.

Conclusion of What Jeans to Wear with Ankle Boots

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of jeans to wear with ankle boots, there's no one-size-fits-all answer. It's important to consider the style of boot you'll be wearing and the look you're going for. Whether you choose skinny, straight-leg, boyfriend, or flared jeans, make sure you feel confident and comfortable in your outfit. With these tips, you'll be a pro at styling ankle boots with jeans in no time!
