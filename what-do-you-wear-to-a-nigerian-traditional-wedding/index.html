What Do You Wear To A Nigerian Traditional Wedding

Written by Aprilia Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Attending a Nigerian traditional wedding can be an exciting and memorable experience. It's an occasion where you get to witness the rich culture and traditions of Nigeria, and of course, dress up in traditional attire. However, figuring out what to wear can be a daunting task. In this blog post, we will explore what to wear to a Nigerian traditional wedding and provide you with some tips and ideas to help you make the perfect outfit choice.

When it comes to attending a Nigerian traditional wedding, there are a few pain points that people often encounter. These include not knowing the dress code, feeling unsure about what is appropriate to wear, and finding it difficult to source traditional attire. These concerns can be particularly challenging for those who are attending a Nigerian wedding for the first time or have never been to Nigeria before.

The dress code for a Nigerian traditional wedding is typically traditional attire. For men, this means wearing a traditional Nigerian outfit such as a Buba, Agbada, or Danshiki. Women, on the other hand, have a wider range of options. They can wear a traditional Nigerian dress known as an Ankara or Gele, which is a headwrap made from a long piece of fabric. Alternatively, they can opt for a modern dress made with Nigerian fabrics or a Western-style outfit with Nigerian-inspired accessories.

In summary, when attending a Nigerian traditional wedding, it's important to dress in traditional attire, either a Buba, Agbada, or Danshiki for men and an Ankara or Gele for women. However, there are modern and Western-style options available as well.

What to Wear to a Nigerian Traditional Wedding: Personal Experience

When I attended my first Nigerian traditional wedding, I was unsure what to wear. I didn't want to offend anyone by wearing the wrong thing, so I did some research beforehand. I ended up wearing a modern dress made with Nigerian fabric and accessorized with a Gele. It was a great choice because I felt comfortable, stylish, and respectful of the tradition.

For men, my friend who attended a Nigerian wedding wore an Agbada and accessorized with a matching cap and shoes. He looked fantastic and received compliments throughout the night.

Where to Find Traditional Attire

One of the most challenging aspects of attending a Nigerian traditional wedding is finding traditional attire. However, there are several options available. You can try shopping at local Nigerian markets or online stores that specialize in traditional clothing. Alternatively, you can have a tailor make you a custom outfit. Make sure to plan ahead and give yourself enough time to find the perfect outfit.

Tips for Choosing the Right Outfit

When choosing an outfit for a Nigerian traditional wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the color scheme of the wedding. Nigerian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, so try to choose an outfit that complements the wedding colors. Secondly, make sure to wear comfortable shoes as you will be expected to dance at some point during the wedding. Lastly, accessorize with traditional Nigerian jewelry or headwraps to complete the outfit.

How to Tie a Gele

A Gele is a headwrap made from a long piece of fabric. It's a traditional Nigerian accessory worn by women at special occasions such as weddings. Tying a Gele can be a challenging task, but there are several tutorials available online that can help. It's best to practice beforehand or have someone help you tie it on the day of the wedding.

What Do You Wear to a Nigerian Traditional Wedding: Personal Experience

During my cousin's Nigerian traditional wedding, I wore an Ankara dress accessorized with a matching Gele. It was my first time wearing a Gele, and I was nervous about tying it correctly. Luckily, my aunt helped me, and it turned out great. I received many compliments on my outfit, and it made the occasion even more special.

Question and Answer

Q: Can men wear Western-style suits to a Nigerian traditional wedding?

A: While traditional attire is preferred, men can wear Western-style suits as long as they are respectful and appropriate for the occasion.

Q: Is it appropriate to wear black to a Nigerian traditional wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing black to a Nigerian wedding as it's considered a mourning color in Nigerian culture.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a Nigerian traditional wedding?

A: No, jeans are not appropriate for a Nigerian traditional wedding. Stick to traditional attire or a smart casual outfit.

Q: Can I wear a floor-length gown to a Nigerian traditional wedding?

A: Yes, a floor-length gown made with Nigerian fabric or a traditional Nigerian dress such as an Ankara is appropriate for a Nigerian traditional wedding.

Conclusion of What Do You Wear to a Nigerian Traditional Wedding

Attending a Nigerian traditional wedding is an exciting experience, but figuring out what to wear can be a challenge. Remember to dress in traditional attire, choose an outfit that complements the wedding color scheme, and accessorize with traditional Nigerian jewelry or headwraps. With these tips, you'll be sure to make the perfect outfit choice.
