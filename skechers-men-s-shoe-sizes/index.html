When it comes to buying shoes, measuring your feet is the first step to finding the right size. To measure your feet, you will need a ruler, a piece of paper, and a pen or pencil. Here's how to do it:

1. Place the piece of paper on the floor and stand on it with your heel against a wall.

2. Use the pen or pencil to mark the longest part of your foot on the paper. This is usually your big toe, but it can be different for some people.

3. Use the ruler to measure the distance between the wall and the mark on the paper. This is the length of your foot.

4. Repeat the process for your other foot, as your feet may be slightly different sizes.

Once you have measured your feet, you can use the measurements to find the right size of Skechers shoes. You can find size charts online that will help you determine the right size based on your measurements.
