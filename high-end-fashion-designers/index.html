Discovering High End Fashion Designers A Guide To The Best In The Industry

Written by Robby Oct 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of browsing through the same old high street stores and want to elevate your wardrobe with clothing that is truly exceptional? Look no further than high end fashion designers who create clothing that is both luxurious and unique.

While high end fashion designers may seem out of reach for the average person, there are ways to incorporate their designs into your wardrobe without breaking the bank. The key is to invest in timeless pieces that will last for years to come, rather than constantly buying fast fashion items that quickly fall apart.

So, who exactly are high end fashion designers and what makes them so special? In short, high end fashion designers are those who create clothing that is made with the highest quality materials and is often hand-crafted by skilled artisans. These designers work tirelessly to create clothing that is both innovative and visually stunning, using their creativity and expertise to push the boundaries of fashion.

The Iconic Fashion Houses

One of the most recognizable names in high end fashion is Chanel, founded by Coco Chanel in 1909. Chanel is known for their iconic tweed suits, quilted handbags, and timeless designs that have remained relevant for over a century.

Another major player in the industry is Gucci, founded in Florence, Italy in 1921. Gucci is known for their bold and colorful designs that often incorporate the brand's signature double G logo.

Personal Experience

As someone who loves fashion, I have always been drawn to the world of high end fashion designers. I vividly remember the first time I stepped into a Chanel boutique and was in awe of the beautifully crafted pieces on display. While I couldn't afford to purchase anything at the time, I left feeling inspired and determined to one day own a piece from this iconic brand.

Emerging Designers

While the established fashion houses may be the most well-known, there are also a number of up-and-coming designers who are making waves in the industry. These designers offer a fresh perspective on fashion and often incorporate sustainable and ethical practices into their designs.

One such designer is Marine Serre, who has gained a following for her futuristic designs that often incorporate recycled materials. Another emerging designer is Peter Do, who previously worked at Celine and has since launched his own line of clothing that is both minimalist and elegant.

The Cost of High End Fashion Designers

It's no secret that high end fashion designers come with a hefty price tag. However, it's important to remember that these pieces are often made with the highest quality materials and are designed to last for years. In fact, investing in high end fashion can actually be more cost-effective in the long run, as you won't need to constantly replace your clothing.

How to Incorporate High End Fashion Designers into Your Wardrobe

If you're interested in incorporating high end fashion designers into your wardrobe, there are a few key tips to keep in mind. First, invest in timeless pieces that you will wear for years to come. This could be a classic black blazer or a simple silk blouse.

Second, consider purchasing pre-owned pieces from consignment stores or online marketplaces. This is a more affordable way to get your hands on high end fashion designers, while also supporting sustainable fashion practices.

Personal Experience

One of my favorite ways to incorporate high end fashion designers into my wardrobe is by investing in statement accessories. I have a pair of Gucci loafers that I wear constantly and they always elevate my outfit to the next level. While they were an investment, I know that they will last for years and never go out of style.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end fashion designers only for the wealthy?

A: While high end fashion designers can be expensive, there are ways to incorporate their designs into your wardrobe without breaking the bank. Consider purchasing pre-owned pieces or investing in timeless staples that will last for years.

Q: Are there any emerging high end fashion designers to watch?

A: Yes! Marine Serre and Peter Do are just two of the many emerging designers who are making waves in the industry.

Q: What makes high end fashion designers different from fast fashion brands?

A: High end fashion designers prioritize quality over quantity and often incorporate sustainable and ethical practices into their designs. Fast fashion brands, on the other hand, prioritize producing a high volume of clothing at a low cost.

Q: Can high end fashion designers be considered sustainable?

A: Many high end fashion designers are incorporating sustainable practices into their designs, such as using recycled materials or implementing zero-waste production methods. However, it's important to do your research and look for designers who prioritize sustainability.

Conclusion of High End Fashion Designers

While high end fashion designers may seem out of reach for the average person, there are ways to incorporate their designs into your wardrobe without breaking the bank. By investing in timeless pieces and considering pre-owned options, you can elevate your wardrobe with clothing that is both luxurious and sustainable. Whether you opt for an iconic Chanel suit or a statement accessory from Gucci, high end fashion designers offer a level of quality and craftsmanship that is truly unparalleled in the fashion industry.
