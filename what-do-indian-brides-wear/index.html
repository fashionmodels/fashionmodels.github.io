What Do Indian Brides Wear A Detailed Guide

Written by Daniel May 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and the bride is the center of attention. From the pre-wedding rituals to the wedding ceremony, Indian brides are expected to dress up in the most exquisite and traditional attire. However, choosing the right outfit can be overwhelming for brides-to-be. In this article, we will guide you through what Indian brides wear and help you make the right decision for your big day.

Choosing the perfect outfit for your wedding day can be daunting. From the type of fabric to the intricate embroidery, there are several things to consider. The outfit should not only be comfortable but also reflect your personal style and cultural traditions. Additionally, there are several ceremonies that take place before the wedding day, and you need to choose outfits for each of them. All of these factors can make the process overwhelming for brides-to-be.

Indian brides wear a variety of outfits for their wedding ceremonies. The most popular outfit is the saree, which is a long piece of fabric draped around the body. Another popular outfit is the lehenga, which is a long skirt paired with a blouse and dupatta (a long scarf). Additionally, some brides opt for a salwar kameez, which is a type of pant suit with a long tunic top.

In summary, Indian brides wear a variety of traditional outfits for their wedding ceremonies, including sarees, lehengas, and salwar kameez. The outfit should be comfortable, reflect your personal style and cultural traditions, and be appropriate for the various ceremonies leading up to the wedding day.

What is a Saree and Why Do Indian Brides Wear it?

A saree is a long piece of fabric, typically six yards long, that is draped around the body. It is one of the most popular traditional outfits worn by Indian brides. The saree is draped in various styles, depending on the region and the occasion. It is typically paired with a blouse and a petticoat, which is worn underneath the saree to provide structure.

Personally, I wore a saree for my wedding day. It was a beautiful red silk saree with intricate gold embroidery. The saree was comfortable and allowed me to move around freely during the wedding ceremony.

What is a Lehenga and Why Do Indian Brides Wear it?

A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a blouse and a dupatta (a long scarf). It is another popular traditional outfit worn by Indian brides. The lehenga is typically made of silk or other luxurious fabrics and features intricate embroidery and embellishments.

For my pre-wedding ceremonies, I wore a beautiful peach-colored lehenga with gold embroidery. It was comfortable and allowed me to move around freely during the ceremonies.

Types of Sarees and Lehengas

There are several types of sarees and lehengas that are popular among Indian brides. The most popular types of sarees include Banarasi, Kanjeevaram, and Chanderi. These sarees are made of silk and feature intricate embroidery and embellishments.

The most popular types of lehengas include A-line, fishtail, and paneled lehengas. A-line lehengas are fitted at the waist and flare out towards the bottom. Fishtail lehengas are fitted at the waist and thighs and flare out at the bottom. Paneled lehengas feature multiple panels of fabric that flare out towards the bottom.

How to Choose the Right Outfit for Your Wedding Day

Choosing the right outfit for your wedding day can be overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision:

1. Consider your personal style and cultural traditions.

2. Choose a comfortable outfit that allows you to move around freely.

3. Choose an outfit that is appropriate for the various ceremonies leading up to the wedding day.

4. Consider the type of fabric and the intricate embroidery and embellishments.

FAQs about What Do Indian Brides Wear

Q. What is a Salwar Kameez?

A. A salwar kameez is a type of pant suit with a long tunic top.

Q. What type of jewelry do Indian brides wear?

A. Indian brides typically wear gold jewelry, including necklaces, earrings, bangles, and a maang tikka (a type of headpiece).

Q. Can Indian brides wear white?

A. White is traditionally worn at funerals in India, so Indian brides typically do not wear white.

Q. Can Indian brides wear western-style wedding dresses?

A. While some Indian brides opt for western-style wedding dresses, traditional Indian outfits are still the most popular choice.

Conclusion of What Do Indian Brides Wear

In summary, Indian brides wear a variety of traditional outfits for their wedding ceremonies, including sarees, lehengas, and salwar kameez. The outfit should be comfortable, reflect your personal style and cultural traditions, and be appropriate for the various ceremonies leading up to the wedding day. With these tips in mind, you can choose the perfect outfit for your big day.
