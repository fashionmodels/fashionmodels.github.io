Top Interior Designers in the World 2020: A Guide to the Best in the Industry

When it comes to creating a beautiful and functional living space, few things are as important as finding the right interior designer. From choosing the perfect color scheme to selecting the right furnishings and decor, an experienced interior designer can help transform any space into a welcoming and inviting home. However, with so many interior designers out there, it can be difficult to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to the top interior designers in the world for 2020.

At its core, the goal of this guide is to help you find the right interior designer for your needs. Whether you're looking for someone who specializes in modern design or you need help creating a more traditional space, we've got you covered. We've scoured the internet and spoken to industry experts to compile a list of the top interior designers in the world, so you can be sure you're getting the best of the best.

So, what can you expect from this guide? First, we'll give you a brief overview of the top interior designers in the world for 2020, including their specialties and what sets them apart from the competition. Next, we'll delve deeper into each designer, exploring their backgrounds, design philosophies, and notable projects. Along the way, we'll also provide tips for choosing the right interior designer for your needs, so you can be sure you're making an informed decision.

The Best Interior Designers in the World for 2020

One of the top interior designers in the world for 2020 is Kelly Wearstler. With a signature style that blends bold patterns, textures, and colors, Wearstler is known for creating spaces that are both glamorous and sophisticated. Her work has been featured in top publications such as Elle Decor and Architectural Digest, and she has designed spaces for high-profile clients such as Cameron Diaz and Gwen Stefani.

Another top interior designer to watch in 2020 is Axel Vervoordt. Based in Belgium, Vervoordt is known for his minimalist approach to design, which emphasizes natural materials and muted colors. His work has been featured in top publications such as Vogue and The New York Times, and he has designed spaces for clients such as Robert De Niro and Kanye West.

Choosing the Right Interior Designer for Your Needs

When it comes to choosing the right interior designer for your needs, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, consider your budget. While some top interior designers may be out of reach for the average person, there are plenty of talented designers who offer a range of services at different price points. Next, think about your design style. Do you prefer modern, minimalist spaces, or do you crave a more traditional feel? Finally, consider the designer's experience and track record. Look for someone who has a proven track record of success and who has worked on projects similar to yours in the past.

Design Philosophy of Kelly Wearstler

Kelly Wearstler's design philosophy is centered around the idea of creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. She believes that good design should be both timeless and livable, and she often incorporates unexpected elements into her designs to create a sense of drama and excitement. Wearstler is also known for her use of bold colors and patterns, which can add a sense of playfulness and whimsy to any space.

Tips for Working with an Interior Designer

If you're planning to work with an interior designer, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, be clear about your goals and expectations from the outset. This will help ensure that you and your designer are on the same page and that you're both working towards the same end result. Next, be open to feedback and suggestions from your designer. Remember, they are the expert, and they may have ideas that you haven't considered. Finally, be patient. Good design takes time, and it's important to give your designer the space they need to create something truly special.

Notable Projects from Axel Vervoordt

One of Axel Vervoordt's most notable projects is the recently opened Kanaal, a mixed-use development in Belgium that incorporates art galleries, offices, and residential spaces. The project was designed with Vervoordt's signature minimalist style, and features natural materials such as wood and stone throughout. Another notable project is the redesign of the Palais de Justice in Antwerp, which involved transforming a historic courthouse into a modern, multi-use space.

Frequently Asked Questions About Top Interior Designers in the World for 2020

Q: How much do top interior designers charge?

A: The cost of hiring an interior designer can vary widely depending on a number of factors, including the designer's experience, the scope of the project, and the location of the project. Some top designers may charge several thousand dollars per hour, while others may offer more affordable rates.

Q: How long does it take to work with an interior designer?

A: The timeline for working with an interior designer can vary depending on the scope of the project and the designer's availability. However, most projects will take several months to complete, from the initial consultation to the final installation.

Q: How involved will I be in the design process?

A: The level of involvement you have in the design process will depend on your preferences and the designer's approach. Some designers may prefer to take the lead and present you with a finished design, while others may work more collaboratively with you throughout the process.

Q: Can I hire an interior designer for a small project?

A: Yes, many interior designers are happy to take on small projects such as a single room or a small renovation. However, be prepared to pay for their time and expertise, as even small projects can require a significant amount of work.

Conclusion of Top Interior Designers in the World for 2020

Whether you're looking to transform your entire home or just one room, working with a top interior designer can help you achieve the look and feel you've always wanted. By using this guide to the top interior designers in the world for 2020, you can be sure that you're getting the best of the best. Remember to consider your budget, design style, and the designer's experience and track record when making your decision, and be sure to communicate your goals and expectations clearly from the outset. With the right interior designer by your side, you'll be well on your way to creating a space that is both beautiful and functional.
