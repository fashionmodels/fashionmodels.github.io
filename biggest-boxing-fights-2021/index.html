The Highly Anticipated Rematch: Fury vs. Wilder III

One of the biggest fights of the year is the trilogy fight between Tyson Fury and Deontay Wilder. Fans are eagerly anticipating the third fight, which has been rescheduled multiple times due to the pandemic.

This fight is significant for several reasons, one of which is the controversy surrounding the second fight. Many fans felt that Fury should have won, while others believed that Wilder was robbed. The third fight will be the deciding factor, and both fighters are determined to come out on top.

The Undisputed Cruiserweight Championship: Usyk vs. Gassiev

Oleksandr Usyk and Murat Gassiev will compete for the undisputed cruiserweight championship, with all four major titles on the line. This is the first time in history that the cruiserweight division will have an undisputed champion, making it a historic fight.

Both fighters are undefeated and have impressive records, making this an exciting matchup. Usyk is a former Olympic gold medalist and has held all four cruiserweight titles, while Gassiev has a reputation for being a knockout artist.

The Return of Canelo: Alvarez vs. Saunders

Saul "Canelo" Alvarez is one of the most popular boxers in the world, and his return to the ring is highly anticipated. He'll be facing off against Billy Joe Saunders, a talented fighter with a record of 30-0.

Alvarez is the favorite to win, but Saunders is a skilled fighter who shouldn't be underestimated. This fight is significant because it's part of Alvarez's plan to become the undisputed super middleweight champion, with just one more belt to go after this fight.
