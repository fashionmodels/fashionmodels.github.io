How Much Does A Boxer Puppy Cost In India

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you interested in getting a Boxer puppy but unsure of how much it will cost in India? The cost of a Boxer puppy in India can vary depending on several factors. As a potential pet owner, it's important to understand the cost and what you can expect to pay for a Boxer puppy.

The average cost of a Boxer puppy in India is between ₹25,000 to ₹45,000. However, the price can vary depending on the breeder, location, and availability of the breed. It's important to note that the cost of a Boxer puppy can also increase if the puppy comes from a champion bloodline or has a purebred lineage.

Overall, the cost of a Boxer puppy in India can be considered reasonable for its unique qualities and characteristics. Boxers are known for their loyalty, intelligence, and playful nature, making them an ideal pet for families and individuals alike.

Factors that Affect the Cost of a Boxer Puppy in India

The cost of a Boxer puppy in India can vary depending on several factors such as:

Breeder

The breeder can have a significant impact on the cost of a Boxer puppy. Reputable breeders who specialize in Boxers may charge more for their puppies due to their experience, expertise, and commitment to breeding healthy and high-quality dogs.

Location

The location can also impact the cost of a Boxer puppy. Breeders who live in metropolitan cities may charge more for their puppies due to the higher cost of living and demand for the breed.

Personal Experience

I had always wanted a Boxer puppy and decided to adopt one from a reputable breeder in my city. The breeder had a good reputation and was known for breeding healthy and high-quality Boxer puppies. I paid around ₹35,000 for my Boxer puppy, and I can say that it was worth every penny. My Boxer is now six months old and has become an integral part of my family.

Tips for Buying a Boxer Puppy in India

If you're interested in buying a Boxer puppy in India, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Do Your Research

Research different breeders and read reviews from previous customers. Look for breeders who specialize in Boxers and have a good reputation for breeding healthy and high-quality puppies.

Visit the Breeder

Visit the breeder in person to see the puppies and their living conditions. Make sure that the breeder is taking good care of the puppies and that they're in good health.

Question and Answer

Q: Are Boxers good with children?

A: Yes, Boxers are generally good with children and make great family pets.

Q: Do Boxers require a lot of exercise?

A: Yes, Boxers are an active breed and require regular exercise and playtime.

Q: Do Boxers shed a lot?

A: Yes, Boxers do shed, but regular grooming can help manage the shedding.

Q: Are Boxers prone to health problems?

A: Boxers can be prone to certain health problems such as hip dysplasia and heart disease. It's important to buy from a reputable breeder who screens their dogs for these health issues.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Boxer Puppy Cost in India

The cost of a Boxer puppy in India can vary depending on several factors such as the breeder, location, and availability of the breed. However, the average cost of a Boxer puppy in India is between ₹25,000 to ₹45,000. As a potential pet owner, it's important to research different breeders and ensure that you're buying from a reputable source who specializes in Boxers and breeds healthy and high-quality puppies. With their loyalty, intelligence, and playful nature, Boxers make great family pets and are worth the investment.
