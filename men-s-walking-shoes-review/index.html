Choosing The Best Men s Walking Shoes A Comprehensive Review

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Walking is a great form of exercise and an enjoyable way to spend time outdoors. However, walking for long periods of time can take a toll on your feet if you're not wearing the right shoes. Finding the perfect pair of men's walking shoes can be a daunting task, especially if you have specific needs such as arch support or wide feet. In this article, we will review some of the best men's walking shoes and provide you with the information you need to make an informed purchase.

Are you tired of dealing with blisters and sore feet after a long walk? Do you have trouble finding shoes that fit comfortably? Are you looking for a shoe that offers support and stability? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then this review is for you.

The target of this men's walking shoe review is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you choose the best shoe for your needs. We will cover the top men's walking shoes on the market, along with their features and benefits, so you can make an informed decision.

In summary, we will review the best men's walking shoes available on the market, provide information on their features and benefits, and help you choose the perfect pair for your needs.

Men's Walking Shoes Review: Comfort and Style

When it comes to men's walking shoes, comfort and style are two of the most important factors. A comfortable shoe can make all the difference in your walking experience, while a stylish shoe can make you feel confident and put-together. That's why we've chosen two of the best men's walking shoes that offer both comfort and style.

First on our list is the New Balance Men's 847 V4 Walking Shoe. This shoe offers exceptional support and cushioning, making it perfect for those with flat feet or overpronation. It also features a stylish design that will complement any outfit. The shoe's Ortholite foam insert provides added comfort and support, while the Ndurance rubber outsole offers superior durability.

Another great option is the Skechers Men's Go Walk Max Sneaker. This shoe is designed with a breathable mesh upper and a Goga Max insole that provides high-rebound cushioning. The shoe's 5GEN sole offers excellent shock absorption, making it perfect for long walks. It also features a sporty design that will look great with casual outfits.

Men's Walking Shoes Review: Arch Support and Stability

If you have high arches or require extra stability, then choosing a men's walking shoe with arch support and stability features is crucial. Here are two of the best options on the market:

The Brooks Men's Addiction Walker Walking Shoes are an excellent choice for those with high arches or overpronation. The shoe's BioMoGo DNA midsole provides adaptive cushioning, while the Extended Progressive Diagonal Rollbar offers added support and stability. The shoe also features a slip-resistant outsole, making it perfect for those who frequently walk on wet or slippery surfaces.

The ASICS Men's Gel-Tech Walker Neo 4 Walking Shoe is another great option for those who need extra arch support and stability. The shoe's Gel Cushioning System provides superior shock absorption, while the Trusstic System reduces the shoe's weight without sacrificing support. The shoe also features a comfortable and breathable upper that will keep your feet cool during long walks.

Men's Walking Shoes Review: Wide Feet

If you have wide feet, finding a comfortable and supportive walking shoe can be a challenge. However, there are several options available that will accommodate wider feet.

The New Balance Men's MW877 Walking Shoe is designed specifically for those with wider feet. The shoe's ABZORB midsole provides excellent shock absorption, while the Walking Strike Path technology helps guide your foot through the walking gait cycle. The shoe also features a durable leather upper that will provide long-lasting support.

The Propet Men's LifeWalker Strap Walking Shoe is another great option for those with wider feet. The shoe's adjustable hook and loop closure allows for a customizable fit, while the removable cushioned orthotic provides added comfort and support. The shoe's lightweight EVA midsole also provides excellent shock absorption.

Men's Walking Shoes Review: How to Choose the Right Shoe

Now that you know some of the best men's walking shoes on the market, it's important to understand how to choose the right shoe for your needs. Here are a few tips:

  • Consider your foot type: Do you have flat feet, high arches, or overpronation? Choose a shoe that offers the appropriate support for your foot type.
  • Think about your walking routine: Do you walk on pavement, trails, or both? Choose a shoe with the appropriate sole for your walking surface.
  • Try on multiple sizes: Make sure to try on several sizes to ensure the best fit. Your shoe should fit snugly but not be too tight or too loose.
  • Consider the shoe's features: Look for shoes with features that are important to you, such as breathability, durability, and style.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I replace my men's walking shoes?

A: It's recommended to replace your walking shoes every 300 to 500 miles or every 6 to 8 months, whichever comes first.

Q: Can I wear running shoes for walking?

A: While it's not recommended to wear running shoes for walking, some running shoes may work well for walking if they offer the appropriate support and cushioning.

Q: What should I look for in a men's walking shoe?

A: Look for shoes with appropriate support and cushioning for your foot type, a sole that is suitable for your walking surface, and features that are important to you such as durability and style.

Q: Can I wear men's walking shoes for other activities?

A: While men's walking shoes are designed specifically for walking, they may work well for other low-impact activities such as light hiking or casual wear.

Conclusion of Men's Walking Shoes Review

Choosing the perfect pair of men's walking shoes can make all the difference in your walking experience. By considering your foot type, walking routine, and shoe features, you can make an informed purchase that will keep your feet comfortable and supported. Whether you need extra arch support, a shoe for wider feet, or a stylish and comfortable option, there is a men's walking shoe out there for you. We hope this review has been helpful in your search for the perfect pair of walking shoes.
