Panettone

During the Christmas season, panettone is a popular Italian dessert. This sweet bread is filled with raisins and candied fruits and is perfect for sharing with family and friends. Making panettone can be a bit of a challenge, but it's worth the effort. I remember my grandmother making this dessert every Christmas, and it was always a highlight of the holiday season.

Pandoro

Pandoro is another popular Italian Christmas dessert. It is a sweet bread that is similar to panettone but without the candied fruits and raisins. Instead, it is dusted with powdered sugar, which gives it a snow-covered appearance. Pandoro is perfect for breakfast or dessert, and it's easy to make.

Torrone

Torrone is an Italian nougat that is popular during the Christmas season. It is made with honey, sugar, and egg whites, and it's filled with toasted almonds or hazelnuts. Torrone is a bit hard to make, but the end result is worth it. This dessert is perfect for sharing and will impress your family and friends.

Struffoli

Struffoli is a traditional Christmas dessert from the Campania region of Italy. These small, fried dough balls are coated in honey and sprinkles and are perfect for sharing with family and friends. Struffoli are easy to make and are a great alternative to traditional Christmas cookies.
